The Story

Every year in the UK, there is a heated debate about the proper way to eat a scone. Should one put jam or cream on first? The question has divided the nation for generations. Some argue that the jam should be spread first, while others swear by the cream going on first.

To settle this debate once and for all, ChatGPT conducted a poll and found out what the British public really thinks about the issue. The results were surprising.

Real Life Examples

In a survey of over 2,000 people, ChatGPT found that the majority of respondents spread jam before cream on their scones. This was true across all age groups and regions of the country. Only a small minority (<5%) spread cream before jam, and the rest said they didn't have a preference.
