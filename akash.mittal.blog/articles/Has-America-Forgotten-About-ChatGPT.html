Has America Forgotten About ChatGPT?

  • IBM Watson

The Rise of ChatGPT

It's been a few years since artificial intelligence entered our homes and offices. From Alexa to Siri, AI programs are springing up all over the place. But one particular AI tool that has steadily gained traction and has proven to be one of the most promising is ChatGPT. ChatGPT is a natural language processing AI tool that is capable of understanding and answering complex queries posed by humans. It is becoming more popular among businesses and individuals who want quick and accurate answers to their questions.

IBM Watson's Failure

Back in 2011, IBM launched Watson, an AI program that could understand natural language and answer questions. The program was a massive success when it won a game of Jeopardy against two of the show's all-time champions. But despite all the hype, Watson ultimately failed to live up to its own hype. IBM was unable to generate significant revenue from the software, and it quickly fell out of favor.

ChatGPT's Real-Life Examples

While Watson suffered from a lack of real-world application, ChatGPT has been implemented successfully in various businesses and industries. One prominent example is OpenAI, which has developed Generative Pre-trained Transformer 3 (GPT-3), the largest AI language model in the world. Another example is Hugging Face, which developed Transformers, an open-source library that has become a go-to resource for developers working on natural language processing projects. Even the New York Times has harnessed the power of ChatGPT, developing The New York Times Messenger, which provides news updates to readers via Facebook Messenger.

Conclusion

  1. Artificial intelligence is continuously evolving, and businesses need to keep up with the latest technology in order to remain competitive.
  2. Although IBM Watson was a massive failure, it served as a valuable lesson for the industry; companies cannot expect to succeed by simply hyping up a new technology without real-world implementation.
  3. ChatGPT is a promising technology that has demonstrated real-world success. While it remains to be seen if America has forgotten about it, it's clear that the technology has a promising future.
