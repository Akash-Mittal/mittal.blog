The Journey of Samanyou Garg

Samanyou Garg is a young entrepreneur hailing from New Delhi, India. His passion for technology started when he was just a teenager, experimenting with coding on his computer. He pursued his interest in technology by studying Computer Science and Engineering at the prestigious Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Delhi.

After completing his studies, Garg started his career at Oracle, where he worked on database systems and cloud computing. However, his real entrepreneurial journey started when he co-founded the startup 'SqrFactor' in 2014. SqrFactor aimed to provide a platform for student housing in India, where usually students struggle to find good accommodation.

Although the startup didn't succeed, Garg did not give up. He moved on to co-found another startup named 'ZunRoof', which provides solar energy solutions to Indian homes. Today, ZunRoof is one of the leading solar energy companies in India, with over 10,000 installs and a revenue of over $1 million. Garg's innovative idea has helped millions of people in India to implement eco-friendly ways of living.
