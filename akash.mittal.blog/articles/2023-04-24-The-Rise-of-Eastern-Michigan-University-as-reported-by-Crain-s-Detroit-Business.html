The Rise of Eastern Michigan University

Eastern Michigan University has come a long way since its humble beginnings in 1849, when it was founded as Michigan State Normal School. Today, the university is a leading institution of higher education, offering over 200 undergraduate and graduate programs to more than 20,000 students.

An Interesting Story

One of the most striking examples of Eastern Michigan University's growth and innovation is its new Student Center, which opened in fall 2020. The $40 million, state-of-the-art facility boasts a three-story atrium, a 1,000-seat ballroom, a food court, meeting rooms, and plenty of study spaces, among other amenities. It's a true hub of campus life, where students can socialize, learn, and engage with the university community.

Concrete Examples

But the Student Center is just one of many examples of how Eastern Michigan University is expanding its reach and impact. Here are a few more:

  • The university's online program offerings have grown significantly in recent years, with more than 70 programs now available online.
  • The College of Business has earned accreditation from the Association to Advance Collegiate Schools of Business (AACSB) International, a distinction held by less than 5% of the world's business programs.
  • Eastern Michigan University has invested heavily in research and innovation, with more than $40 million in research expenditures in 2020.
