The Most Bizarre Things People Have Hooked Up To ChatGPT

ChatGPT is one of the most popular chat services available today, with millions of users worldwide. While most people use it to chat with friends and family, others have taken the service to the extremes, hooking up some of the most bizarre things imaginable. Here are some real-life examples:

1. A Toaster

Believe it or not, someone once hooked up a toaster to ChatGPT. Whenever someone typed a sentence in the chat, the toaster would start cooking a piece of toast. While it might not be the most practical use of ChatGPT, it certainly was a creative one.

2. A Drone

Another user hooked up a drone to ChatGPT and gave people control of the device via the chat. This led to some wild and crazy adventures, including a drone that flew into a tree and another one that landed in a pond.

3. A Piano

One music enthusiast hooked up a piano to ChatGPT, allowing people to play the instrument via the chat. This led to some beautiful and unique pieces of music being created in real-time, showcasing the power of technology to bring people together.
