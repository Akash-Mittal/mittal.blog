Veloce Takes the Next Step: VEXT in Web3 Evolution

  • Story
  • Examples

Once upon a time, Veloce was known as a software testing company. But over the years, they have grown and evolved into something much bigger. Now, they are taking the next step in Web3 evolution with their latest project: VEXT.

VEXT is a blockchain-based platform that provides decentralized infrastructure for Web3 applications. This means that VEXT will help developers to create faster and more secure applications. Veloce has already partnered with several companies for this project, including:

  • Block.one
  • Arweave
  • dfuse

Together, they will work to build the next generation of Web3 applications.

What Does This Mean for Web3 Evolution?

Veloce's project is a key development towards Web3 evolution. With VEXT, developers will have access to a decentralized platform that can provide faster and more secure infrastructure for Web3 applications. This will help to further decentralize the internet and empower users.

Furthermore, Veloce's partnerships with Block.one, Arweave, and dfuse show that top companies in the blockchain and cryptocurrency space are recognizing the potential of VEXT and its impact on Web3 evolution.
