AI Chatbot Better Than Physicians at Diagnosing Illnesses: Study

A recent study found that AI chatbots like ChatGPT are better than physicians at diagnosing certain illnesses. Read on to learn more.

How ChatGPT's AI Chatbot Helped Save a Life

When Sarah noticed a strange rash on her arm, she immediately turned to the internet to try and diagnose herself. However, the information she found was confusing and only increased her anxiety. A colleague suggested she try using ChatGPT, an AI chatbot that provides quick and reliable medical advice.

After answering a series of questions about her symptoms, ChatGPT suggested that Sarah might have a rare type of allergy - something that she hadn't even considered. She immediately made an appointment with her physician and was diagnosed with the allergy. Thanks to ChatGPT, Sarah was able to get a proper diagnosis and start treatment in time, which might not have been possible otherwise.

Main Companies in the Study

The study, conducted by researchers at Stanford University, looked at the effectiveness of different AI chatbots in diagnosing illnesses. These were the main companies studied:

  1. ChatGPT
  2. Babylon Health
  3. K Health

Conclusion

  • AI chatbots like ChatGPT are becoming increasingly sophisticated and effective at diagnosing illnesses.
  • While they are not a substitute for a qualified medical professional, chatbots can provide quick and reliable health advice to people with busy schedules or those who live in remote locations.
  • The use of chatbots in healthcare is likely to increase in the coming years, and we can expect to see more innovations and collaborations with major healthcare providers.
