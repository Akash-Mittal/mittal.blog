How to Create an Outstanding CV

A few years ago, Reddit user u/jenniferjones01 posted a question asking for advice on how to create an outstanding CV. She had applied to over 50 jobs and hadn't received a single interview call. After receiving many helpful tips from the Reddit community, she revamped her CV using the suggestions provided.

The result? She received over 25 interview calls from various companies and landed a job she loved. The only thing that had changed was her CV.

Concrete Examples

Here are a few concrete examples that can help you create an outstanding CV:

  • Use bullet points to highlight your achievements and experiences.
  • Keep your CV short and concise â no more than two pages.
  • Customize your CV for each application to highlight the skills that match the job requirements.
  • Include relevant keywords and industry-specific terminology to make it easier for recruiters to find you.
  • Use clear and readable fonts that complement your CV's overall design.

Three Key Takeaways

Creating an outstanding CV can seem overwhelming, but remember these three key takeaways:

  1. Focus on your achievements and experiences instead of just job titles and responsibilities.
  2. Customize your CV for each application to showcase how your skills align with the job requirements.
  3. Keep your CV short, concise, and visually appealing.
