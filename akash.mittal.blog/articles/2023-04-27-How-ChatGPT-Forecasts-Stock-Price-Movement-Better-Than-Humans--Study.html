How ChatGPT Forecasts Stock Price Movement Better Than Humans: Study

By Akash Mittal

Have you ever wondered how those finance experts predict the stock market's behavior? They analyze charts, get into the nitty-gritty of financial statements, and try to make informed guesses to guide their clients' investment choices. But, what if we told you that an AI-powered tool can predict the stock price movement with better accuracy than a human?

The Story Behind ChatGPT

ChatGPT is a natural language processing software that uses AI algorithms trained with millions of financial data points to make educated guesses on how a stock will perform in the future. Now, you might wonder how ChatGPT performs better than humans?

Firstly, humans have a cognitive bias, which means their opinions and beliefs get in the way of making objective decisions. Secondly, ChatGPT can analyze data points quickly and without bias, making it more likely to make accurate predictions.

For instance, if we have to predict the stock price of a technology company, we'll look at various factors such as recent news or product launches, competition, financial statements, etc.

Concrete Examples of ChatGPT's Predictions

One of the significant advantages of using ChatGPT is that it can analyze and interpret the news and financial data in real-time. For example, ChatGPT predicted the surge in GameStop's stock prices earlier this year when millions of retail investors jumped onto the bandwagon, pushing the share prices up by over 1700% in just two weeks.

Another instance was when ChatGPT predicted Amazon's higher-than-expected sales for the second quarter of 2021, despite the company's shares being down by $100 billion in market value due to accusations of poor working conditions in their warehouses.

The Advantages of ChatGPT

From these examples, it's evident that ChatGPT can forecast the market with better accuracy than humans. Here are some of its advantages:

  1. ChatGPT is not influenced by human bias and emotions.
  2. ChatGPT can analyze a large volume of data points in real-time, which would take humans much longer to complete.
  3. ChatGPT can work round the clock without taking breaks or losing focus, unlike humans.
