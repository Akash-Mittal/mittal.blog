Drake AI-Generated "Not A Game" Track: Research Article

Picture this: Drake is in his studio, working on his latest album. But this time, he's not alone. He's accompanied by an AI program which is generating melodies and beats for him to work with. The result? His latest hit "Not A Game".

Drake is not the only musician using AI to refine his music. In fact, many major music companies including Sony, Google, and IBM are investing in music AI research.

One such company is Amper Music, which offers a platform that allows users to create custom music with the help of AI. Another example is Jukedeck, which uses AI to generate royalty-free music for businesses.

Conclusion

  1. The use of AI in music is becoming increasingly common, and is raising questions about the role of AI in artistic creation.
  2. While AI-generated music may not have the emotional depth of human-made music, it does have the potential to assist in the creative process.
  3. As AI technology continues to evolve, it will be interesting to see how it transforms not just the music industry, but other art forms as well.
