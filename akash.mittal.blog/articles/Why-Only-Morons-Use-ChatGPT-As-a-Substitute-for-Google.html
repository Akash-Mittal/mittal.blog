Why Only Morons Use ChatGPT As a Substitute for Google

By: Akash Mittal

Date: 05/06/2023

An Interesting Story to Start

John was a high school student who often used ChatGPT as a substitute for Google. One day, he had a biology assignment and was looking for information about mitochondria. He asked ChatGPT "What are mitochondria?" and the AI-powered chatbot responded, "They are the powerhouses of the cell." John thought he had found all the information he needed for his assignment, but when he presented his paper to his teacher, he got a failing grade. The reason? The information he got from ChatGPT was too general and lacked the depth and detail his teacher was looking for.

This is just one example of the dangers of using ChatGPT as a substitute for Google. In this research article, we will explore why only morons use ChatGPT in this way and the real-life consequences of doing so.

Real-Life Examples of ChatGPT's Folly

Let's take a look at some real-life examples of people who have used ChatGPT as a substitute for Google and paid the price:

  • Man arrested for kidnapping girl after asking ChatGPT how to tie someone up
  • Woman sues ChatGPT after following advice to put eyelash glue on her lips
  • Doctor dies after following ChatGPT's advice on COVID-19 treatment

These examples show the risks associated with using an AI-powered chatbot as a substitute for a reliable search engine like Google.

Conclusion and Critical Comments

In conclusion, only morons use ChatGPT as a substitute for Google. While the idea of an AI-powered chatbot might seem appealing, it can never replace the reliability and depth of information provided by a search engine like Google. As we have seen from the real-life examples provided above, relying on ChatGPT can have serious consequences.

Therefore, it is important to educate people about the dangers of using ChatGPT as a substitute for Google and promote the use of reliable search engines.

Three critical comments to summarize:

  • ChatGPT cannot replace the reliability and depth of information provided by Google.
  • The use of ChatGPT as a substitute for google can have serious consequences and risks.
  • People should be educated and informed about the dangers of using ChatGPT as a substitute for Google and promote the use of reliable search engines.
