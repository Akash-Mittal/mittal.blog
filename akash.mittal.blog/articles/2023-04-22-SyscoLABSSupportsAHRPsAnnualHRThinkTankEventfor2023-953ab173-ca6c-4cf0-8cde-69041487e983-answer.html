Sysco LABS Supports AHRP's Annual HR Think Tank Event for 2023

Once upon a time, there was a company called Sysco LABS. They were a technology-focused division of Sysco, the global leader in foodservice distribution. Sysco LABS believed in the power of innovation and collaboration, and they were always looking for ways to support those who shared their values.

That's why when they heard about AHRP's annual HR Think Tank Event for 2023, they knew they had to get involved. AHRP, or the Association of Human Resource Professionals, was dedicated to advancing the HR profession through education, advocacy, and community. Their annual conference brought together HR leaders from around the country to share ideas, learn from one another, and discuss the challenges facing the industry.

Sysco LABS saw this as an incredible opportunity to support the HR community and promote innovation in the industry. They became a proud sponsor of the event, providing resources and expertise to help make it a success.

Concrete Examples

At the HR Think Tank Event for 2023, Sysco LABS was able to connect with some of the most innovative HR professionals in the industry. They shared their own experiences and insights, and learned from others who were doing incredible work.

For example, one of the keynote speakers was a CHRO who had led a groundbreaking diversity and inclusion initiative at their company. They shared best practices and lessons learned with the audience, inspiring many to take action in their own organizations.

Sysco LABS also hosted a breakout session on the future of HR technology. They talked about the latest trends and innovations in areas like AI, machine learning, and automation, and explored how these technologies could transform the HR function.

Overall, Sysco LABS was thrilled to support AHRP's annual HR Think Tank Event for 2023. They believe in the importance of community and collaboration, and they were proud to be a part of an event that promoted these values.
