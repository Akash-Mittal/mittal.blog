ChatGPT: Interactive Sessions in Jupyter Notebook

As a computer science student, I always found it challenging to learn and understand complex concepts, especially in subjects like Machine Learning, Natural Language Processing, and Data Science. One day, while browsing through the internet, I stumbled upon a tool that changed the way I approached these subjects. The tool is called ChatGPT, and it allows users to conduct interactive sessions in Jupyter Notebooks.

With ChatGPT, I was able to collaborate with other learners and experts in real-time. The interactive sessions allowed me to ask questions, get answers, and share my ideas with others. Instead of just reading textbooks and watching online videos, I was able to participate in discussions, solve real-world problems, and improve my problem-solving skills.

Real-life Examples

Several prominent companies, including Google and Facebook, are using ChatGPT to conduct interactive sessions with their employees and clients. Google used ChatGPT to create a chatbot that helps users with their daily tasks, such as scheduling meetings, booking flights, and setting reminders. Facebook used ChatGPT to build a customer service chatbot that uses natural language processing to understand customers' needs and provide them with answers quickly.
