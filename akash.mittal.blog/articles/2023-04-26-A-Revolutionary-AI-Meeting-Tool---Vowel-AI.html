Vowel AI - Transforming AI Meeting experience

By: Akash Mittal

An Interesting Story of AI Meeting

A few months back, I was working on a project with my team. We had to attend a meeting with our clients who were situated in different parts of the world. Due to time zones, it was challenging to schedule a meeting that suits everyone's availability. Finally, we scheduled a meeting, but when we joined the meeting, we experienced voice distortion, background noise, and the meeting tool crashed multiple times. We were frustrated and decided to look for a more reliable meeting tool.

That's when we came across Vowel AI, a revolutionary meeting tool that uses AI to make remote meetings productive, engaging and collaborative.

Concrete Examples of Vowel AI Meeting Features

Vowel AI provides advanced features like auto transcription, recording, summarization, and action items. It enables teams to collaborate efficiently and make meeting notes available to all members in real-time. Here are some concrete examples:

  • Auto-transcription: Vowel AI transcribes conversation into an organized transcript to make it easy to follow the conversation for the attendees who couldn't attend the meeting.
  • Summarization: Vowel AI summarizes the key points discussed in the meeting, helping attendees focus on the critical topics.
  • Action Items: Vowel AI extracts the action points and sends them to the respective team members, ensuring accountability and timely completion of tasks.
  • Real-time Collaboration: Vowel AI enables team members to collaborate in real-time using live annotation and screen-sharing features, making meetings more productive and interactive.

Conclusion - How Vowel AI is transforming AI Meeting Experience

After using Vowel AI, our team has experienced a significant improvement in our meeting experience. Here are the top three reasons why Vowel AI is transforming AI Meeting experience:

  1. Efficient Collaboration: Vowel AI promotes efficient collaboration with tools like auto-transcription, summarization, and action items.
  2. Productive Meetings: Vowel AI enables teams to conduct productive meetings with real-time collaboration and interactive features.
  3. Improved Accessibility: Vowel AI ensures accessibility by providing recordings and transcripts that are available to all team members, even those who could not attend the meeting.
