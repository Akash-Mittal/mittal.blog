An Impressive Collaboration: Google Cloud partners with Polygon Labs

Have you ever seen a commercial or a live sports broadcast and wondered how the graphics are produced? It may surprise you to learn that much of the graphics production work is done through advanced cloud computing. This is where the collaboration between Google Cloud and Polygon Labs comes in.

Polygon Labs is a renowned creator of real-time 3D graphics software, and their partnership with Google Cloud has opened up exciting new possibilities for the industry.

One concrete example of how this partnership has come to fruition is the use of Polygon Labs' software during the 2021 NFL Draft. The software was utilized to create impressive on-screen graphics that displayed the team selection process in real-time, generating excitement for viewers around the world.

Another example of how this collaboration has improved the industry is the ability for reporters to produce news segments from their homes, thanks to the power of cloud computing. The result? More coverage, better quality, and cost savings for news organizations, allowing them to focus on other areas.

Overall, this partnership between Google Cloud and Polygon Labs is revolutionizing the graphics and media industry. With the combined power of advanced technology and innovative software, we can expect to see more stunning and captivating graphics in future broadcasts and commercials.

Conclusion

  1. Google Cloud and Polygon Labs partnership has brought revolutionary changes in the graphics and media industry.
  2. This collaboration has opened up exciting new possibilities like remote reporting and real-time 3D graphics for the industry.
  3. The future is bright for graphic design and media production thanks to this powerful partnership.
