Amazon Plans to Add ChatGPT Style Search to Its Online Store

Amazon, the world's largest online retailer, recently announced plans to revolutionize the online shopping experience by adding ChatGPT Style Search to its online store. According to Amazon, the addition of ChatGPT Style Search will make shopping on its site more intuitive, personalized, and efficient. The company plans to roll out the new feature gradually over the next few months, starting with its flagship categories like electronics and home goods.
