Biden buttons up his new campaign branding for a tough fight

Joe Biden knows better than anyone that the 2020 presidential election is not going to be an easy one. With incumbent Donald Trump running for re-election, Biden's team recognizes the need for a strong and consistent message that can help sway voters in his favor. That's why they're currently implementing a new branding strategy for his campaign.

Biden's campaign branding is centered around the theme of "restore the soul of America". This message is meant to convey a return to traditional American values and a sense of unity that the nation has been lacking in recent years. The branding uses a palette of blue, white, and red, which are the colors of the American flag, along with a custom-designed serif font that exudes a classic, presidential feel.

This branding strategy is not unique to Biden's campaign, however. Many successful companies have used similar tactics to develop a cohesive brand image that resonates with their target audience.

Real-life Examples

One company that has achieved success with branding focused on restoring traditional values is Chick-fil-A. Despite controversy surrounding their owners' religious affiliations, the fast food chain has managed to create a loyal customer base by promoting their chicken sandwiches as a symbol of heartfelt, traditional values.

Another example of successful branding is Apple Inc.. The tech giant's branding is centered on simplicity and elegance. They use a minimalistic logo and sleek packaging to convey a sense of sophistication and exclusivity, which has helped the company establish itself as a preeminent player in the industry.

Like Biden's campaign, both of these companies have used branding to create a distinct image that resonates with their customers. They've incorporated traditional values and simplicity into their branding messaging, which has proven effective.

Conclusion

In summary, Joe Biden's campaign branding is a reflection of his goal to restore traditional American values and promote unity among the nation. He hopes that this messaging will resonate with voters and help him secure a victory in the upcoming election. Many successful companies, such as Chick-fil-A and Apple Inc., have used similar branding strategies to create a cohesive brand image that appeals to their target audience. In today's political climate, branding is an essential component of any successful campaign, and Biden's team recognizes that fact.
