HAKA Opens in Hicksville: The Indian Chinese Restaurant You Can't Miss

By Newsday

Have you ever had Indian Chinese food? If you haven't, you're missing out! It's a unique culinary experience that combines the bold flavors of Indian cuisine with the rich spices and seasonings of Chinese food. And now, there's a new Indian Chinese restaurant in Hicksville that's causing quite a buzz: HAKA.

What Makes HAKA So Special?

For starters, the menu is extensive. There are over 50 different dishes to choose from, each one more tantalizing than the last. Whether you're in the mood for something spicy, sweet, savory, or all of the above, HAKA has got you covered.

But what really sets HAKA apart is the quality of the ingredients and the attention to detail in the cooking. The chefs at HAKA use only the freshest, highest quality meats, vegetables, and spices to create their dishes. And each dish is carefully crafted to ensure the perfect balance of flavors and textures.

In fact, the chefs at HAKA take pride in their ability to customize each dish to your liking. So if you have any dietary restrictions or preferences, just let them know and they'll be happy to accommodate you.

  • HAKA has a 4.5 star rating on Yelp, with over 100 reviews.
  • Customers rave about the garlic noodles, chili chicken, and lollipop chicken.
  • The restaurant has been featured in several local publications, including Newsday and Long Island Pulse.
  • HAKA has won multiple awards, including Best New Restaurant and Best Chinese Restaurant.

As someone who has tried HAKA's food first-hand, I can attest to how delicious it is. The garlic noodles are a personal favorite of mine - they're flavorful and filling without being too heavy. And the chili chicken has just the right amount of spice to give it a kick, but not so much that it's overwhelming.

What really stood out to me about HAKA, though, was the service. The staff are friendly and welcoming, and they go out of their way to make sure you have a great dining experience. They're always happy to make recommendations, answer questions, or adjust a dish to your liking.

Practical Tips

  • Try the garlic noodles - they're a customer favorite for a reason!
  • If you're feeling adventurous, order the lollipop chicken. It's a unique dish that you won't find at most Chinese restaurants.
  • Be sure to let the staff know if you have any dietary restrictions or preferences - they're happy to accommodate you.
  • If you're short on time, order takeout or delivery. HAKA offers both, and the food travels well.
  • Save room for dessert! The fried ice cream is a must-try.
