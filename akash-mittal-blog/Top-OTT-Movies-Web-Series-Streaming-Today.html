Let's take a look at some of the most popular movies and web series streaming on OTT platforms nowadays:

  • Bhediya: Starring Varun Dhawan and Kriti Sanon, this Indian horror-comedy film follows the story of a man who transforms into a were-wolf and the chaos that ensues.
  • City Of Dreams 3: This Indian political thriller web series, now in its third season, is the story of a powerful family grappling to maintain control of Mumbai after the assassination of their patriarch.
  • Poniyin Selvan II: Based on an epic historical novel by Kalki Krishnamurthy, this upcoming Tamil movie boasts an all-star cast and centers around the story of a young prince striving to reclaim his throne.

These are just a few examples of the incredible content available on OTT platforms. The engaging stories, intense performances, and breathtaking visual effects will leave you spellbound and craving for more.
