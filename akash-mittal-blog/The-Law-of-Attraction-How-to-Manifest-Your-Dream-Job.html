**Step 2 - Believe You'll Get It**

Many people fail to manifest their dream job because they don't truly believe they'll get it. You need to feel a sense of unwavering knowing that the job is yours, and that the universe is conspiring to make it happen. It's essential to take action towards your dream job, but it's equally important to take inspired action.
