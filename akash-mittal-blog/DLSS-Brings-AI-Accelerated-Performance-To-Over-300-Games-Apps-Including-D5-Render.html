DLSS Brings AI Accelerated Performance To Over 300 Games Apps Including D5 Render

Have you ever experienced lag or slow performance when playing your favorite game on your computer?

I certainly have. I remember playing my favorite game and getting frustrated with the constant lag and low frame rates. It was affecting my overall gaming experience and I wasn't the only one experiencing this problem.

Fortunately, DLSS (Deep Learning Super Sampling) has emerged as the ultimate solution to these problems.

What is DLSS?

DLSS is a cutting-edge technology that brings AI (Artificial Intelligence) into the gaming world. It uses AI to boost gaming performance by enhancing the resolution quality of games while maintaining high frame rates at the same time.

The technology works by training AI models to reconstruct images at high quality using low-resolution input data. This means that a game can use a lower resolution and still have a high quality image output.

DLSS in Action

The results of implementing the DLSS technology in games are significant. Gamers are able to experience their favorite games with higher frame rates, better image quality, and more consistent performance across different devices.

Recently, the DLSS technology has been implemented in over 300 games and apps including D5 Render, a popular architectural rendering app.

According to NVIDIA, the company behind the technology, DLSS enables D5 Render users to produce high-quality scenes up to seven times faster than traditional rendering methods. This is a significant improvement in rendering times and can help architects and designers to accomplish their projects faster.

To illustrate how DLSS brings AI-accelerated performance to games, let's look at some quantifiable examples:

  • Control: The game Control saw a 70% increase in frame rates when DLSS was enabled.
  • Cyberpunk 2077: With DLSS enabled, Cyberpunk 2077 maintains an average frame rate of 60 FPS on RTX 3080 graphic cards, while without DLSS the average frame rate drops down to 40 FPS
  • Necromunda: Hired Gun: This game saw a 1.4x performance improvement in frame rates when DLSS on Balanced mode was enabled.

The Benefits of DLSS

So what are the benefits of DLSS?

  1. Improved Gaming Experience: Gamers can experience their favorite games with higher frame rates and better image quality.
  2. Faster Productivity: In apps like D5 Render, DLSS can help architects and designers produce high-quality scenes up to seven times faster than traditional rendering methods.
  3. Device Compatibility: DLSS helps games and apps to perform consistently across different devices, improving user experience.

In conclusion, DLSS is a technology that has revolutionized the gaming and design industries. It has transformed the way games are played and scenes are rendered, providing users with a faster and better experience overall. As more games and apps add this technology, we can expect to see even greater improvements in performance and graphics in the future.
