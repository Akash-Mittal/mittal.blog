The 25 Best Places for Single Moms to Live and Work in 2023

Being a single mom is not an easy task. Raising a child alone while juggling work and household responsibilities can be overwhelming. However, the good news is that today there are many places in the US where single moms can find a job and have a good quality of life. We have compiled a list of the 25 best places for single moms to live and work in 2023.

A Tale of Two Moms

Jennifer and Sarah are two single moms who live in different parts of the US but share a common objective - to provide the best life possible for their children. They both have good-paying jobs and live in cities that are kid-friendly, affordable, and offer good quality of life.

Jennifer, who lives in Austin, Texas, works for a tech company and earns $85,000 per year. She has a 6-year-old daughter, and they live in a 2-bedroom apartment that costs $1,500 per month. Austin is a vibrant city with great outdoor activities and plenty of cultural events. Jennifer and her daughter love going to the Austin Nature and Science Center and the Austin Zoo.

Sarah, who lives in Boise, Idaho, works for a healthcare organization and earns $75,000 per year. She has two children, a 10-year-old daughter and a 7-year-old son, and they live in a 3-bedroom house that costs $1,200 per month. Boise is a family-friendly city with low crime rates and great schools. Sarah and her children love going to the Boise State University Planetarium and the Boise River Greenbelt.

Both Jennifer and Sarah feel fortunate to live in cities that offer them and their children a great quality of life. They have good jobs, affordable housing, and plenty of activities to enjoy with their children.

According to a recent study by WalletHub, a personal finance website, these are the top 10 best cities for single moms to live in 2023:

  1. Madison, Wisconsin
  2. Plano, Texas
  3. Fremont, California
  4. Seattle, Washington
  5. Irvine, California
  6. Scottsdale, Arizona
  7. Overland Park, Kansas
  8. Virginia Beach, Virginia
  9. Boise, Idaho
  10. San Francisco, California

The rankings were based on factors such as cost of living, child care costs, job opportunities, and overall quality of life.

For example, Madison, Wisconsin, ranked first in the overall ranking thanks to its quality of life, job opportunities, and low child care costs. Plano, Texas, ranked second thanks to its low cost of living and good job opportunities. Boise, Idaho, ranked ninth thanks to its family-friendly environment and affordable housing.

Conclusion

Single moms can thrive anywhere with hard work and dedication. However, living in a city that is affordable, offers good job opportunities, and has a great quality of life can make a huge difference. Here are three key takeaways from our article:

  • There are many cities in the US that offer good job opportunities and a great quality of life for single moms.
  • WalletHub's study is a great resource for single moms who are looking for a new place to live and work.
  • If you are a single mom, prioritize your job and living situation to ensure that you and your children have the best possible life.
