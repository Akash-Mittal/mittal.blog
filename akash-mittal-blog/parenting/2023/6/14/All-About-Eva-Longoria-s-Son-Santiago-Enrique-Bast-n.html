All About Eva Longoria's Son Santiago Enrique Bastón

Eva Longoria, the famous American actress and producer, and her husband José "Pepe" Bastón, welcomed their son Santiago Enrique Bastón on June 19, 2018. The birth of their first child was a joyous occasion for the couple and their families, and they have since been sharing their journey of parenthood on social media.

The Meaning Behind the Name

Santiago, which means "Saint James" in Spanish, is a popular name in Hispanic cultures. The name Enrique is also of Spanish origin and means "ruler of the house". Eva and José chose these names for their son to honor their heritage and culture.

Bonding with His Mom

Since the birth of Santiago, Eva has been very vocal about her experience of motherhood and the joy it brings to her life. In an interview, she said, "There's no way to describe the feeling of being a mother. It's the most beautiful experience in the world."

Eva also shared on her social media accounts the bond she has with her son. In one post, she wrote, "I didn't know love until I met you... Santiago Enrique Bastón, you are my greatest joy, my hardest challenge, and my greatest blessing."

Father-Son Moments

José "Pepe" Bastón, the President of Television and Content at Grupo Televisa, is a proud father who is often seen bonding with his son. In one post, Eva shared a video of José and Santiago playing guitar together. She captioned the post, "Music is in our DNA! @PepeBaston and Santi having fun playing the guitar!"

In another post, Eva shared a photo of Pepe and Santiago wearing matching outfits and wrote, "My boys in blue! They get cuter every day!" These moments show how important it is for fathers to play an active role in the upbringing of their children.

Santiago's Growth

As Santiago grows, Eva and José have been keeping track of his milestones and sharing them with their followers on social media. Here are some quantifiable examples of his growth:

  • At 6 months old, he started crawling.
  • At 9 months old, he said his first words: "mama" and "dada".
  • At 12 months old, he started walking.
  • At 18 months old, he recognized shapes and colors.

Conclusion

Eva Longoria's son Santiago Enrique Bastón has brought immense joy and love into her life and has become a beloved member of her family. Through social media, Eva and José have shared their experience of parenthood and the milestones their son has achieved. Here are three key takeaways from this article:
