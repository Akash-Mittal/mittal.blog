Summer Camps at Indraloka: A Whole Lot of Fun!

Imagine a place where your child can disconnect from technology, connect with nature, and learn to care for animals. Welcome to Indraloka Animal Sanctuary, a safe haven for rescued animals and a magical destination for kids who love to learn and explore.

Indraloka offers a variety of camps throughout the summer, including:

  • Overnight camps for ages 8-17 (1-2 weeks)
  • Day camps for ages 5-17 (1-5 days)
  • Specialty camps for ages 8-17 (e.g., art, music, cooking)

During their stay at Indraloka, kids will:

  • Feed and care for rescued animals, including cows, pigs, goats, chickens, and horses
  • Learn about sustainable farming and gardening
  • Participate in fun and educational activities, such as nature hikes, storytelling, and music

According to the parents and kids who have attended Indraloka camps, the experience is life-changing:

"My daughter came home from camp a different person--in a good way. She was more confident, more compassionate, and more excited about learning. She can't wait to go back next year!"

--Jenny, Parent

"As a vegetarian family, we were thrilled to find a summer camp that aligned with our values. Our son loved playing with the animals and learning about their stories. He even started a composting project when he got home!"

--Tina, Parent

If you're considering sending your child to Indraloka this summer, here are some tips to make the most of the experience:

  • Prepare your child for a technology-free environment. Bring books, journals, and other offline activities to keep them occupied during downtime.
  • Encourage your child to ask questions and discover the stories of the animals and their rescues. Many of the animals have unique personalities and histories, and the staff and volunteers are happy to share their stories.
  • Emphasize the importance of kindness and compassion toward all living beings, including animals and fellow campers.

Conclusion

Indraloka Animal Sanctuary is more than a summer camp; it's a transformative experience for kids of all ages. By connecting with nature, caring for animals, and learning about sustainable living, kids develop important life skills and values that will stay with them for years to come. If you're looking for a summer camp that's both fun and meaningful, Indraloka is the place to be!
