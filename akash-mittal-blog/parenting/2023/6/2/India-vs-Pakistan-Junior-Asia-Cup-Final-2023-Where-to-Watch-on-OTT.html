India vs Pakistan Junior Asia Cup Final 2023: Where to Watch on OTT

The India vs Pakistan rivalry is one of the most intense in sports, especially in hockey. The Junior Asia Cup Final in 2023 promises to be a thrilling encounter between the two nations. If you're wondering where to catch all the action on OTT, here's what you need to know.

Hotstar

Hotstar is the official streaming partner for the Junior Asia Cup Final 2023. You can watch the match live on Hotstar, which is available on both web and mobile platforms. To access the live stream, you will need a Hotstar subscription, which costs Rs. 299 per month. However, if you're a new user, you can avail of the free trial for the first month.

JioTV

If you're a Jio user, you can watch the match on JioTV for free. JioTV is a live TV streaming app that allows Jio users to watch live TV channels and catch-up TV programs on their mobile devices. The app is available for free download on both Android and iOS devices.

Airtel Xstream

Airtel Xstream is another streaming app that offers live TV channels, movies, and TV shows. Airtel users can watch the Junior Asia Cup Final 2023 live on Airtel Xstream for free. The app is free to download on both Android and iOS devices. However, you will need an Airtel SIM card to log in to the app.
