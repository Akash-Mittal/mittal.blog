How Families Rely on Disney for Parenting Advice



Image Credits https://insidethemagic.net/2023/06/how-families-rely-on-disney-for-parenting-advice-cm1/

Our Story

Do you remember watching Disney movies as a child and thinking that the characters were your role models? It turns out that many parents today still feel the same way. In fact, families are increasingly relying on Disney for parenting advice.

Take Maria, for instance. She is a mother of two young children and says that she uses Disney movies as a tool to teach her children valuable life lessons. "My kids respond really well to the characters and stories," she says. "They listen more when it comes from Mickey Mouse or Elsa than when it comes from me."

Maria is not alone. Many parents have turned to Disney movies, television shows, and theme parks as a way to teach their children about things like honesty, friendship, and perseverance. And the trend shows no signs of slowing down.

  • According to a survey conducted by the Center for Generational Kinetics and Disney Institute, 55% of parents say that Disney movies and characters have influenced their parenting style.
  • A study by Common Sense Media found that more than half of parents (58%) say that using media and technology for educational purposes is extremely important, and 38% of those parents say that Disney movies and shows are among the top resources they use.
  • The Disney Junior channel, which targets children under the age of 7, is the #1 preschooler network for the fourth consecutive year, surpassing Nickelodeon and Cartoon Network in ratings.

Practical Tips

  • Watch Disney movies and shows with your children and use them as teaching moments.
  • Encourage your children to role-play as their favorite Disney characters to help reinforce the lessons they learn from the movies.
  • Visit a Disney theme park or take a Disney cruise to experience the magic firsthand and create lasting memories with your family.

Conclusion

  1. Disney movies, television shows, and theme parks have become powerful tools for parents to teach their children about important life lessons.
  2. The influence of Disney on parenting styles is growing, with more than half of parents saying that they have been influenced by Disney characters and stories.
  3. By watching Disney movies and shows with their children, encouraging role-play, and experiencing the magic of Disney firsthand, parents can use the power of Disney to create meaningful and memorable family experiences.
