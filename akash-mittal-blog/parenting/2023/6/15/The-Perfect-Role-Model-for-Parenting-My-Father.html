The Perfect Role Model for Parenting: My Father



Image Credits https://www.devdiscourse.com/article/entertainment/2489643-my-father-was-a-perfect-example-of-good-parenting-says-tv-actor-reena-kapoor-as-she-remembers-her-dad

There are some people in our lives who leave a significant impact on us, and my father is one such person. He was not just a parent but a friend, a guide, and a mentor. Growing up, I have seen him play different roles in my life, and I can confidently say that he was a perfect example of good parenting.

The Beginning of the Journey

I still remember the day when I was born; my father was elated to become a dad, and from the very beginning, he was committed to providing me with the best upbringing possible. Despite his busy schedule, he would always take out time to spend with me, and as a child, I would eagerly wait for his return from work so that we could play together.

My father was not just a playmate, but he was also an excellent tutor. Whenever I had doubts or needed help with my studies, he would sit with me and teach me patiently. He was never harsh or critical and always celebrated my achievements, no matter how small they were.

Qualities That Made My Father a Perfect Parent

As I grew older, I began to understand the qualities that made my father such a wonderful parent. Here are some of the qualities that he possessed:

  • Patient and Understanding: My father was incredibly patient and understanding. He would always listen to me intently and never rushed me. He never gave up on me and always believed in me.
  • Discipline: My father was strict when it came to discipline, but he was also fair. He would always explain why rules were important and taught me to respect them. His discipline helped me become responsible and independent.
  • Supportive: My father has always been my biggest supporter. He never judged me, no matter how silly my pursuits were. He always stood by me and pushed me to achieve my goals.
  • Compassionate: My father was a compassionate person who would always help others. He instilled in me the importance of helping those in need and being kind to everyone.
  • Presence: Despite his busy schedule, my father always made sure to be present in my life. He would never miss any important events, whether it was a school function or a sports match.

Conclusion

My father has been an integral part of my life, and he has taught me a lot about parenthood. Here are some of the key takeaways from my father's parenting approach:

  1. Be patient and understanding with your children.
  2. Discipline your children in a fair and respectful way.
  3. Be supportive and help your children achieve their goals.
  4. Be compassionate and kind to everyone.
  5. Be present in your children's lives.

Parenting is not an easy job, but with the right approach, anyone can become a good parent. My father's parenting approach has been a guiding light for me, and I hope it can help others too.
