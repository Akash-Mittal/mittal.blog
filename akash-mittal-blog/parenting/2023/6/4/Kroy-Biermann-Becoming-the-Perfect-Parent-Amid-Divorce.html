This caught the attention of many people and raised the question, can you become the perfect parent even amid a divorce?

The "Good Parenting" course that Biermann took is a program that was designed to improve parenting skills and ultimately enhance the relationship between parents and children. It is made up of several courses and tests that will evaluate an individual's performance as a parent. The courses cover various topics, such as effective communication, discipline, emotional intelligence, and many more.

Biermann, being the motivated individual that he is, took the courses seriously and applied each lesson to his parenting. He even shared some of the things he learned on his social media accounts. One of the lessons he shared was about active listening. He shared how he learned to listen actively to his children and be present in the moment when they talk to him. This made a huge difference in his relationship with his kids, and he noticed that they became more open and honest with him.
