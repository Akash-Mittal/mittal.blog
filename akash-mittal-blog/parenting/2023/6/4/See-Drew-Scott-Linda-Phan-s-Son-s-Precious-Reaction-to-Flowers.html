See Drew Scott & Linda Phan's Son's "Precious" Reaction to Flowers

Published on October 14,

It's no secret that Drew Scott and his wife Linda Phan are proud parents to their son, whom they adoringly call "Little Drew". Recently, Linda Phan shared a heartwarming video on Instagram of her son's reaction to a bouquet of flowers.

In the video, Little Drew can be seen sitting on the floor, surrounded by toys and books, when his mom brings in a beautiful bouquet of flowers. As soon as he spots the flowers, his face lights up with joy and excitement. He claps his hands and says "wow" repeatedly, clearly in awe of the colorful blooms. Linda can be heard in the background saying "are those flowers for Mommy? Oh no, they're for Little Drew, because he's such a good boy."

This adorable reaction from Little Drew is not only heartwarming to watch, but it also illustrates the positive impact that flowers can have on our lives. Flowers have been shown to have numerous benefits, including improving our mood, reducing stress levels, and even boosting our cognitive function.

the Benefits of Flowers

  • A study conducted by researchers at Rutgers University found that "the presence of flowers triggers happy emotions, heightens feelings of life satisfaction and affects social behavior in a positive manner far beyond what is normally believed."
  • Another study published in the Journal of Environmental Psychology found that "participants who saw flowers in the morning reported feeling less anxious and more positive throughout the day."
  • Research also shows that having flowers in the home can improve our quality of sleep and increase feelings of compassion towards others.

So, next time you're thinking of ways to brighten up your day or someone else's, consider the simple act of gifting a bouquet of flowers.
