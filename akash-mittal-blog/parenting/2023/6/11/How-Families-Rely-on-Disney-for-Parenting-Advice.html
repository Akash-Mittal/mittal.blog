How Families Rely on Disney for Parenting Advice



Image Credits https://insidethemagic.net/2023/06/how-families-rely-on-disney-for-parenting-advice-cm1/

When Sarah became a mom, she was excited and terrified all at once. She wanted to be the best mom she could be but didn't know where to start. She was a huge fan of Disney movies and decided to turn to them for guidance. She loved the messages of love, bravery, and determination in films like 'Frozen' and 'Moana'. Whenever she faced a parenting challenge, she would ask herself, 'What would Elsa do?' Sarah is not alone; many young families are turning to Disney for parenting advice.

A study conducted by Common Sense Media found that parents who watch Disney movies with their kids are more likely to discuss meaningful topics like kindness, bravery, and perseverance. Additionally, a poll conducted by the site Babble showed that 85% of parents believe that Disney movies teach valuable lessons to their kids.

Furthermore, the popularity of Disney's parenting blog, 'Disney Family', is a testament to the fact that families are turning to Disney for parenting advice. The blog provides tips on everything from crafts to cooking to parenting.

One mom, Lisa, found that the movie 'Coco' helped her explain death to her young children. She says, "The movie opened up a conversation about our family members who had passed away. My kids were able to understand the concept of death in a way that was appropriate for their age."

Another mom, Maria, found that 'The Lion King' helped her teach her son about responsibility. She says, "My son loved the movie, and we were able to have a discussion about why it's important to take care of others and stand up for what's right. The movie became a lesson on leadership and courage."

Practical Tips

  • Watch Disney movies with your kids and use them as a jumping-off point for discussions about important topics
  • Check out Disney's parenting blog, 'Disney Family', for practical tips and inspiration
  • Encourage your kids' love of Disney by incorporating it into everyday activities, like cooking Disney-themed meals or doing Disney-themed crafts
