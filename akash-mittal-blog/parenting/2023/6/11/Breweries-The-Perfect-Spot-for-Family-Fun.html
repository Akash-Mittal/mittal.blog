A Mom's Perspective

Why Breweries Are Better Than Playgrounds

As a mom of three, I'm always looking for fun and unique places to take my kids. And that's how we discovered breweries. Yes, you read that right: breweries.

It might seem like an odd choice for a family outing, but hear me out.

One day, I was meeting some friends at a local brewery for a beer. I didn't want to leave my baby home, so I decided to bring her along. To my surprise, the brewery turned out to be the perfect spot for a family outing. Here's why:

  1. Breweries offer a relaxed and casual atmosphere.
  2. Breweries are usually spacious and have plenty of seating.
  3. Breweries often have board games, cornhole, or other fun games for all ages to enjoy.

Since that day, I've been taking my kids to breweries and it has become our go-to hangout spot!

Still not convinced that breweries are better than playgrounds for families to hang out? Let me give you some quantifiable examples:

  • According to a study by the American Academy of Pediatrics, playground injuries result in an estimated 200,000 emergency department visits each year.
  • On the other hand, the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC) has reported that moderate alcohol consumption by adults can decrease the risk of heart disease, stroke, and diabetes.
  • Furthermore, the National Institute on Alcohol Abuse and Alcoholism (NIAAA) states that children and adolescents who have positive family relationships and strong parental monitoring are less likely to engage in risky behaviors such as drinking alcohol.

So while playgrounds can be risky for kids, breweries can actually have health benefits for adults and can promote positive family relationships.

Conclusion: Why Breweries Are the Perfect Spot for Family Fun

In summary, here are three reasons why breweries are better than playgrounds for families to hang out:

  1. Breweries offer a relaxed and casual atmosphere that is perfect for all ages.
  2. Breweries provide opportunities for family bonding over board games and other fun activities.
  3. Breweries can have health benefits for adults and can promote positive family relationships.

So next time you're looking for a fun family outing, consider a brewery. You might be surprised at how much fun you have!

and Practical Tips

As promised, here are some personal anecdotes and practical tips for taking your family to a brewery:

  • Bring your own snacks and non-alcoholic drinks for the kids.
  • Look for breweries that have outdoor seating or a play area for the kids.
  • Plan your visit during off-peak hours to avoid crowds and noise.
  • Don't be afraid to ask the brewery staff for recommendations on family-friendly activities.
  • Be responsible and limit your alcohol consumption if you're driving with your family.

And for those who might be worried about exposing their children to alcohol, there are plenty of breweries that promote a family-friendly atmosphere and have strict policies against underage drinking. Just do your research beforehand and choose a brewery that fits your family's needs.
