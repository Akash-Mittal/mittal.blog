The Mess of Parenting: A Gift

Introduction

Being a parent is not an easy task, and when you add a divorce to the mix, things can get even more complicated.

However, Jennifer Garner, actress and mother of three, believes that parenting can also be a gift, despite the mess that comes with it.

In a recent interview with Allure, Garner opened up about co-parenting with her ex-husband Ben Affleck and the challenges they faced while raising their kids.

According to the American Psychological Association, children of divorce may experience a range of difficulties, such as:

  • Higher rates of emotional and behavioral problems
  • Lower academic achievement, especially among boys
  • Increased risk of substance use and delinquent behavior
  • Greater likelihood of experiencing poverty and economic hardship

However, Garner and Affleck have managed to overcome some of these challenges by prioritizing their children's well-being and working together as a team.

For example, they have:

  • Agreed to live near each other for the sake of the kids
  • Attended school events and family gatherings together
  • Coordinated their schedules to make sure someone is always there for their children

These efforts have not gone unnoticed by their kids, as Garner explains:

"It's always like, 'Dad gets to do this' or 'Mom gets to do this,' and we're like, 'No, no, no! It's everybody's! We all get to go!'"

Garner also emphasizes the importance of communication and honesty in co-parenting:

"If you're angry with each other or if you're feeling resentful or if you're feeling any of those negative emotions that come up, it has to be put aside because it's not about us. It's about the kids."

Garner shares some of her own experiences as a mom, and how she embraces the messiness of parenting:

"When you're a mom, you think, 'Well, I love my kids. I'm a good mom.' But is that true? I shouldn't just think about what my kids need; I should think about what I need. Am I nurturing myself? Yes and no."

She also admits that parenting can be overwhelming at times:

"Is it difficult to balance everything? Absolutely. Is it a mess of a journey? Absolutely. But I wouldn't have it any other way."

Garner believes that the challenges of parenting are part of the journey, and that they can be a source of growth and learning:

"I feel like parenting is about...learning who you are, taking responsibility for your life. Everything is about learning, but it's also about having fun."

She encourages other parents to embrace the mess of parenting and find joy in the journey:

"This is your life. Own it. Don't let somebody else's expectations take away your joy."

Conclusions

Parenting can be a challenging and messy journey, especially for divorced couples. However, as Jennifer Garner and Ben Affleck show, it can also be a gift if approached with the right mindset and priorities.

To make co-parenting work, it's important to:

  • Put the children's well-being first
  • Communicate honestly and respectfully
  • Coordinate schedules and prioritize family time

As for parenting in general, Garner advises:

  • Nurture yourself as well as your children
  • Embrace the messiness and learn from it
  • Find joy and have fun
