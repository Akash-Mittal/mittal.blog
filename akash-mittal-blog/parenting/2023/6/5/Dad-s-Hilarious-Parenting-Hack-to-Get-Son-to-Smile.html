Dad's Hilarious Parenting Hack to Get Son to Smile

Being a parent can be tough, especially when trying to get your child to smile for a photo. One dad from Florida, however, has come up with a hilarious parenting hack that has gone viral on social media.

When Greg Eichhorn's son refused to cooperate for a family photo, Eichhorn decided to get creative. He fashioned a fake set of teeth made from candy corn and popped them into his mouth. The result was an instant burst of laughter from his son, who couldn't help but smile at the sight of his dad's new look.

Eichhorn shared a video of the hilarious moment on TikTok, and it quickly went viral. Parents everywhere have been using Eichhorn's hack to get their own kids to smile for photos, and it has become a trend on social media.

The popularity of Eichhorn's parenting hack is evidenced by the numbers. The video has garnered over 5 million views on TikTok alone, and has been shared on countless parenting blogs and social media sites.

But the real proof of its success can be seen in the countless photos and videos shared by parents who have tried the candy corn teeth trick on their own children. One parent on Twitter shared a video of their son laughing uncontrollably at the sight of his dad's new teeth, with the caption "It really works!"

Another parent on Instagram shared a before-and-after photo of their daughter, who went from a grumpy expression to a big smile thanks to the candy corn teeth.
