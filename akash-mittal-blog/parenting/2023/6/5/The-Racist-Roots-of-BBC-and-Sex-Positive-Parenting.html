The Importance of Sex Positive Parenting

Sex positive parenting involves teaching children to have a healthy and positive relationship with their bodies and sexuality. It is a way of promoting open communication and affirms that their body is their own and they have the right to make choices about it.

This approach can help to prevent the perpetuation of harmful stereotypes and to create a more inclusive and accepting society.

Some examples of sex positive parenting include:

  • Talking openly and honestly with your children about their bodies and sexual health
  • Teaching them about consent and boundaries from a young age
  • Encouraging them to explore their own bodies in a safe and healthy way
