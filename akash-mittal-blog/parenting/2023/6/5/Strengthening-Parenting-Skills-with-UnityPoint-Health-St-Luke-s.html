Practical Tips for Strengthening Parenting Skills

Here are some practical tips for strengthening your parenting skills, based on the techniques taught in the UnityPoint Health St. Luke's Parenting Program:

  • Practice active listening skills when communicating with your child
  • Set clear and consistent boundaries and consequences for inappropriate behavior
  • Reinforce positive behaviors through praise and rewards
  • Establish a routine and schedule for daily activities
  • Practice self-care and seek support from family, friends, or a therapist when needed
