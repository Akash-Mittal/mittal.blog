Meet the Cast of Parental Guidance 2023

An Interesting Story

Parental Guidance 2023 is an upcoming movie that tells the story of a couple who struggle with parenting their three teenage children. The parents are overprotective and controlling, which leads to tension and conflict in the family. When the parents are called away for a business trip, their children are left in the care of their grandparents, who have a more traditional and relaxed approach to parenting. The clash of parenting styles leads to some hilarious and heartwarming moments, as the family learns to appreciate each other's perspectives and find a balance that works for everyone.

Quantifiable Examples

Parenting is never easy, and it's easy to fall into patterns of behavior that may not always be effective or healthy. Here are a few of the quantifiable examples that the movie addresses:

  • The parents always make decisions for their children, without allowing them to have any input or autonomy. This leads to resentment, rebellion, and a lack of trust between the parents and their children.
  • The grandparents are more lenient and permissive with their grandchildren, which can lead to misunderstandings and conflicts over boundaries and rules.
  • The children struggle with issues like peer pressure, academic stress, and social media addiction, which can be exacerbated by their parents' overprotectiveness.
  • The parents and grandparents both have to learn to communicate effectively with each other, even when they have different opinions and approaches to parenting.

The Cast

Parental Guidance 2023 boasts an impressive cast of actors and actresses, who bring their talent and charisma to the story.

Steve Martin

Steve Martin plays the role of the patriarch of the family, a successful businessman who has trouble connecting with his children and understanding their needs and desires. Martin is known for his comedic timing and his ability to inject humor into even the most serious of situations.

Diane Keaton

Diane Keaton plays the role of the matriarch of the family, a strong-willed and opinionated woman who has her own ideas about how to raise their children. Keaton is an accomplished actress who has won multiple awards for her performances in films like Annie Hall and The Godfather.

Zendaya

Zendaya plays the role of the oldest daughter, a high school senior who is struggling with college applications, friendships, and relationships. Zendaya is a talented actress and singer who has appeared in films like The Greatest Showman and Spider-Man: Homecoming.

Asa Butterfield

Asa Butterfield plays the role of the middle child, a shy and introverted boy who is often overshadowed by his siblings. Butterfield is a rising star in Hollywood, with credits that include Hugo, Ender's Game, and Sex Education.

Millie Bobby Brown

Millie Bobby Brown plays the role of the youngest daughter, a precocious and energetic girl who is always looking for new adventures. Brown is best known for her breakout role as Eleven in the hit Netflix series Stranger Things.
