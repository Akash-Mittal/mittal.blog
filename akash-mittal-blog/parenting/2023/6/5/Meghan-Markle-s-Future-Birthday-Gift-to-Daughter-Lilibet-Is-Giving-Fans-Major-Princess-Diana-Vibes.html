Meghan Markle's Future Birthday Gift to Daughter Lilibet Is Giving Fans Major Princess Diana Vibes

It's no secret that Meghan Markle is all about honoring the memory of Princess Diana, especially when it comes to her children. Recently, sources close to the Duchess of Sussex have revealed that she has a future birthday gift in mind for her now two-month-old daughter Lilibet that is giving fans major Princess Diana vibes.

According to sources, Markle plans to give Lilibet a special piece of jewelry on her 18th birthday. The piece in question is reportedly a diamond-encrusted bracelet that belonged to Princess Diana, which Markle was gifted by Prince Harry after their engagement.

This gesture is not surprising coming from Markle, who has been very vocal about her admiration for the late Princess Diana, and has even worn some of her jewelry in the past. But what is it about this gift that is giving fans major Princess Diana vibes?

  1. Legacy: Princess Diana was known for her humanitarian work, and her legacy has continued through her sons, Prince Harry and Prince William, who have both carried on her work through their own charitable organizations.
  2. Jewelry: Princess Diana was known for her love of jewelry, and many of her pieces have become iconic, such as her sapphire engagement ring, which now belongs to the Duchess of Cambridge.
  3. Motherhood: Princess Diana was a devoted mother to her sons, and Markle seems to be following in her footsteps, even naming her daughter Lilibet after Queen Elizabeth II, who was a mother to four children herself.

These examples highlight the ways in which Princess Diana's memory has continued to live on, and how Markle is honoring her legacy in her own way.
