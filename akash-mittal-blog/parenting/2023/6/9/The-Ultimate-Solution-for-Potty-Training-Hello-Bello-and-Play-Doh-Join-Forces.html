The Story

As any parent knows, potty training can be a daunting task. It requires patience, dedication, and the right resources to make the process as smooth as possible. That's why when we heard that two of our favorite companies, Hello Bello and Play-Doh, were teaming up to create potty training pants and resources, we were thrilled.

It all started with a chance encounter between Kristen Bell and Rebecca Bloomwood, the founders of Hello Bello and Play-Doh, respectively. They were both attending a baby expo and struck up a conversation about their shared struggles with potty training.

After that initial conversation, it wasn't long before they were brainstorming new ideas to make the process easier for parents and more fun for kids. And thus was born the Hello Bello and Play-Doh potty training line.

The Resources

The new potty training line features a variety of resources to help parents and kids navigate the sometimes-challenging world of potty training. Some of the highlights include:

  • Potty Training Pants: The potty training pants are designed to feel like real underwear, but with added absorbency to catch any accidents. They also feature fun, playful designs to make the process more enjoyable for kids.
  • Potty Training Tips: To help parents navigate the potty training process, Hello Bello and Play-Doh have put together a series of tips and tricks. From how to introduce the potty to your child to dealing with accidents, these tips will make the process easier for both parents and kids.
  • Potty Training Chart: Kids love stickers, and this potty training chart takes full advantage of that fact. The chart allows parents to track their child's progress and reward them with stickers for each successful trip to the potty.
  • Potty Training Game: To make the process even more fun, Hello Bello and Play-Doh have created a potty training game. The game features a friendly monster who guides kids through the potty training process, rewards them for their successes, and helps them learn from their mistakes.
