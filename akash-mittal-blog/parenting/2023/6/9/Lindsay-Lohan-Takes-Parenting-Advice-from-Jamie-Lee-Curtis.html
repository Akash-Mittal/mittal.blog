Once upon a time, Lindsay Lohan was known more for her partying and troubled behavior than her parenting skills. However, that all changed when she met Jamie Lee Curtis, who became a mentor and friend to the actress.

It was during a time when Lohan was struggling with her own personal issues, including alcohol and drug addiction, and she knew she needed help to be a better parent to her two children, Aliana and Cody.

Enter Curtis, who not only played Lohan's mother in the 2003 film "Freaky Friday," but also saw the potential in the troubled star and offered her guidance and support.

"She's really taken me under her wing," Lohan said of Curtis in an interview with People magazine. "She always tries to give me parental advice. She's really amazing."

So, what kind of parenting advice has Curtis been offering Lohan? Here are some quantifiable examples:

1. Prioritize Your Children

Curtis has stressed the importance of putting her children first, no matter what. Lohan has taken this advice to heart, and has made a concerted effort to be there for her children, even amid her busy work schedule.

For example, Lohan recently turned down a lucrative offer to appear on a reality TV show in order to spend more time with her kids. "My children are my life, and I want to be the best mother I can be for them," she told People.

2. Practice Good Self-Care

In addition to emphasizing the importance of being there for her children, Curtis has also stressed the importance of taking care of oneself in order to be a better parent.

Lohan has taken this advice to heart as well, and has made a concerted effort to prioritize self-care, including regular exercise and therapy sessions, in order to be in the best possible mental and physical health for her children.

3. Lead by Example

Curtis has also stressed the importance of leading by example, both in terms of behavior and attitudes.

Lohan has taken this advice to heart as well, and has worked hard to model positive behavior for her children, including healthy communication and conflict resolution skills.

Overall, Lohan credits Curtis with helping her become a better parent and person, and is grateful for the guidance and support she has received.

Conclusion:

In conclusion, the lessons that Lindsay Lohan has learned from Jamie Lee Curtis are simple, yet invaluable.

First, prioritize your children and always strive to be there for them, no matter what.

Second, practice good self-care in order to be the best parent you can be.

Finally, lead by example and model positive behavior for your children.

By following these simple yet powerful lessons, Lohan has become a better parent and a stronger person.
