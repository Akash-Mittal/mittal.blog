Jennifer Lawrence Compares Her Parenting Style to These Reality TV Stars!



Image Credits https://www.etonline.com/media/videos/jennifer-lawrence-compares-her-parenting-style-to-these-reality-tv-stars-exclusive

Jennifer Lawrence is known for her witty remarks, funny jokes, and unique parenting style. Recently, she sat down with ET and revealed that she models her parenting style after certain reality TV stars.

One day, while Jennifer was on a flight, she encountered a crying baby. The mother was doing her best to calm the child down, but nothing seemed to work. That's when Jennifer stepped in and offered to hold the baby. Miraculously, the baby stopped crying and fell asleep in Jennifer's arms.

This incident made Jennifer realize that she has a way with children and her parenting style might just work for her.

Jennifer Lawrence models her parenting style after three reality TV stars - Kim Kardashian, Kris Jenner, and Bethenny Frankel.

  • Kim Kardashian - Jennifer loves how Kim balances her work life and family life. She admires how Kim is always present for her children, even with her busy schedule.
  • Kris Jenner - Jennifer loves how Kris always puts her family first. She admires how Kris is always there for her children, no matter what.
  • Bethenny Frankel - Jennifer loves how Bethenny is a single mother who manages to juggle her career, her child, and her personal life. She admires how Bethenny shows her daughter that it's possible to have it all.

Jennifer Lawrence's Unique Parenting Style: Modeled After Reality TV Stars

  1. Jennifer Lawrence has a unique parenting style that works for her.

  3. Jennifer offers a fresh perspective on motherhood and parenting.

Jennifer shared a personal story with ET about how she handles her son's tantrums. Whenever her son throws a tantrum, she puts on loud music and starts dancing. Her son always joins in and the tantrum is diffused. Jennifer says that this is her secret weapon for handling tantrums and it works every time.
