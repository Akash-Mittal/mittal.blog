Kelly Clarkson Talks Parenting On Glennon Doyle's Podcast



Image Credits https://www.today.com/parents/moms/kelly-clarkson-parenting-divorce-glennon-doyle-rcna89112

Many parents look up to Kelly Clarkson as a role model. She seems to have it all: a successful music career, a popular daytime talk show, and four children to raise. However, parenting is not always easy and Clarkson recently shared some of her struggles and insights on Glennon Doyle's "We Can Do Hard Things" podcast.

  • Clarkson has four children, two of whom she gave birth to and two who she adopted. This reflects the growing trend of parents adopting children in addition to having biological children.
  • Clarkson talked about the importance of prioritizing self-care as a parent. This is a key factor in preventing burnout which can lead to mental health issues like depression and anxiety.
  • Clarkson shared her experience dealing with postpartum depression after the birth of her second child. This is an important issue that affects many new mothers and needs to be addressed more openly.

Practical Tips

In addition to sharing her personal experiences, Clarkson also gave some practical tips for parenting:

  • Set boundaries with your children. This includes setting limits on screen time, bedtime, and other activities.
  • Be open and honest with your children about your feelings. This can help normalize emotions and show that it's okay to express them.
  • Make time for self-care. This can be as simple as taking a walk or reading a book, but it's important to prioritize your own mental health.

  1. Parenting is not always easy and even successful celebrities like Kelly Clarkson struggle with it.
  2. Adopting children is a growing trend that reflects the changing nature of family structures.
  3. Prioritizing self-care and setting boundaries are important for parents to prevent burnout and maintain their mental health.
