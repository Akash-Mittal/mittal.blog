Ryan Reynolds & Blake Lively's Kids Get a Shout Out From Taylor Swift

It's always heartwarming to see celebrities supporting each other, but it's even more special when they acknowledge each other's children. That's exactly what Taylor Swift did recently when she sent a sweet gift and a special shout out to Ryan Reynolds and Blake Lively's kids.

The famous couple has three daughters together, James, Inez and Betty. Taylor Swift is a close friend of Ryan Reynolds and Blake Lively, and she took the time to send a custom made "Folklore" cardigan to each of their children. The cardigans even had the initials of each girl on them, making the gift extra personal. Taylor included a handwritten note in each package, which read "For Betty, James, and Inez. I adore you. Taylor."

This sweet gesture from Taylor Swift is just one example of how celebrities can use their platform to bring joy and positivity to others.
