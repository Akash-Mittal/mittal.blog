To Build Open Source Generative AI Models, Together Raises 20M

Discover how Together is bringing innovative AI technology to the open source community!

Why Generative AI Models Are Game-Changing

Artificial intelligence is rapidly advancing, and one of the most exciting developments in the field is generative AI models. Rather than simply analyzing data to uncover patterns and make predictions, generative models can create entirely new data that is similar to what it has been trained on.

Generative AI models have huge potential for a variety of industries, from music and art to medicine and finance. For example, generative models can:

  • Compose original songs, paintings, and stories
  • Create realistic images and video based on a text description
  • Generate medical images to help identify diseases before they become life-threatening
  • Develop financial models that can predict stock prices and market trends

However, one of the biggest challenges with generative AI models has been their lack of accessibility to the wider open source community. Until now.

Introducing Together: A Revolution in AI Technology

Together is a new company that has recently raised $20 million in funding to create open source generative AI models. Their mission is to democratize the field of AI and enable anyone, regardless of their expertise or resources, to create and use generative models.

How It Works

Together's platform consists of a suite of tools and resources that make it easy for anyone to create, train, and deploy generative AI models. This includes:

  • A user-friendly interface for designing and creating models
  • A library of pre-trained models that can be customized for specific use cases
  • A large database of training data for a variety of industries and applications
  • A community forum for sharing knowledge and getting support

With Together, users can easily create their own generative AI models and integrate them into their existing workflows and technologies. This opens up a whole world of possibilities for businesses, artists, scientists, and anyone else who wants to explore the power of generative models.

Examples of Success

Already, Together has seen some impressive examples of success from users who have created their own generative models and integrated them into their workflows. Here are just a few:

  1. Music Composition: A startup called Amper Music used Together to create an AI-powered music composition tool that allows users to create original songs without any music theory knowledge. The tool has been used to create music for hit TV shows and movies, and has raised $20 million in funding.
  2. Art Restoration: The Mauritshuis museum in the Netherlands used Together to develop a generative model that can digitally restore damaged masterpieces. They were able to restore a painting that was previously considered unsalvageable, and the project has gained international recognition.
  3. Fashion Design: Designer Pinar Yanardag used Together to create a generative model that can design clothing based on a person's body shape, preferences, and other factors. The model was used to create a line of custom-fit dresses that have been featured in major fashion publications.

Conclusion: Why Together Is a Game-Changer in AI Technology

Here are three key takeaways from Together's innovative platform:

  • Accessibility: By providing a suite of tools and resources that make it easy for anyone to create and use generative AI models, Together is democratizing the field of AI and making it accessible to a wider audience.
  • Creativity: Generative AI models have the potential to radically transform industries as diverse as music and medicine, and Together is helping to unlock that potential by empowering users to create their own unique models.
  • Collaboration: By creating an open source community around generative AI models, Together is encouraging collaboration and knowledge-sharing, which will only lead to more innovation and creativity in the field.

Overall, Together's platform is a game-changer in AI technology, and has the potential to unlock new levels of creativity and innovation for businesses, artists, scientists, and anyone else who wants to explore the power of generative models.
