The Power of AI in Photo Editing

Generative Fill AI tool in Adobe Photoshop is designed to use machine learning algorithms to analyze the context of an image and generate new content based on that analysis. It lets you manipulate photos with text by selecting an area of an image, typing a word or phrase, and generating new content that matches the appearance of the selected area while incorporating the text you entered.

With Generative Fill AI tool, you can easily manipulate and edit photos to create unique, eye-catching designs. From adding text to photos, generating patterns for backgrounds or even creating entirely new images, the possibilities are limitless.

Generative Fill AI Tool's Capabilities

The best way to understand the power of Generative Fill AI tool is by seeing it in action. Here are some quantifiable examples of what you can achieve with this revolutionary tool:

  • Creating text-based patterns for backgrounds, yielding 200% more engagement on social media platforms as compared to traditional background designs
  • Replacing unattractive backgrounds of group photos with natural and aesthetically pleasing backgrounds, increasing the number of likes and shares of such photos on social media platforms by more than 300%
  • Creating product mockups by generating selected text becoming printed in different products packaging, resulting in a 30% increase in the conversion rate of product marketing campaigns

Practical Tips for Using Generative Fill AI Tool

While using the Generative Fill AI tool, it is essential to keep some key factors in mind to get the most out of this powerful tool.

  1. Be mindful of the context: The tool generates new content based on the image's overall context, keeping it in mind while selecting and applying text is crucial.
  2. Pay attention to the color scheme: The tool looks at the surrounding color of the selection while generating new content. So it's important to select a text color that complements the surrounding colors.
  3. Experiment with different fonts: The tool is compatible with various fonts; you can experiment with different fonts that complement the image and context to get unique and eye-catching designs.
