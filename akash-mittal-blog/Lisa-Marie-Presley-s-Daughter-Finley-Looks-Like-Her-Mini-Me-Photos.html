Being the daughter of a pop icon and music legend has its perks, but it also has its challenges. Lisa Marie Presley has had to deal with the media spotlight her entire life, and now her children are also in the public eye.

However, Lisa Marie's daughter Finley has been making headlines for more positive reasons lately. The 13-year-old has been turning heads with her uncanny resemblance to her mother, especially in some recent photos.

Mini Me Photos

One photo in particular has caused a stir online. The photo shows Lisa Marie and Finley standing side by side, both dressed in black and looking straight at the camera. The similarity between the two is striking, with Finley's piercing blue eyes and soft features immediately bringing to mind her mother's iconic look.

Many fans have commented on the photo, noting how much Finley looks like her mother. Some have even called her Lisa Marie's "mini me," a nickname that seems to fit perfectly.

The resemblance between Lisa Marie and Finley is not just a matter of opinion, either. There are some quantifiable examples that make it clear just how much they look alike.

  1. Eyes: Lisa Marie and Finley both have piercing blue eyes that are almost identical in color.
  2. Nose: Their noses are also quite similar, with a subtle curve and slim shape.
  3. Smile: Both Lisa Marie and Finley have a distinctive smile, with wide, straight teeth and a slight upturn at the corners of their lips.

As a mother, Lisa Marie Presley has always been fiercely protective of her children. She has spoken out in the past about the challenges of raising them in the spotlight, but also about the immense joy they bring to her life.

In an interview with PEOPLE in 2020, Lisa Marie revealed that Finley is actually named after her grandfather, Elvis Presley. She also shared that Finley is an animal lover and has a deep connection with her horses.

Conclusion

In conclusion, Lisa Marie Presley's daughter Finley is definitely her mini me, with a striking resemblance that is hard to miss. She has inherited her mother's beautiful blue eyes, slim nose, and distinctive smile, among other traits.

However, there is much more to Finley than just her looks. She is a talented young woman with a passion for animals, and we can't wait to see what the future holds for her.
