How 9 Years of PM Modi Took India from Developing Nation to Democracy Worthy of Emulation

  • Interesting Story
  • Quantifiable Examples
  • Practical Tips
  • Conclusion

When Narendra Modi took over as the Prime Minister of India in May 2014, he inherited a country that was struggling with a host of socio-economic problems. India was ranked 134 out of 187 countries in the UNDP's Human Development Index, and its position in various other international rankings was also dismal.

Modi had a clear vision for India and wasted no time in implementing it. His efforts over the last 9 years have transformed India from a developing nation to a democracy worthy of emulation by others.

Under PM Modi's leadership, India has made significant progress in various spheres:

  • India's GDP growth rate has been consistently high, and it is now the world's sixth-largest economy in terms of nominal GDP.
  • The government has launched several schemes aimed at improving the lives of the poor and marginalized sections of society. The Pradhan Mantri Jan Dhan Yojana, which aims to provide every household with a bank account, is one such example.
  • The Digital India initiative has brought about a digital revolution in the country and has significantly increased access to the internet and digital services.
  • The Swachh Bharat Abhiyan, which aims to make India clean and free from open defecation, has been successful in improving sanitation in the country.

These are just a few examples of the progress that India has made under PM Modi's leadership. His government has also initiated several reforms aimed at streamlining the bureaucratic process, improving infrastructure, and attracting foreign investment.

Practical Tips

If you are an aspiring leader who wishes to bring about positive change in your own country, here are some practical tips that you can learn from PM Modi:

  • Have a clear vision for your country and work tirelessly to achieve it.
  • Initiate reforms that are aimed at improving the lives of the people, especially the poor and marginalized sections of society.
  • Use technology and innovation to bring about a digital revolution in your country.
  • Focus on improving infrastructure and attracting foreign investment.
  • Streamline the bureaucratic process and reduce corruption.

Conclusion

PM Modi's 9-year tenure as the Prime Minister of India has been transformative for the country. His clear vision and tireless efforts have led to significant progress in various spheres, and India is now a democracy worthy of emulation by others. To bring about positive change in your own country, follow the practical tips outlined above.
