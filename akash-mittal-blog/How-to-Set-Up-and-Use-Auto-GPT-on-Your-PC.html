What is Auto GPT?

Auto GPT is a partition manager that allows you to easily partition and format your hard drive, and set up GPT without the need for complicated command-line prompts. It is a simple yet powerful tool that helps to streamline the process of setting up and managing partitions on your computer.

Auto GPT simplifies this process by automating many of the technical steps involved, and providing a user-friendly interface that makes it easy to manage your partitions. It can also resize and extend partitions, making it a powerful tool for managing your disk space efficiently.
