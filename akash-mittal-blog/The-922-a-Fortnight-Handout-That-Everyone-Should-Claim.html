Quantifiable Examples

According to the Australian Government's official website, over 4.5 million people received the COVID-19 Disaster Payment between August and September 2021. This payment was designed to support people who lost work due to lockdowns and restrictions that impacted their ability to earn an income. On average, eligible recipients could receive $300 to $750 per week, depending on their circumstances.

However, despite the widespread availability of this payment, there are still many Australians who fail to claim it. In fact, some reports show that up to 30% of eligible recipients have not yet received their payment.
