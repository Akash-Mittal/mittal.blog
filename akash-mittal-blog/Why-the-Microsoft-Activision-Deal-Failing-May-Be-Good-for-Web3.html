The Microsoft-Activision Deal and Web3

So, how does this relate to the Microsoft-Activision deal?

One theory is that Microsoft may have been hesitant to embrace Web3 technologies, which could have clashed with Activision's focus on more traditional gaming models. By acquiring Activision, Microsoft would have essentially been doubling down on centralized gaming, while Web3 technologies continued to gain popularity.

If the deal does end up falling through, it could be seen as a win for Web3 advocates. It would show that centralized entities are not the only way forward for gaming, and that there is real demand for decentralized, community-driven models.
