SHIB: A Quick Introduction

SHIB is a cryptocurrency that was launched in August 2020 by an anonymous developer who goes by the name "Ryoshi." This coin was inspired by Dogecoin and named after the famous Japanese dog breed. Its aim was to serve as a joke currency, but it was far from being a laughing matter. In fact, SHIB quickly became one of the hottest cryptocurrencies on the market, gaining immense popularity and attention from investors.

SHIB's Price Trajectory

SHIB's price has been anything but predictable. In May 2021, the currency's value soared by more than 1,000% in just a few weeks. This was courtesy of a mention from Tesla's CEO, Elon Musk, who tweeted a picture of his own Shiba Inu dog, named Floki, and said he's getting a new adoptable puppy and that Shiba Inu is the future. Bitcoin had never seen anything quite like this.

However, in June 2021, the bubble that skyrocketed the value of cryptocurrencies, especially Shiba Inu, burst with Bitcoin losing about 50% of its value. SHIB was not left out of this wave of losses as it shed about 50% of its total value. This was a clear example of how volatile cryptocurrencies could be.
