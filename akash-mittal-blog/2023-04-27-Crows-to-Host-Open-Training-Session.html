Crows to Host Open Training Session

The Adelaide Football Club (AFC) has announced that they will be holding an open training session on the 15th of August at the Adelaide Oval for their loyal supporters.

This is a great opportunity for fans to witness their favorite players from the Crows up close and in action. Attendees of the session will also have the chance to meet and greet the players and take photos with them.

Why attend this open training session?

  • You get to see the Crows in action: This is a rare chance for fans to watch their favorite players in training and get a glimpse of the team's tactics and strategies.
  • You get to meet the players: Fans can meet and greet the players, take photos with them, and even get autographs.
  • Fun for the whole family: The open training session is a family-friendly event, with activities for kids and food available for purchase.

If you're a Crows fan, this is an event you won't want to miss! Be sure to mark your calendars for August 15th and head to Adelaide Oval for an unforgettable day of football.
