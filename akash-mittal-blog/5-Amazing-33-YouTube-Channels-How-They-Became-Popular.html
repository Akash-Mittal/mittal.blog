Introduction

Have you ever thought about what you can do in 33 minutes? You can probably cook a meal, watch an episode of your favorite TV show, or even take a power nap. But what if I tell you that you can learn something new, feel inspired, or have a good laugh in just 33 minutes by watching incredible YouTube channels? Here are 5 amazing 33 YouTube channels that became popular with their unique content and style.

1. Vox - Informative Journalism

Vox is a news and information website that has gained popularity for its data-driven journalism and explanatory videos. Vox started as an American news and opinion website but later expanded to video content and podcasting.

One of their famous series is called "Explained," where they give context and background behind different topics such as the stock market, the science of sleep, or the history of the world's water crisis. They use engaging visuals, animations, and interviews to make complex ideas more accessible and understandable in just 33 minutes.

Example: The 2019 Hong Kong Protests Explained in 18 Minutes - https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=GX6F5f-89ZE

2. Bon Appétit - Mouth-Watering Culinary Experience

Bon Appétit is a popular food and cooking magazine that has an amazing YouTube channel with over 7 million subscribers, thanks to their fun and entertaining videos about food and cooking.

They have a series called "Test Kitchen," where their chefs and editors cook and explore different recipes and techniques while having a casual conversation and lots of humor. They also have videos that feature celebrities cooking and trying new things, and food experts sharing their wisdom and tips.

Example: Pastry Chef Attempts to Make Gourmet Sour Patch Kids - https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=TgRmGk_4HyE

3. TED - Inspiring Talks

TED (Technology, Entertainment, Design) is a nonprofit organization that spreads ideas and knowledge through engaging talks, conferences, and events. TED started as a conference where leading experts and intellectuals shared their ideas and insights but later transformed into a digital platform with millions of viewers worldwide.

TED talks cover various topics such as science, business, social issues, creativity, and personal growth. They aim to inspire, educate, and entertain their audience with unique perspectives and engaging stories in just 33 minutes.

Example: How to Make Stress Your Friend by Kelly McGonigal - https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=RcGyVTAoXEU

4. Safiya Nygaard - Creative and Funny Fashion

Safiya Nygaard is a YouTuber and former BuzzFeed video producer who has gained popularity for her creative and experimental fashion videos. She often explores different fashion trends, styles, and cultures in a fun and entertaining way.

She also has a series called "Wearing $5 Clothes from Wish for a Week," where she orders and tests cheap clothes from the popular online store "Wish" and documents her experience and reactions. Her humor, creativity, and personality make her videos enjoyable and relatable for many viewers.

Example: Going to the WORST Reviewed Nail Salon in my City (1 star) - https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=GVErcy6AQmY

5. The Slow Mo Guys - Fascinating Slow-Motion Videos

The Slow Mo Guys are a British duo who create slow-motion videos of various objects and phenomena by using high-speed cameras. They started their channel in 2010 and have amassed over 14 million subscribers with their fascinating and mesmerizing videos.

They often explore science experiments, explosions, stunts, and nature in slow motion, showing the hidden beauty and detail of things that are too fast or small for the naked eye to see. Their videos are both entertaining and educational, making science and physics more accessible and fascinating to the audience.

Example: Rainbow Jelly Tennis in Slow Motion - The Slow Mo Guys - https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ImhxihkUqBw

Conclusion

These 5 33 YouTube channels are just a few examples of how content creators can entertain, educate, and inspire viewers in just 33 minutes. By using creativity, humor, and expertise, they have managed to attract millions of subscribers and fans who eagerly await their next videos.

If you want to learn new things, try new recipes, explore different cultures, or simply enjoy some entertaining content, these YouTube channels are worth checking out.
