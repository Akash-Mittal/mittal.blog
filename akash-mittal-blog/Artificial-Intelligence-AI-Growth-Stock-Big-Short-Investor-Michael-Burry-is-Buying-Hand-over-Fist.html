AI Growth Stock

According to a report by MarketsandMarkets, the AI market is expected to grow from $21.5 billion in 2018 to $190.6 billion by 2025. This is a Compound Annual Growth Rate (CAGR) of 36.6% during the forecast period. The report also indicates that the North American region is expected to play a significant role in the AI market's growth due to the high adoption of AI technology in various industries.

Some of the top AI stocks in the market right now include NVIDIA Corporation (NVDA), Alphabet Inc. (GOOGL), International Business Machines Corporation (IBM), and Microsoft Corporation (MSFT).
