Tony Prochaska: Former Player

After graduating from Grand Ledge High School, Tony went on to play college baseball at Alma College, where he continued to excel both on and off the field. Dave Stapleton: Coach

Dave Stapleton is a former coach who has spent over 20 years dedicating his time and expertise to the Grand Ledge Baseball Association. Dave started coaching in the 1980s when his sons were young, and he quickly fell in love with the game. Over the years, he has coached youth teams at all levels, and he has helped mold countless young athletes into outstanding players and individuals.

As a coach, Dave was known for his calm demeanor and his ability to connect with his players. Dave's dedication to the game and to his players did not go unnoticed, and he was a beloved figure within the Grand Ledge baseball community.

Tom Sietsema: Community Leader
