An interesting story

Have you ever scrolled through your TikTok feed and seen videos that look like they came straight out of a comic book? If you have, you've probably witnessed the latest trend on the app: the AI Webtoon filter. This filter uses artificial intelligence to transform your face into a cartoon character, complete with bold lines and vivid colors. It's fun, creative, and it's taking over TikTok.

The AI Webtoon filter has been around for a while, but it has recently gained popularity thanks to TikTok users sharing their videos and challenging others to do the same. You can find thousands of videos using the filter by searching for the hashtag #aiwebtoonfilter or #webtoonchallenge. And the best part? It's easy to do!

Practical tips

So how can you join the trend and do the AI Webtoon filter? Here are some practical tips:

  1. Open TikTok and tap on the plus sign to create a new video.
  2. Tap on the effects icon and search for "AI Webtoon" or "Webtoon."
  3. Select the filter and adjust it to your liking.
  4. Record your video and share it with the hashtag #aiwebtoonfilter or #webtoonchallenge.
  5. Challenge your friends to do the same!

It's that simple! You can also experiment with different poses, outfits, and backgrounds to make your video more unique and creative.
