Kim Kardashian Admits She Cries Herself to Sleep Due to Parenting Struggles

Read about Kim Kardashian's struggles as a parent and how she copes with them.

Being a parent is not an easy job, and it's no different for celebrities like Kim Kardashian. In a recent interview, the reality TV star opened up about her struggles as a parent and how they keep her up at night.

With four children aged between 1 and 7 years old, Kim has her hands full. She has talked openly about the challenges of balancing her career and family life, and how she copes with the stresses that come with being a mom.

Parenting Struggles

Kim admits that there are times when she finds parenting overwhelming and it gets her down. She says that she often cries herself to sleep when she's feeling particularly stressed. This is something that many parents can relate to, as raising children can be exhausting both physically and emotionally.

Kim says that one of the hardest things about being a parent is trying to meet everyone's needs and keep everyone happy. She says that her children have different personalities and different needs, and it can be a challenge to manage all of them at the same time.

"You want to give your kids everything, but sometimes it's just not possible," she says. "It's tough seeing your kids struggle or feel left out, and you can't always fix it."

Practical Tips

Despite the challenges, Kim says that she's found some ways to cope with the stresses of parenting. Here are some of her tips:

  • Take time for yourself
  • Put your phone away
  • Delegate tasks
  • Be present in the moment

Kim says that taking time for herself is essential for her mental health. She likes to do things like go for a walk, meditate, or read a book. By taking some time for herself, she's better able to cope with the demands of parenting.

She also advises putting your phone away when you're spending time with your kids. "It's easy to get distracted by social media or emails, but your kids deserve your full attention when you're with them," she says.

Delegating tasks is another way to reduce stress, according to Kim. She says that she often enlists the help of her husband or a nanny to get things done. By sharing the load, she's able to spend more quality time with her kids.

Finally, Kim emphasizes the importance of being present in the moment. She says that it's easy to get caught up in the future or the past, but it's important to focus on what's happening right now. By doing this, she's able to fully enjoy the time she spends with her children.

Conclusion

Kim Kardashian may be a celebrity, but she faces many of the same challenges as any other parent. She admits to struggling at times, but she's found some ways to cope with the stresses of parenting.

Whether you're a celebrity or an average Joe, being a parent is never easy. But by taking some time for yourself, putting your phone away, delegating tasks, and being present in the moment, you can make the job a little more manageable.
