Web 3.0 Blockchain Market Poised to Reach USD 11.33 Billion in 2022

It was a quiet Sunday morning when John, a small business owner, received a notification that his online store was being subjected to a Distributed Denial of Service (DDoS) attack. Despite having security measures in place, John's website was bombarded with traffic that made it impossible for his customers to access the site, resulting in a significant loss for his business. What John needed was a system that could ensure the safety and security of his online store, and that's when he learned about blockchain.

Blockchain technology, also known as Web 3.0, is a decentralized system that provides a secure and transparent way of accessing and sharing data. The technology uses cryptography to create a ledger that is virtually impossible to hack, making it a reliable solution for individuals and businesses alike.

Blockchain Success

  • According to a report by MarketsandMarkets, the global blockchain market is projected to reach USD 11.33 billion by 2022, growing at a CAGR of 79.6%.
  • The healthcare industry has seen a significant impact from blockchain technology, with companies like Guardtime and PokitDok using the technology to streamline medical billing and secure patient data.
  • Walmart implemented a blockchain system to track the origin of its products, resulting in a 50% reduction in the time it takes to trace the source of contaminated food products.
