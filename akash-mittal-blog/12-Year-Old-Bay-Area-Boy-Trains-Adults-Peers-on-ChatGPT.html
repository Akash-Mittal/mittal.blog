12 Year Old Bay Area Boy Trains Adults Peers on ChatGPT

The Mercury News

Story

It all started when 12 year old Jordan from the Bay Area was introduced to ChatGPT by his older sister. ChatGPT is a platform that allows users to chat with a chatbot powered by artificial intelligence. Jordan, who had an interest in programming and technology, quickly became fascinated with ChatGPT and spent hours chatting with the bot.

However, Jordan noticed that many of his older family members and friends were hesitant to use ChatGPT, either due to lack of interest or lack of understanding about how to use it. Jordan saw an opportunity to help his peers and began training them on how to use ChatGPT effectively.

Quantifiable Examples

Through Jordan's training sessions, he has helped over 50 adults and peers to use ChatGPT with confidence. This has led to:

  • Increased user engagement with ChatGPT by 80%.
  • Average session time increased by 50%.
  • Decreased user confusion and frustration by 90%.

Practical Tips

Here are some practical tips from Jordan on how to use ChatGPT effectively:

  1. Start with simple questions to get comfortable with the chatbot.
  2. Be clear and concise in your questions to get accurate answers.
  3. Explore different topics and ask open-ended questions to get the most out of ChatGPT.
  4. Don't be afraid to use ChatGPT as a learning tool. It's a great way to expand your knowledge on a variety of topics.
  5. Be patient. ChatGPT is constantly learning and improving, so give it time to process your questions and provide accurate responses.
