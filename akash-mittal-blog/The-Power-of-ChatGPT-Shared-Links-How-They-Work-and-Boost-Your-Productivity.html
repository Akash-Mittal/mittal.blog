How ChatGPT Shared Links Work

ChatGPT Shared Links are curated links to online content, such as articles, blog posts, videos, or podcasts, that have been hand-picked and shared by someone you trust or find valuable. They are shared on messaging platforms like Slack, Discord or even WhatsApp groups, and they allow you to share relevant content with a specific audience.

Unlike social media, where you're bombarded with posts from every account you follow, ChatGPT Shared Links are targeted to the specific interests of a group. This not only helps you save time and avoid distractions but it also allows you to engage in more meaningful conversations.

When you come across an interesting piece of content that you want to share with a specific audience, you simply copy the link and share it on your chat group. Your audience can then click on the link and engage with the content, leaving comments, asking questions, and sharing their own thoughts.

Practical Tips for Using ChatGPT Shared Links

Now that you understand how ChatGPT Shared Links work and their benefits, here are some practical tips for using them effectively:

  • Curate your links carefully: Only share content that's relevant to your audience and that you believe will add value to their lives or work.
  • Create different groups: Depending on your audience, create different groups with specific interests or goals. This will help you share more targeted content.
  • Encourage conversations: Once you've shared a link, encourage your audience to engage by asking thought-provoking questions or sharing your own thoughts.
  • Be consistent: Regularly share links with your audience to keep them engaged and informed.
  • Respect others' time and space: If you're sharing a link with someone, make sure they're interested in receiving it. Don't spam them with irrelevant content.
