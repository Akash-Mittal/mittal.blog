Web3 Conference: Gathering Web3 Builders and Creators in Amsterdam

Web3 Conference is one of the biggest conferences for web3 builders and creators around the world. Every year, hundreds of developers, entrepreneurs, investors, and enthusiasts gather to discuss the latest trends, showcase their projects, and network with like-minded individuals.

The conference provides a platform to showcase innovative projects that are transforming the web3 ecosystem. This year, the conference will be held in Amsterdam, one of the most vibrant cities in Europe, from May 17 to May 19.

One of the real-world examples of a project that will be showcased at the conference is Proton Chain, a high-speed blockchain designed to enable faster and more secure transactions. The blockchain aims to solve the limitations of existing blockchains like slow transaction speeds and high fees. Proton Chain has already partnered with major companies like Samsung Blockchain, Binance, and Wanchain.

Another project that will be showcased at the conference is Veil, a decentralized prediction market platform built on top of the Ethereum blockchain. Veil allows users to create and participate in prediction markets for events like elections, sports games, and more. The platform has gained popularity among crypto enthusiasts, and has already been used to predict the outcome of the 2020 U.S. presidential election.

The conference will also feature keynote speakers from major companies in the web3 space, including Ethereum, Chainlink, and Polkadot.
