A chilling tale of horror

ChatGPT, an online platform for chatting, has recently spooked the internet with its 2-line horror story alert. People all around the world are trembling with fear after reading those short but scary messages. Here's an example:

"I can hear my mom calling my name from downstairs. The only problem is, my mom died 3 years ago."

These stories may be short, but they can send shivers down your spine. ChatGPT has always been known for its unique and creative content, and this horror story alert is no exception.
