Practical Tips

If you find yourself in a difficult co-parenting relationship, there are things you can do to make it easier:

  • Put your children's needs first. Remember that your children are the most important thing in this situation, and prioritize their well-being.
  • Communicate effectively. Keep communication open and respectful. If you can't communicate effectively, consider using a mediator or counselor.
  • Be willing to compromise. Co-parenting requires a lot of compromise. Be willing to work with your co-parent to find solutions that work for everyone.
  • Stay positive. You may not always agree with your co-parent, but staying positive can help keep your relationship on good terms.
