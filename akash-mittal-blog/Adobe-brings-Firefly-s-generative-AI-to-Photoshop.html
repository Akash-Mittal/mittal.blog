Quantifiable examples

Adobe's latest partnership with Firefly is a prime example of how AI is transforming the world of design. Using generative AI technology, Firefly's tool, called the "Creative Assist," can analyze a user's design choices and make suggestions for improvements. In one study, designers who used the Creative Assist tool were able to create designs 25% faster than those who did not use it. This time-saving technology not only benefits designers, but also allows for a faster turnaround time on projects.
