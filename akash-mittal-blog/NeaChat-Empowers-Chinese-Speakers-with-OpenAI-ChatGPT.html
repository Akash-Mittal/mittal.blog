NeaChat Empowers Chinese Speakers with OpenAI ChatGPT

How NeaChat uses OpenAI's ChatGPT version 4 to offer innovative conversational AI solutions for Chinese speakers.

Before the pandemic, Susan used to travel frequently to China for business. As a non-native Mandarin speaker, she often struggled to communicate with her Chinese colleagues and clients. But with the help of NeaChat's conversational AI system powered by OpenAI's ChatGPT, Susan can now chat fluently and confidently in Mandarin with her Chinese counterparts.

NeaChat, a leading conversational AI provider for businesses, releases a new version of its ChatGPT that supports the Chinese language. The company aims to help non-native speakers, like Susan, communicate more effectively in Mandarin.

The integration of ChatGPT with Chinese marks a major milestone for NeaChat. Companies operating globally can now leverage AI technology to eliminate language barriers and improve communication with their Chinese customers and partners.

Real-Life Examples

NeaChat's ChatGPT solution has already impressed some of the biggest names in business. For example, Alibaba, the multinational e-commerce giant, now uses NeaChat's solution to offer 24/7 customer service in Mandarin to its international customers.

Another example is Marriott International, a hospitality company that uses NeaChat's solution to assist front desk staff in communicating with Chinese guests who speak little English. The tested ChatGPT solution speeds up the check-in and check-out process, saves time and improves customer satisfaction.
