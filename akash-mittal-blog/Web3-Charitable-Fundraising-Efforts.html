Web3 Charitable Fundraising Efforts

Have you ever donated to a charity and wondered where exactly your money is going? With traditional charitable organizations, it can be difficult to track how your donations are being used and if they are truly making an impact. This is where Web3 charitable fundraising efforts come into play.

Web3, also known as the decentralized web, is changing the way we think about charitable giving. Using blockchain technology, Web3 allows for greater transparency and accountability in charitable fundraising efforts. When you donate to a charity using Web3, you can track exactly where your money is going and see how it is being used to make a difference.

One example of a Web3 charitable fundraising effort is the Endaoment platform, which allows users to create and donate to donor advised funds (DAFs) using cryptocurrency. DAFs are similar to a charitable savings account, where donors can contribute funds to be used for charitable giving in the future. Endaoment's Web3 platform allows for greater transparency and control over these funds.

Endaoment CEO, Andy Bromberg, explains that "with Endaoment, donors have full transparency and control over their philanthropic capital. They can track the impact of their giving, see how their donations are being used, and even nominate the charities that receive gifts from their donor advised fund."

Web3 charitable fundraising efforts have already made a significant impact in the world of charitable giving. According to a report by Fidelity Charitable, which is the largest donor advised fund sponsor in the United States, there was a 30% increase in cryptocurrency donations from 2018 to 2019.

Another example of the impact of Web3 charitable fundraising can be seen in the COVID-19 pandemic. As the pandemic swept across the globe, many charitable organizations struggled to keep up with the demand for their services. Web3 platforms like Endaoment stepped in to assist with fundraising efforts.

Endaoment launched the COVID-19 Response Fund, which allowed donors to contribute cryptocurrency to aid in COVID-19 relief efforts. The fund has raised over $200,000 worth of cryptocurrency, which has been used to support organizations such as Direct Relief and the World Health Organization.

and Case Studies

One of the unique aspects of Web3 charitable fundraising is the ability for donors to have more control over their charitable giving. Endaoment CEO, Andy Bromberg, shares a personal anecdote about the impact of this control:

"One of our donors had a passion for animal welfare and wanted to support organizations that were making a difference in this area. With Endaoment, they were able to create a donor advised fund specifically for animal welfare and see the impact of their giving in real time. They were able to track how their donations were being used and see the difference they were making in the lives of animals."

Another case study of the impact of Web3 charitable fundraising comes from the international development organization, UNICEF. UNICEF launched a fundraising campaign using cryptocurrency mining in February 2018. The campaign, called "Game Chaingers," allowed individuals to voluntarily donate their computer's unused processing power to mine cryptocurrency for UNICEF France. The funds raised were used to provide children with access to clean water, education, and healthcare.

  2. Using blockchain technology allows for greater transparency and control over charitable giving.
  3. Web3 platforms like Endaoment and UNICEF are making a tangible impact on the world of charitable fundraising.
