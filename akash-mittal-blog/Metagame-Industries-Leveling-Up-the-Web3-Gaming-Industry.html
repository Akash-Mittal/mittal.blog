Metagame Industries: Leveling Up the Web3 Gaming Industry

When it comes to the gaming industry, it's no secret that Web3 technology is changing the game. With the rise of blockchain, NFTs, and cryptocurrency, developers are now able to create games that offer not only immersive gameplay experiences but also ownership and value to their players.

But with the opportunity comes the challenge of navigating the new territory. That's where Metagame Industries comes in. This web3 game studio has been raising the bar for the industry, pushing the boundaries of what's possible and delivering innovative solutions for both creators and players alike.

The Metagame Approach

One of the things that sets Metagame Industries apart is its holistic approach to the gaming industry. Instead of simply focusing on creating games or developing blockchain solutions, they aim to tackle all aspects of the Web3 gaming ecosystem—from game design to tokenization and community building.

This approach is evident in their flagship project, Neon District—a sci-fi RPG game that uses blockchain technology to enable players to own and trade their in-game assets. But Neon District is just the beginning. Metagame Industries has also created the Metaverse Index, a collection of NFTs that represents the value of the Web3 gaming industry as a whole.

The Results

So far, Metagame Industries' approach seems to be paying off. Neon District, which launched in late 2019, has gained a steady following of players and investors who appreciate its innovative gameplay and ownership model. The Metaverse Index, which launched in early 2021, has also seen success, with its value doubling within just a few months of launch.

  • Neon District has over 1,500 active players and has sold over 500 NFTs.
  • The Metaverse Index reached $2 million in market cap within the first month of launch.
  • Metagame Industries has raised over $3 million in funding to date.

The Future

When it comes to the future of the Web3 gaming industry, Metagame Industries is well-positioned to lead the way. With its innovative solutions and collaborative approach, it's paving the path for a more inclusive, decentralized, and rewarding gaming experience for all players.

As CEO Bill Bonney put it, "We're really trying to create a virtual world where people can come together and do things that aren't necessarily possible in the real world. We're hoping that everyone in the world will be involved in the metaverse at some point."

  3. Metagame Industries is paving the way for a more inclusive, decentralized, and rewarding gaming experience for all players.
