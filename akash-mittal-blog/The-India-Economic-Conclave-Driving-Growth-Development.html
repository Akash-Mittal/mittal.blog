The India Economic Conclave – Driving Growth & Development

India Economic Conclave, the annual flagship event of the Times Network, has become one of the most anticipated events of the year for business leaders, policymakers, and thought leaders. The conclave aims to provide a platform for discussions and deliberations on issues of economic development in India.

The two-day event, which will be held on December 17 and 18, will be the 9th edition of the conclave. It will revolve around the theme of "Driving Growth & Development" and is expected to attract over 300 delegates from across the country. The event will feature engaging discussions on a wide range of topics, including policy reforms, economic growth, and innovation.
