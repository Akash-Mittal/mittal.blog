The Real Reason Why ChatGPT Failed to Pass Accounting Exams

A Case Study on the Importance of a Solid Accounting Background for Startups

ChatGPT, a startup that develops chatbots for businesses, made headlines last month when its CEO announced that the company had failed its accounting exams.

Many were puzzled by this news, as ChatGPT had been successful in securing funding from prominent investors and had even been featured in popular tech publications.

But the truth was that ChatGPT's lack of accounting knowledge had been holding the company back from its true potential.
