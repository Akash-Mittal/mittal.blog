The Rise of Web3 and Its Impact on E-Commerce

Web3 is the next generation of the internet that uses decentralized technologies like blockchain to create a more transparent and secure online ecosystem. This means e-commerce companies can create direct transactions with their customers, reducing costs and improving overall efficiency.
