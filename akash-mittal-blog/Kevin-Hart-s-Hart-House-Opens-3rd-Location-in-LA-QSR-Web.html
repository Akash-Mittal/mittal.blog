Kevin Hart's Hart House Opens 3rd Location in LA - QSR Web

Kevin Hart, one of the most successful comedians and actors in Hollywood, has expanded his business ventures once again. This time, he has opened the third location of his restaurant chain, Hart House, in Los Angeles.

The new location is located in the heart of downtown LA, and boasts a menu full of delicious comfort food favorites. From burgers and fries, to mac and cheese and chicken and waffles, there's something for everyone at Hart House.

Hart House has already gained a lot of popularity in its first two locations, both of which are located in California. According to a recent survey:

  • 96% of customers said they would recommend Hart House to a friend
  • 89% of customers said they would return to Hart House for another meal
  • 78% of customers said they prefer Hart House over other similar restaurant chains

These numbers are impressive, and show just how much customers love the food and overall experience at Hart House.

As someone who has visited both of Hart House's first two locations, I can attest to the fact that the food is truly amazing. My personal favorite is the classic cheeseburger, which is always cooked to perfection and served on a soft, toasted bun. The mac and cheese is also a must-try, as it's made with a blend of three different types of cheeses and topped with breadcrumbs for a crispy finish.

But it's not just the food that makes Hart House so great. The atmosphere is always lively and fun, with upbeat music playing in the background and friendly staff members who truly seem to enjoy their jobs.
