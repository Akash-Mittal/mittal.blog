TikTok Testing AI Chatbot Tako: All You Need To Know

TikTok, the popular video sharing app, has been experimenting with a new feature called the Tako chatbot. Tako, which is currently being tested in the app's beta version, is an AI-powered chatbot that will be available to users to answer their queries and provide them with recommendations.

According to reports, the chatbot will be available on the app's main interface and can be accessed through a button or a voice command. The whole idea behind Tako is to provide users with a more personalized experience by understanding their preferences and interests. It is also expected to reduce the number of queries that the app's customer service team receives.

While the Tako chatbot is still in its testing phase, there have been some notable examples of its potential benefits. For instance, a user who was looking for recommendations on how to get more followers on the app was able to receive personalized advice from Tako. Another user who was having trouble with her account was able to get help from the chatbot without having to contact the app's customer service team.

In addition to these examples, a report by TechCrunch suggests that Tako could be a game-changer for TikTok in terms of customer engagement. The report cites the success of other AI-powered chatbots such as KLM's BlueBot, which was able to handle over 2.5 million messages in its first year of operation. With more than 1 billion active users, TikTok has the potential to see similar success with Tako.
