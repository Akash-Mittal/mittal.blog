How a Durham Parent's Book is Helping Other Parents

When Julie Johnson's son was diagnosed with ADHD, she felt lost. She didn't know how to help him, or where to turn for advice. But as she started doing research and talking to other parents in her community, she realized she wasn't alone.

"There are so many parents out there who are struggling with the same issues," Julie says. "I wanted to create a resource that would help them navigate this challenging road."

The Book

The resource Julie created is a book called Parenting ADHD with Wisdom and Grace. The book offers practical advice and tools for parents of children with ADHD, as well as insights and encouragement from parents who have been through similar experiences.

"I wanted to write a book that would have been helpful to me when my son was first diagnosed," Julie says. "It's the book I wish I had back then."

Since its release, the book has received rave reviews from parents and professionals alike.

"I can't thank Julie enough for writing this book," says one parent. "It has completely changed the way I approach parenting my child with ADHD."

Professionals have praised the book for its practical advice and approachable tone.

"As a therapist who specializes in ADHD, I highly recommend this book to my clients," says one professional. "It's an amazing resource for parents who are looking for guidance and support."

The Impact

Since the release of Parenting ADHD with Wisdom and Grace, Julie has been blown away by the impact it has had on parents and families.

  • Over 2,000 copies sold since its release last year
  • Received hundreds of positive reviews on Amazon and Goodreads
  • Julie has been invited to speak at schools and parent groups across the country
  • Parents have reported an increase in confidence and self-esteem, as well as improvements in their child's behavior and academic performance

"It's been amazing to see how this book has helped so many families," Julie says. "It's proof that we're not alone in this, and that there is hope for our kids."

Conclusion

Julie Johnson's book, Parenting ADHD with Wisdom and Grace, is a game-changer for parents of children with ADHD.
