Why PM Modi is the Boss

It was the summer of 2014, when Narendra Modi was announced as the Prime Minister of India. The nation was gripped with excitement and anticipation for the change that the new leader promised to bring. As a young Indian, I was caught up in the frenzy as well. But little did I know, that I was about to witness a leader who would not only bring change, but change the game altogether.

From the very beginning, PM Modi has been a game changer. He has brought reforms in various sectors, given priority to digitalization and technology, and made India a global leader. His work has been remarkable and has brought India to the forefront in the world stage. Here are some quantifiable examples on why PM Modi is the boss:

  1. Prime Minister Modi started the Swachh Bharat Abhiyan campaign in 2014. Since then, more than 110 million toilets have been built across India, and 95% of Indian households now have access to toilets.
  2. Under the Pradhan Mantri Jan Dhan Yojana (PMJDY), more than 400 million bank accounts have been opened in India. This has enabled the poorest sections of society to access banking facilities and get direct benefits transfer.
  3. In 2021, India launched the world's largest COVID-19 vaccination drive. As of August 2021, over 600 million doses have been administered nationwide, making it one of the fastest vaccination drives in the world.

These are just a few examples of the many reforms that PM Modi has brought to India. His vision is not limited to just these projects. He has been a true visionary who has brought about major economic, social, and political changes in India. His approach, which is a combination of technology, innovation, and inclusiveness, has brought a new era of development in India.

Here are some more reasons why PM Modi is the boss:

  1. PM Modi has an unwavering dedication to the nation. His commitment to public service is seen in every action he takes. His speeches and addresses have always been focused on the welfare of the nation rather than his own political ambitions.
  2. He is a true leader who inspires and motivates the young generation. His vision of a new, modern India and his emphasis on skill development has led to an entrepreneurial spirit among the youth. His focus on innovation and start-ups has led to a new era of job creation and economic development.
  3. PM Modi is a global leader who is respected and adored by leaders across the world. He has represented India with dignity and pride in international forums, and has brought India to the forefront of global politics and economics. His focus on diplomacy has resurrected India's image as a superpower.

It is clear that PM Modi's work has been remarkable and his vision is unparalleled. He is a true technocrat who has brought about a paradigm shift in the way India is run. His focus on technology, innovation, and inclusiveness has transformed India from a developing nation to a developed one in just a few years.

PM Modi is a leader who has always been ahead of the curve. He has a comprehensive knowledge of technology and understands the importance of its implementation in governance. He has always encouraged the use of technology and has made governance a mobile and accessible experience.

In conclusion, PM Modi is the boss because of his revolutionary and transformational leadership. His vision, dedication, and commitment to public service have led to a new era of growth and development in India. His focus on innovation, technology, and diplomacy has made India a global powerhouse. PM Modi is a leader who has brought change, and will continue to bring change, for the betterment of India and its people.
