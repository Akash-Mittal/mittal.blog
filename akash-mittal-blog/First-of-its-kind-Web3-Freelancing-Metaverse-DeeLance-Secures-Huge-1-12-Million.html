First of its kind Web3 Freelancing Metaverse DeeLance Secures Huge $1.12 Million

Imagine a world where freelancers can connect with clients from all around the globe in a virtual metaverse, where they can collaborate, communicate and complete projects in an immersive environment. This might sound like something out of a science fiction movie, but it is now a reality thanks to DeeLance.

DeeLance is a platform that leverages the power of Web3 technology to create a freelancing metaverse, where freelancers and clients can engage in a decentralized ecosystem secure and efficient. Its unique business model allows for users to access a wide range of high-quality services from around the world while maintaining control over their assets and data.

Recently, DeeLance made headlines by securing a huge $1.12 million in seed funding. This landmark investment will allow the platform to grow even further, opening up new possibilities for the freelancing metaverse and changing the economics of work forever.

What sets DeeLance apart from traditional freelancing platforms is its innovative use of Web3 technology, particularly the use of digital wallets, smart contracts and NFTs for work and payment. This effectively eliminates the need for intermediaries and empowers freelancers by giving them full control over their pay and intellectual property.

  • DeeLance offers a wide range of services, including graphic design, web development, writing, translation and more.
  • DeeLance's decentralized platform has already attracted more than 2,000 freelancers from around the world.
  • DeeLance's use of NFTs means that freelancers can protect and monetize their intellectual property, leading to greater profitability in the long run.
  • DeeLance's efficient payment system ensures that freelancers receive their payment in a timely and secure manner, without any delays or hidden fees.
