How Google's New AI Models are Revolutionizing Ads and Helping YouTube Creators

By CNBC

Once upon a time, creating ads and optimizing them for maximum impact was a daunting task. Advertisers struggled to find the perfect balance between creativity and data-driven insights. However, with the advent of Google's new AI models, the game has changed.

Google's new AI models are cutting-edge technology that are capable of analyzing ad performance data in real-time. With this technology, advertisers can now optimize their ads for conversions, clicks, or impressions in mere seconds. This feature not only saves time but also helps advertisers make informed decisions about their ad spend.

Even more exciting is the role these new AI models are playing in the world of YouTube creators. It's no secret that YouTube has become an integral part of many people's lives. In fact, YouTube now has over 2 billion monthly active users, making it one of the largest search engines in the world.

The impact that Google's new AI models is having on ads and YouTube creators can be seen in quantifiable ways. For instance, Google reported that using AI models for bidding led to a 15% increase in conversions for advertisers. For YouTube creators, AI-generated thumbnails increased click-through rates by up to 50%. This means that fewer people are scrolling past YouTube creators' content and, instead, engaging with it.
