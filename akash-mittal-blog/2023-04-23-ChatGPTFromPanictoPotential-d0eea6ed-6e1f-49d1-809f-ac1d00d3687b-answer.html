ChatGPT - From Panic to Potential

An Interesting Story

Two years ago, ChatGPT was in the news for all the wrong reasons. The online chatbot, designed to assist students in their studies, was being used as a tool for cheating on tests and exams. Teachers and administrators were outraged, and there was even talk of banning ChatGPT from schools and universities altogether.

However, something interesting began to happen. Some teachers and professors started to notice that their students were using ChatGPT not just to cheat, but also to learn. They saw the potential of the technology to assist in education in ways they had never predicted.

Concrete Examples

Here are a few examples of how ChatGPT has been transformed into a legitimate learning aid:

  • A high school English teacher uses ChatGPT to help students understand difficult texts, by asking the chatbot to explain specific words or passages.
  • A college math professor assigns ChatGPT as a supplemental resource for students who need extra help understanding concepts or working through problems.
  • An adult learner uses ChatGPT to practice conversation skills in a new language, getting instant feedback and corrections from the chatbot.
