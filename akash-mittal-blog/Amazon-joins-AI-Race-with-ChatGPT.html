Amazon to add ChatGPT like search joining Google and Microsoft in generative AI race

  • In the race for Artificial Intelligence, Amazon has recently announced that it will be adding a new AI-powered search tool called ChatGPT.
  • ChatGPT is an advanced machine learning model that creates human-like responses to natural language, making it easier for customers to search for what they need.
  • With the addition of ChatGPT, Amazon will be joining the likes of Google and Microsoft in the competitive market of generative AI technology.

Quantifiable Examples

In the world of online shopping, finding the right product can be a time-consuming and frustrating task. However, with the help of AI-powered search tools, customers can now easily and quickly narrow down their search and find what they need.

  • According to a recent survey, 44% of online shoppers say they would prefer using chatbots or virtual assistants instead of calling customer service.
  • Another study found that 65% of customers who used chatbots had a positive experience, while only 24% had a negative experience.
  • AI-powered search tools like ChatGPT not only make customer search easier, but they can also help businesses increase sales. In fact, companies that use AI-powered chatbots have seen a 20% increase in sales on average.

Why Join the Generative AI Race?

Generative AI is an exciting and rapidly evolving field that has the potential to change the way we live, work and interact with technology. Companies like Google, Microsoft and Amazon are investing heavily in AI research and development because they see the potential for major advancements and new markets in the future. Here are three reasons why Amazon is taking part in the generative AI race:

  • Innovation: Amazon is known for being a company that prioritizes innovation and pushing the boundaries of what is possible with technology. By investing in generative AI, Amazon can continue to develop new products and services that can benefit customers.
  • Competitive advantage: As more companies invest in AI research and development, those that fail to keep up risk falling behind. By staying at the forefront of AI technology, Amazon can maintain a competitive advantage in the market.
  • Revenue: As mentioned earlier, companies that use AI-powered chatbots see an increase in sales. By investing in ChatGPT and other AI technologies, Amazon can increase its revenue and maximize profits.
