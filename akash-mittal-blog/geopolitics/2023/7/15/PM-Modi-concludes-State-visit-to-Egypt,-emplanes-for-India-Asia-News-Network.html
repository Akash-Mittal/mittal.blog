PM Modi concludes State visit to Egypt, emplanes for India



Image Credits https://asianews.network/pm-modi-concludes-state-visit-to-egypt-emplanes-for-india/

Prime Minister Narendra Modi concluded his successful State visit to Egypt and is now en route back to India. During his visit, PM Modi held bilateral talks with President Abdel Fattah el-Sisi and discussed various issues of mutual interest.

The visit aimed to strengthen ties between the two nations and explore avenues for increased cooperation in areas such as trade, investment, defense, and cultural exchange. The leaders of both countries expressed satisfaction with the outcome of the discussions and pledged to work together for the benefit of their people.

As part of the visit, multiple agreements and MoUs were signed between India and Egypt. These agreements cover a wide range of sectors, including agriculture, renewable energy, healthcare, tourism, and education. The implementation of these agreements is expected to further deepen the bilateral relationship and foster greater collaboration.

Prime Minister Modi also delivered a keynote address at the Al Azhar University, highlighting the shared values of peace, harmony, and tolerance between India and Egypt. He stressed the need for closer cultural and educational exchanges to promote mutual understanding and strengthen people-to-people ties.

The visit to Egypt provided an opportunity for PM Modi to engage with the Indian community residing in the country. He interacted with them and discussed their concerns and aspirations. The Prime Minister assured them of the government's commitment to addressing their issues and emphasized their role as important ambassadors of India overseas.
