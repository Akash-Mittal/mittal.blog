Examples of China's Tea Culture

China has thousands of different types of tea, each with its unique flavor and aroma. Some of the most well-known teas include:

1. Green Tea: This is the most popular type of tea in China, and it is known for its grassy flavor and fresh aroma. It is made by steaming the tea leaves and then drying them, which gives them their distinctive flavor.

2. Black Tea: This is a popular type of tea in the West and is known for its strong flavor and dark color. It is made by fermenting the tea leaves, which gives it its unique taste.

3. Oolong Tea: This is a semi-fermented tea that is known for its fruity flavor and floral aroma. It is made by partially fermenting the tea leaves, which gives it a unique flavor profile.
