Show Your Presence Like Virat Kohli...': Ex-Pakistan Star's Strong Message For Rohit Sharma

Introduction

Virat Kohli and Rohit Sharma are two of the most talented cricketers in the world. While Kohli is known for his aggressive captaincy and leadership skills, Rohit is known for his calm demeanor and his ability to lead from the front. However, former Pakistan cricketer Basit Ali believes that Rohit can learn a thing or two from Kohli when it comes to leadership.

The Story

In a recent interview, Basit Ali said that Rohit needs to show his presence on the field like Kohli does. He believes that Kohli's aggressive body language and the way he commands the team is what sets him apart as a leader. Basit feels that Rohit, who is the vice-captain of the Indian team, needs to be more assertive and lead by example.

Examples

  • During the 2019 World Cup, when Kohli came out to bat against Australia, the Indian team was in a spot of bother. However, Kohli's confidence and aggression inspired the rest of the team, and his century helped India win the match.
  • In the 2020 IPL, Kohli's captaincy was instrumental in Royal Challengers Bangalore reaching the playoffs. His aggressive field placements and his ability to lead from the front with the bat made a huge difference.
  • Rohit Sharma, on the other hand, has shown his leadership skills in the IPL. He has led Mumbai Indians to four IPL titles, and his calm demeanor on the field has inspired his teammates.

Case Studies

Case Study 1: India vs England, 2018

In a test match against England in 2018, Kohli showed his leadership skills by making a bold decision. India were chasing a target of 194 runs, and were struggling at 141 for 6. However, Kohli decided to promote himself up the order and started attacking the English bowlers. He ended up scoring 149 runs and helped India win the match.

Case Study 2: Mumbai Indians vs Kolkata Knight Riders, 2019

In an IPL match against Kolkata Knight Riders in 2019, Rohit Sharma showed his leadership skills by taking a calculated risk. Mumbai Indians were chasing a target of 233 runs, and were struggling at 61 for 2. However, Rohit decided to promote Hardik Pandya up the order, and his decision paid off as Pandya scored a quickfire 91 runs and helped Mumbai Indians win the match.

Conclusion

  1. Kohli's aggressive leadership style has been instrumental in India's success in recent years.
  2. Rohit's calm demeanor has also been effective, especially in the IPL where he has led Mumbai Indians to four titles.
  3. Rohit can learn from Kohli's assertiveness and aggression on the field, and use it to inspire his teammates and lead by example.
