India's Ties with Russia



Image Credits https://www.pbs.org/newshour/world/heres-what-to-know-about-indias-ties-with-russia

India and Russia share a long-standing relationship that dates back to the Soviet era. It is one of the few relationships that have stood the test of time, and the two countries continue to be strong allies in the modern era. Here is an interesting story that showcases the strong ties between India and Russia:

The Story of the MiG-21 Fighter Jet

In 1961, the Indian Air Force was looking to modernize its aging fighter jets and needed a new aircraft to match the Chinese threat on its borders. After evaluating several options, India decided to purchase the MiG-21 fighter jet from the Soviet Union.

Over the next few decades, India became a significant customer of Russian military hardware, including submarines, tanks, and fighter jets. Russia also provided India with critical support during times of crisis, including the 1971 war with Pakistan.

The MiG-21 fighter jet became an iconic symbol of the enduring relationship between India and Russia, with the Indian Air Force continuing to use the aircraft well into the 21st century.

Examples of Strong Ties between India and Russia

The MiG-21 fighter jet is just one example of the strong ties between India and Russia. Here are a few more recent examples:

  • In 2018, India and Russia signed a $5 billion deal for India to purchase five S-400 missile defense systems from Russia. The deal faced opposition from the United States, which threatened India with sanctions, but India remained committed to the purchase.
  • In 2019, India and Russia conducted their first joint tri-services exercise, INDRA-2019, which involved land, sea, and air forces from both countries.
  • India and Russia have also collaborated on space exploration, with India launching several satellites for Russia and vice versa.

Case Studies of India-Russia Cooperation

The following case studies showcase the depth and diversity of the cooperation between India and Russia:

Energy Cooperation

India and Russia have a strong partnership in the energy sector, with India purchasing significant amounts of crude oil and natural gas from Russia. In 2018, Indian oil and gas companies signed contracts worth over $20 billion with Russian companies.

Defense Cooperation

India and Russia have a long-standing relationship in the defense sector, with Russia being one of India's largest suppliers of military hardware. In addition to the MiG-21 fighter jet, India also operates other Russian-made aircraft such as the Sukhoi Su-30MKI.

Cultural Cooperation

India and Russia have a rich cultural exchange, with Bollywood movies being popular in Russia and Russian literature being popular in India. The two countries also hold cultural festivals and exhibitions to showcase their respective cultures.

Conclusion

The relationship between India and Russia is one that has stood the test of time. The two countries have deep ties in the areas of defense, energy, space, and culture, and continue to collaborate on new projects and initiatives. In conclusion, here are three points to summarize India's ties with Russia:

  1. India and Russia have a long-standing relationship that dates back to the Soviet era.
  2. The two countries have cooperated on a wide range of issues, including defense, energy, space, and culture.
  3. The relationship between India and Russia is expected to remain strong in the coming years, with both countries working on new joint initiatives.
