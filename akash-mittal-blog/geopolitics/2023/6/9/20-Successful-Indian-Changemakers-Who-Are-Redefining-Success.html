20 Successful Indian Changemakers Who Are Redefining Success

India has always been a land of possibilities where individuals have transformed their lives with the sheer power of their talent, resilience, and potential. From entrepreneurs to social activists, from environmentalists to healthcare pioneers, India has produced some of the most remarkable changemakers that have left an indelible mark on the world through their unique vision, tireless efforts, and relentless pursuit of excellence.

In this article, we bring you the inspiring stories of 20 successful Indian changemakers who are redefining success in their respective fields, and making a difference in the lives of millions of people around them.

The Journey of Dr. Devi Prasad Shetty

One of the pioneers of modern healthcare in India, Dr. Devi Prasad Shetty founded Narayana Health, a chain of multi-specialty hospitals that offer world-class medical services to patients from all walks of life, at an affordable cost. Born in a small village in Karnataka, Dr. Shetty's journey is a testament to his unwavering commitment to provide quality healthcare to the masses.

With over 33,000 surgeries performed, Narayana Health is one of the largest healthcare providers in India. Dr. Shetty's unique approach to healthcare, which involves a combination of technology, innovation, and personalized care, has earned him several accolades and awards over the years, including the Padma Bhushan in 2012.

The Social Activism of Sunitha Krishnan

Sunitha Krishnan is a social activist and founder of Prajwala, an NGO that rescues and rehabilitates victims of human trafficking and sexual exploitation. A survivor of sexual abuse herself, Sunitha Krishnan's journey is one of incredible courage and resilience in the face of adversity.

Through Prajwala, she has rescued and rehabilitated over 17,000 victims of human trafficking and sexual exploitation, and has also worked towards sensitizing law enforcement agencies and the society at large towards the issue of human trafficking. Sunitha Krishnan's efforts have earned her several accolades and awards, including the Padma Shri in 2016.

The Entrepreneurial Spirit of Kunal Bahl

Kunal Bahl is the co-founder and CEO of Snapdeal, one of India's leading e-commerce platforms. With a vision to create a platform that would offer Indian consumers access to a wide range of products and services at affordable prices, Kunal Bahl and his co-founder Rohit Bansal started Snapdeal in 2010.

Today, Snapdeal is one of the largest e-commerce platforms in India, with over 35 million registered users and a presence in more than 6,000 towns and cities across India. Kunal Bahl's entrepreneurial vision and innovative approach to e-commerce have earned him several accolades and awards, including the Entrepreneur of the Year award at the Economic Times Awards for Corporate Excellence in 2012.

The Environmental Activism of Vandana Shiva

Vandana Shiva is an environmental activist and founder of Navdanya, a non-profit organization that promotes biodiversity conservation, seed sovereignty, and organic farming. A leading voice in the fight against genetically modified crops and seed patenting, Vandana Shiva's work has had a profound impact on the way we view agriculture and food production.

Through Navdanya, she has been instrumental in promoting the use of traditional seeds and organic farming practices, and has also worked towards creating awareness about the dangers of genetically modified crops. Vandana Shiva has received numerous awards and accolades over the years, including the Right Livelihood Award in 1993.

The Philanthropic Endeavors of Azim Premji

Azim Premji is a business magnate and philanthropist, and the founder of the Azim Premji Foundation, a non-profit organization that works towards improving the quality of education in rural India. With a personal fortune of over $15 billion, Azim Premji is one of the richest individuals in India, but it is his philanthropic endeavors that have earned him widespread admiration and appreciation.

The Azim Premji Foundation has worked towards improving the quality of education in over 350,000 schools across rural India, and has also undertaken several initiatives towards promoting sustainable development and social equity. Azim Premji has been recognized for his philanthropic efforts with numerous awards and honors, including the Padma Vibhushan in 2011.

The Agricultural Reforms of Kisan Network

Kisan Network is a B2B agri-tech platform that connects farmers with buyers and enables them to sell their produce directly at fair prices. Founded by Ashwin Chandran and Aditya Agarwalla, Kisan Network aims to help farmers achieve better prices for their produce and reduce their dependency on middlemen.

Through its innovative business model, Kisan Network has been able to create a direct link between farmers and buyers, and has also provided them with access to vital information and services such as weather forecasts, crop advisory, and finance, among others. Kisan Network's efforts have had a transformative impact on the lives of farmers across rural India, and have earned it several awards and recognitions, including the Economic Times Awards for Corporate Excellence in 2020.
