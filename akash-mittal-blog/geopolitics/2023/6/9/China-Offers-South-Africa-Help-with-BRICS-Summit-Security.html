This incident is just one example of the importance of security in today's world. With the upcoming BRICS summit in Johannesburg, security is once again at the forefront of everyone's mind. Case Studies

In order to understand the importance of security, let's take a look at a few case studies:

1. The Boston Marathon Bombing

In 2013, two brothers placed bombs near the finish line of the Boston Marathon, killing three people and injuring more than 260 others. The incident highlighted the need for better security at major events, and prompted many cities around the world to review their security measures.

2. The Paris Attacks

In November 2015, a series of coordinated terrorist attacks occurred in Paris, resulting in the deaths of 130 people. The attacks targeted a concert hall, a football stadium, restaurants, and bars. The incident once again raised questions about the effectiveness of security measures and the need for better intelligence gathering.

3. The Rio Olympics

During the 2016 Olympics in Rio de Janeiro, there were concerns about the security of the event. Brazil, which had been plagued by high levels of crime and violence, had to take extra measures to ensure the safety of athletes and spectators. Despite these concerns, the event was largely successful, and there were no major security incidents.

Conclusion

The importance of security at major events such as the BRICS summit cannot be overstated. It is imperative that all necessary measures are put in place to ensure the safety of everyone involved.
