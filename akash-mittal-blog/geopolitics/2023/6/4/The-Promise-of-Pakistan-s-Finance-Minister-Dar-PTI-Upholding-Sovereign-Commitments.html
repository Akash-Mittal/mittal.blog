Examples of Pakistan's Finance Minister Dar PTI Commitment to Upholding Sovereign Commitments

Since taking office, Dar PTI has prioritized upholding Pakistan's sovereign commitments. Here are a few examples of his steps to ensure Pakistan meets its obligations:

1. Dubai Expo 2020

Pakistan was one of the first countries to confirm its participation in the Dubai Expo 2020. As the exhibition's opening date approaches, the country is expected to honor its commitments regarding the funding of its pavilion, and its participation. Finance Minister Dar PTI, has assured Dubai that Pakistan will fulfill its obligations, bolstering investor confidence both in the UAE and globally.

2. IMF Program

Pakistan's recent IMF program was aimed at strengthening the country's economy. Finance Minister Dar PTI and his team have put in place measures to ensure that Pakistan meets all the requirements of the IMF program. Such measures include the passage of the National Accountability Law, an increase in taxes on high earners, and amending the Companies Act. The IMF has praised the implementation of these measures in helping Pakistan to stabilize its economy and meet its commitments.
