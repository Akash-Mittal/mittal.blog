The Ultimate Showdown: India vs Pakistan Hockey Junior Asia Cup

Hockey is more than just a sport, it's an emotion. And when it comes to the battle between India and Pakistan, the emotions run high. The Junior Asia Cup Finals between India and Pakistan is going to be one of the most exciting and thrilling matches of the year.

When and Where to Watch the Final?

The much-awaited final is scheduled to take place on the 7th of October, 2021, at the Maulana Bhasani National Hockey Stadium, Dhaka, Bangladesh. The match timings are 6:00 PM IST/5:30 PM PKT.

The match can be live-streamed on the Star Sports Network, Hotstar, and DD Sports in India. In Pakistan, viewers can watch the match on PTV Sports.

The Rivalry between India and Pakistan

The rivalry between India and Pakistan has been intense and fierce, both on and off the field. The two nations have been competing against each other since the 1950s. The rivalry has only grown over the years, with each match being more intense than the previous one.

Their rivalry in hockey is no different. The two nations have played numerous matches against each other, with India leading the head-to-head record. However, when it comes to a big match, anything can happen. Both teams will be looking to give it their all to win the Junior Asia Cup trophy.

The India vs Pakistan matches have always been nerve-wracking and exciting. Here are some quantifiable examples that show the intensity of the rivalry:

  1. In the 2010 Commonwealth Games, the two teams were playing for the bronze medal. The match was tied 3-3, and it went into a penalty shootout, which lasted for 18 rounds. In the end, India won the contest 4-3.
  2. In the 2014 Asian Games, India and Pakistan played in the semi-finals. The match was tied 2-2, and it went into extra time. In the extra time, India scored two goals, while Pakistan could only manage one, resulting in India winning the contest 4-2.
  3. In the 2018 Hockey Champions Trophy, India and Pakistan played against each other in the pool stage of the tournament. The match went down to the wire, with India winning the contest 4-0.
