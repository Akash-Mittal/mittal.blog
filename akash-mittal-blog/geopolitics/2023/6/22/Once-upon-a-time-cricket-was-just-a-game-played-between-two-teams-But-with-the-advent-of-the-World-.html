But this is not the first time the two teams have faced each other in a World Cup. The first India-Pakistan encounter took place in 1992, where Pakistan emerged victorious. Since then, the two teams have met five more times in the tournament, with India winning all but one of those matches. And despite the one-sided record, every match has been a nerve-wracking affair.

The 2019 World Cup encounter between the two teams was a case in point. Almost half a million fans applied for tickets to watch the match at Old Trafford in Manchester, with thousands more tuning in from across the globe. In the end, it was India who emerged victorious, but not before Pakistan had given them a scare.
