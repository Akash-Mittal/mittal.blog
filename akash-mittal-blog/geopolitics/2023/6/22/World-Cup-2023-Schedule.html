Examples of India-Pakistan Matches

India and Pakistan have played against each other in various international tournaments such as the World Cup, Asia Cup, and Champions Trophy. Some of the memorable matches between these two cricketing giants include:

  • 1992 World Cup: India defeated Pakistan by 43 runs in their first-ever World Cup encounter in Sydney.
  • 1999 World Cup: India lost to Pakistan by 47 runs in the Super Six stage in Manchester.
  • 2003 World Cup: In arguably the most anticipated match between the two countries, India defeated Pakistan by a massive 6 wickets in the Super Six stage in Centurion.
  • 2011 World Cup: India defeated Pakistan by 29 runs in the semi-final in Mohali, and eventually went on to win the World Cup title.
  • 2017 Champions Trophy: Pakistan defeated India by a huge margin of 180 runs in the final in London.
