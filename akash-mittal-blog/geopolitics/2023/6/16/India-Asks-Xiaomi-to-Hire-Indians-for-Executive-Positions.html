India Asks Xiaomi to Hire Indians for Executive Positions



Image Credits https://technode.com/2023/06/15/india-asks-xiaomi-to-hire-indians-for-exec-positions/

Xiaomi, one of the world's leading technology companies, has been operating in India for several years now. The company has seen tremendous growth in the Indian market and has also created a vast network of suppliers and manufacturers in the country. However, recently India has requested Xiaomi to hire more Indians for executive positions in their company. This request comes after seeing an increasing trend of foreign companies hiring foreign executives in the country.

Examples of Companies Hiring Foreign Executives

  • Volkswagen India - hired expatriates as their head of design, sales, and marketing
  • Uber India - appointed a Singaporean as their President for India and South Asia operations
  • Nestle India - hired a Swiss executive as their CEO in 2016

These examples are just the tip of the iceberg. Many other foreign companies are also following this trend, which is leading to concerns among the Indian government and local workforce.

Case Studies of Companies Hiring Local Employees

Google India

Google India hires a diverse workforce, and its management team represents a balance of home-grown talent and global expertise. Currently, the local team drives most of the critical decision-making and strategic functions of the company in the region.

Amazon India

Amazon India has significant plans for the country, and the market is one of Amazon's fastest-growing international businesses. The company is committed to hiring local talent with the goal of having as close as possible to a 100% local workforce.

Tesla India

Recently Tesla was in the news for planning to set up their R&D center in Bangalore, and the CEO Elon Musk also tweeted that the company is planning to hire local employees. This move is expected to create job opportunities and enhance the local talent pool.

Conclusions in 3 Points

  1. The trend of foreign companies hiring foreign executives in India is increasing, and the government is concerned about the impact on the local talent pool.
  2. Companies like Google, Amazon, and Tesla are setting a positive example by hiring locals for their executive roles, which boosts employment opportunities and enhances the local talent pool.
  3. India has requested Xiaomi to hire more Indians for executive positions in their company, which could help create more job opportunities for locals and promote the growth of the Indian talent pool.
