Examples of India vs Pakistan Matches

The India vs Pakistan rivalry is not new. The two nations have been playing against each other since 1952. Their matches have always been high-pressure games with a lot at stake. Here are some examples of famous India vs Pakistan matches:

  • Cricket World Cup 2011: India and Pakistan faced each other in the semi-final of the Cricket World Cup 2011 in Mohali, India. The match was watched by millions around the world. India won the match by 29 runs and went on to win the World Cup.
  • Champions Trophy Final 2017: India and Pakistan once again faced each other in the final of the Champions Trophy in 2017 in England. The match was an intense battle between the two sides, with Pakistan emerging as the winners by 180 runs.
  • Asia Cup 2018 Super Four: India and Pakistan met in the Asia Cup 2018 Super Four match in Dubai. India won the match by 9 wickets, thanks to a brilliant century by Rohit Sharma.

Concerts and other cultural events are often cancelled due to tensions between the two countries. The cultural differences between the two nations have also been highlighted in the media, with Indian and Pakistani celebrities often making controversial comments about each other.
