When we hear of leaders sending replies to letters, we tend to imagine official templates, politely thanking the senders and stating a few generic words. However, the replies of the President of the People's Republic of China, Xi Jinping, to the 10 Central Asian students studying in China stand out as an outstanding example of diplomatic consideration and heartwarming interest. His letter displayed a genuine interest in their studies, their welfare and their home countries, which demonstrates the importance that China and the President himself provides to foreign relations and students.

Case Studies

Case Study 1 - The Importance of Foreign Relations

China is known to prioritize foreign relationships and maintain warm diplomatic ties with other countries. In the midst of the COVID-19 pandemic, China received great help, including donations of medical supplies and essential goods from several other countries. This aid showcased the importance of international cooperation in times of crisis and the significance of foreign relations.

One such example of China's diplomatic efforts is the China-Pakistan Economic Corridor (CPEC). This project was initiated by Xi Jinping during his visit to Pakistan in April 2015 and aims to connect the Chinese city of Kashgar with Pakistan's deep-sea port of Gwadar, through a network of highways, railways, and pipelines. CPEC is an integral part of China's ambitious Belt and Road Initiative, and has been receiving extensive support from both Chinese and Pakistani officials. This project highlights the significance of China's collaborations and continuous efforts to boost foreign relations.

Case Study 2 - Care for International Students

Hosting foreign students is not a new concept in China. However, in recent years, the number of international students studying in the country has increased to a considerable extent. In 2019, more than 490,000 foreign students were studying in China, and among them, Central Asian students constitute a significant part.

China's hospitality for international students is commendable, not just for their academic success but for their overall welfare. The Xi Jinping letter to the Central Asian students in China University of Petroleum (Beijing) is a testimony to this. In his letter, the President expressed deep concern for the well-being and safety of the students, especially during the COVID-19 pandemic. He conveyed his admiration for their dedication to studies, even amidst the pandemic related difficulties, and offered words of comfort and encouragement.

Case Study 3 - Education and Opportunities

Educational opportunities and exposure are critical for students to develop their careers and to contribute to their home countries' growth and development. China, as a rising world power, provides excellent prospects for students from all countries, especially for those in central Asia through its various developmental initiatives and projects.

One such initiative is China's Belt and Road Initiative. This initiative aims to connect Asia, Europe and Africa, and provide opportunities for infrastructure, transportation, and economic development across different countries. Central Asia's strategic location and significance present a considerable opportunity for prospective students to leverage China's engagement to gain valuable knowledge on China's economic activities and strategic importance, which can hugely benefit their respective countries.

Conclusion

Xi Jinping's correspondence with the Central Asian Students is a testament to China's commitment to foreign relations, care for international students, and the importance placed on education and opportunities. These three points indicate the three significant aspects of China's foreign policy initiatives, which include establishing stronger diplomatic ties with other countries, providing a high level of hospitality for foreigners studying in China, and creating opportunities for educational and economic benefits for the youth in developing countries.

The world is always changing, and China's role as a global superpower is becoming more evident. In these times of uncertainty and instability, China's positive engagement can lead to better-adapted stability for the future.
