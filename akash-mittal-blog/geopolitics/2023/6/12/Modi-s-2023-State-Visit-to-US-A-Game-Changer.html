Modi's 2023 State Visit to US: A Game-Changer?



Image Credits https://sundayguardianlive.com/top-five/modis-2023-state-visit-to-us-as-big-a-game-changer-as-dengs-1979-visit

During his upcoming State Visit to the US in 2023, Prime Minister Narendra Modi is expected to take the India-US relationship to new heights. This visit could be as much of a game-changer for the relationship as China's Deng Xiaoping's visit to the US in 1979. The visit could have far-reaching implications, not just for India-US relations, but for the global order as a whole.

The Need for a Closer India-US Relationship

There are several reasons why a closer India-US relationship is needed:

  • Economic benefits: India and the US are two of the world's largest economies. A closer relationship could result in increased trade and investment, benefiting both countries.
  • Security concerns: India and the US face several common security threats, including terrorism and an increasingly assertive China. Closer cooperation between the two countries could help address these threats.
  • Strategic interests: India and the US share common values and have similar interests in maintaining a rules-based global order.

The Potential Impact of Modi's 2023 State Visit

The State Visit could be a turning point in India-US relations. It could lead to:

  • An upgrade in the relationship: The visit could mark an upgrade in the India-US relationship from a 'global strategic partnership' to a 'comprehensive global strategic partnership'.
  • New areas of cooperation: The two countries could explore new areas of cooperation, such as climate change, renewable energy and space exploration.
  • Increased investment: The visit could result in increased US investment in India, particularly in the areas of defence and technology.

Case Studies

There are two case studies that are worth examining to understand how a State Visit can impact bilateral relations:

  • China's Deng Xiaoping's visit to the US in 1979: This visit marked the beginning of a new era in US-China relations, which eventually led to China's economic rise and its emergence as a global superpower.
  • Modi's visit to the US in 2014: This visit resulted in the US removing India from its list of 'restricted countries' for high-tech exports. It also led to increased US investment in India, particularly in the areas of infrastructure and defence.

Conclusion

The upcoming State Visit of Prime Minister Narendra Modi to the US in 2023 has the potential to be a game-changer for the India-US relationship. It could lead to an upgrade in the relationship, new areas of cooperation, and increased US investment in India. The visit could have far-reaching implications, not just for India-US relations, but for the global order as a whole.
