Why Investing in AI Is Not a Fad

The Power of AI

Imagine if there was a technology that could help you diagnose cancer before it spreads. Or a device that could predict natural disasters with accuracy. Or a software that could assist you in developing a self-driving car. All of these seemingly impossible feats are becoming a reality thanks to artificial intelligence (AI). AI is not a passing trend, but rather a transformative technology that is changing the world as we know it.

Leading experts predict that AI is going to be the future of technology. Therefore, it is in the best interest of investors to invest in AI now. The world's leading companies such as Amazon, IBM, and Google are all investing billions of dollars into developing AI technology. In fact, according to a recent report by Grand View Research, the global AI market size is expected to reach $390.9 billion by 2025, growing at a CAGR of 46.2% from 2019-2025. Undoubtedly, AI is an investment opportunity that should not be missed.

AI Success

There are many quantifiable examples of successful AI implementations that have benefitted businesses, society, and the world at large. Here are a few examples:

  1. Google's AlphaGo: In 2016, the AI program AlphaGo created by Google's DeepMind defeated the world champion player of the board game Go. This achievement showed that AI could outperform humans in complex strategic thinking. This is a huge milestone in AI and shows its potential in solving real-world problems.
  2. Hospital Diagnostics: The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) has approved an AI-powered diagnostic tool called IDx-DR that can detect diabetic retinopathy in diabetic patients. The tool is now being used in several US hospitals, improving the diagnosis and treatment of thousands of diabetic patients and preventing blindness.
  3. Amazon's Recommendations: Amazon's recommendation engine is powered by AI that analyzes customer data and provides automated recommendations for products to buy. This has led to a significant increase in sales, customer retention, and satisfaction for Amazon.

Why Invest in AI

Investing in AI is a wise decision for many reasons:

  • High Potential Returns: AI is still in its early stages, and there are many companies and start-ups that are working on cutting-edge AI technology. Investing in AI at this stage can lead to high returns in the long run.
  • Revolutionary Technology: AI has the potential to revolutionize several industries, including healthcare, finance, transportation, and customer service. Investing in AI is an opportunity to be a part of this revolution.
  • Diversification: Investing in AI can provide diversification for your portfolio. AI is a technology that is not dependent on any particular sector and can provide stability to your portfolio.

Conclusion

Investing in AI is not a fad. It is a transformative technology that has the potential to change the world and provide high returns on investment. AI is still in its early stages, and investing in AI technology now can provide diversification and revolutionary technology to your portfolio. AI has already shown its potential through successful implementations such as Google's AlphaGo, AI-powered diagnostics in hospitals, and Amazon's recommendation engine. The future of AI is bright, and investing in AI is a smart choice for investors who are looking for high potential returns and diversification for their portfolios.
