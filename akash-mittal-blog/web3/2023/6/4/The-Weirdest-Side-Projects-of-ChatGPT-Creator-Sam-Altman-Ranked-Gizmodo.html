The Weirdest Side Projects of ChatGPT Creator Sam Altman Ranked

Interesting Story:

Sam Altman, the creator of ChatGPT, is known for his unusual side projects that have little or nothing to do with his work. While some may see them as a distraction or a waste of time, Altman sees them as a way to explore new ideas and connect with people in unexpected ways.

Quantifiable Examples:

  • Altman once spent a week pretending to be homeless in order to better understand the plight of the homeless.
  • He also created a service called Loopt, which allowed users to share their location with friends in real-time. The app was eventually acquired by Green Dot Corporation for $43.4 million.
  • Altman started a blog where he wrote about his experiences with nootropics, or brain-enhancing drugs. The blog became so popular that Altman was eventually invited to speak at a conference on the topic.
