What is Web 3.0?

Web 3.0 is often referred to as the decentralized, or semantic web. It is the next evolution of the internet where data is no longer controlled and managed by a few large corporations like Google and Facebook, but rather by a decentralized network of users. This means that individuals can control their own data and data integrity can be maintained without the need for costly intermediaries.

DPAT is a company that is at the forefront of this new era of the internet. They are developing a decentralized identity platform that allows individuals to take control of their own data and verify their identity in a secure way.
