WEB3: A GAME-CHANGER FOR WOMEN IN FINTECH

"I still remember my first day as an intern at a fintech company. The moment I stepped into the office, I felt like I had entered a fraternity. Young, white, privileged men filled the room, all dressed in similar clothes. I was the only woman in the room and the only person of color. It was intimidating, but I took a deep breath and walked towards the desk where I was supposed to sit. I wanted to prove to myself that I, too, belonged in this space".

This is what Amy, a 26-year-old woman who works in fintech, shared with me during a recent interview. As she spoke, it was clear that her experience was not unique. Despite efforts to create more inclusive workplaces, fintech companies are still dominated by men.

In many ways, fintech has failed to address the systemic gender imbalance that has long existed in finance. However, Web3 technology offers a new opportunity for women to break through the old boys' club and access the benefits of decentralized finance. In this article, we will explore how Web3 can do for women what fintech hasn't: create a more equitable and accessible financial system.

Quantifiable Examples:

Web3 offers unique opportunities for women to participate in decentralized finance. For example, a recent report by Glassdoor found that women make up only 30% of the fintech workforce. However, Web3 technology can help to level the playing field by providing new avenues for women to enter the industry. Some examples of this include:

  • Women can participate in decentralized finance through blockchain-based lending platforms like Aave and MakerDAO. These platforms require no intermediaries, making it easier for women to access capital and invest in new projects.
  • Web3-based crowdfunding platforms, such as Giveth and Gitcoin, allow women to support and invest in projects they believe in without having to go through traditional funding channels.
  • Web3-based gig platforms, such as Human Protocol and LaborX, offer opportunities for women to work on micro-tasks and earn crypto tokens in exchange. This provides a new pathway to financial independence for women who have been shut out of traditional employment opportunities.

Practical Tips:

If you're a woman interested in pursuing a career in fintech or Web3, here are some tips to get started:

  • Research different Web3 projects and platforms to find out which ones align with your interests and values. You can start by browsing sites like CryptoSlate or CoinMarketCap.
  • Join Web3 and fintech communities on social media platforms like Twitter and LinkedIn. This will allow you to connect with other professionals in the space and stay updated on industry news and trends.
  • Attend meetups, conferences, and other events that focus on Web3 and fintech. This will allow you to network with industry professionals and learn more about the latest developments in the space.
