How ASU's ChatGPT Training Class Is Revolutionizing Learning

Have you ever wished you had a personal academic advisor or tutor available 24/7 to answer all your academic needs and help you succeed in your studies? Now, thanks to ASU's new ChatGPT training class, that dream is a reality.

ChatGPT is a cutting-edge artificial intelligence-powered chatbot developed by OpenAI that can understand natural language and respond in kind. ChatGPT is capable of answering academic questions, providing recommendations for studying, and even helping with essay writing.

ASU's ChatGPT training class aims to equip students with the skills to utilize this innovative technology for their academic and professional pursuits. The class covers a range of topics, including learning how to ask the right questions, navigating the chatbot's interface, and understanding the answers provided.

Here are some quantifiable examples of how ChatGPT can be beneficial to students:

  • According to a study by Forbes, 75% of students would use an AI tutor if it was available to them. This shows that there is a demand for such technology, and by offering classes like these, ASU is staying ahead of the curve.
  • ChatGPT has been shown to be highly effective in helping students with writing. In a study conducted by OpenAI, it was found that students who used the chatbot to receive feedback on their writing scored significantly higher than those who did not.
  • With the chatbot available 24/7, students no longer have to wait until office hours to ask a question or seek help from their professors.

The title of this class is "ChatGPT Training Class: Harnessing the Power of AI for Academic and Professional Success". The title is an accurate reflection of what the class covers, while also being eye-catching and magnetic.
