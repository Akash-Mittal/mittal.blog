PayPal Ventures backs Magic with 52m for web3 revolution

Once upon a time, there was a group of entrepreneurs who were passionate about the possibilities of the web3 revolution. They knew that the future of finance was going to be decentralized and that the world of blockchain was going to play a key role in it. They decided to create a company called Magic that would help users to manage their digital assets, securely authenticate with blockchain-powered services, and access new decentralized finance (DeFi) opportunities. This company quickly gained a lot of attention, not only from everyday users but also from big players in the industry.

Recently, it was announced that Magic had secured $52 million in Series A funding from PayPal Ventures, along with other investors such as Tiger Global and Digital Currency Group. This investment is a clear validation of Magic's vision and a sign of the interest and importance of the web3 revolution for the finance sector.

So, what does this mean for the industry and for users? Here are some quantifiable examples:

  • Magic already has over 100,000 users in its platform and is adding more every day.
  • In the past year, the total value locked in DeFi protocols has increased from $800 million to over $80 billion, a 10,000% growth.
  • PayPal has over 392 million active users and processed $311 billion in payments in the first quarter of 2021.
  • Magic's technology can reduce the risk of fraud by up to 95% and simplify the onboarding process for DeFi protocols, which currently require a lot of technical knowledge and complex setups.

What's in it for Users?

For users, the benefits of Magic and the web3 revolution are clear:

  • Users can take control and ownership of their digital assets, such as cryptocurrencies and digital identities, without intermediaries or centralized authorities.
  • Users can access a wide range of DeFi protocols and services, such as lending, borrowing, trading, or staking, with lower fees and higher returns than traditional finance.
  • Users can benefit from increased privacy and security, as the blockchain technology behind web3 provides a tamper-proof and transparent record of transactions and interactions.
  • Users can participate in new forms of governance and decision-making, as many DeFi protocols are governed by decentralized autonomous organizations (DAOs) that allow users to have a say in the direction and development of the protocol.

Conclusion

In conclusion, the investment of PayPal Ventures in Magic and the web3 revolution is a clear sign of the disruption and transformation that is taking place in the finance industry. The web3 revolution is not just a buzzword or a trend, but a fundamental shift in the way we think about money, trust, and power. The benefits of web3 for users are many and significant, and will only continue to grow as more people and companies adopt this technology.

Therefore, we can expect to see more investments, partnerships, and developments in the web3 space in the coming years, as the possibilities are limitless for those who embrace this new paradigm.
