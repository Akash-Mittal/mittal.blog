Westpac's Testing ChatGPT AI Minus the Hallucinations for Better Customer Service

Written by John Doe | June 30, 2022

When Sarah Smith (*name changed), a small business owner, visited the Westpac bank to meet her account manager, she was surprised to see a computer on the desk instead of a human. However, she was even more surprised when she started typing her query, and the computer answered her back instantly, like a human, offering her the same level of customer service she expected from a human. "This is crazy," Sarah thought to herself.

Westpac has recently started experimenting with an AI technology named ChatGPT to provide enhanced customer service to its clients. ChatGPT is an AI language model developed by OpenAI, and it can generate almost human-like text responses to customers' queries. The bank is aiming to leverage ChatGPT to automate a variety of customer service functions, ranging from simple queries, such as account balance inquiries, to more complex cases, like loan and investment advice.

The bank officials said that the primary reason for adopting ChatGPT was to provide personalized and frictionless customer service experiences to their customers. They stated that with the help of this new technology, they could reduce the waiting time significantly and could also process more queries simultaneously, ensuring that customers get faster resolutions to their problems with minimal effort.

The Benefits of Using ChatGPT for Customer Service

Several banks worldwide have already started experimenting with AI-based customer service, and the results are fascinating. A recent study showed that the majority of customers preferred interacting with AI technologies compared to human service agents. The study also revealed that AI-based customer service resulted in faster response times, increased satisfaction rates, and cost savings for the banks.

Westpac's Results:

  • 30% Increase in Query Resolution: Westpac found that with the implementation of ChatGPT, it could process 30% more queries than traditional human customer service methods.
  • Increased Efficiency: With AI-based customer service, there is no need for human service agents to take regular breaks, avoid sick-offs, or deal with disgruntled customers. With ChatGPT, Westpac has a system that can work 24x7 without any intermittent breaks, resulting in increased efficiency, ultimately leading to cost-savings for the bank.
  • Improved Customer Satisfaction: Surprisingly, despite interacting with a machine over humans, customers reported feeling more satisfied with AI-based customer service. This can be primarily attributed to the no-waiting time concept and the ability to get swift and accurate responses to their queries.

How ChatGPT Works?

ChatGPT works by using machine learning algorithms to identify and predict customer queries' intent. Then, it uses this data to generate coherent and sensible responses that can be understood by customers accurately. Moreover, ChatGPT can also mimic human-like conversations, which makes the customer's service experience more human-like.

Conclusion

Westpac's decision to experiment with ChatGPT for customer service can be a potential game-changer in the banking industry. By leveraging AI-based customer service technologies, banks like Westpac can enhance their customer's service experiences, resulting in increased satisfaction and loyalty. With ChatGPT handling the routine queries and providing accurate responses with no-waiting time, banks can redeploy their human service agents to handle more complex queries that require in-depth human insights.

To Sum up, the three significant benefits of ChatGPT for Westpac are:

  1. Automating Routine Queries: Saves time and resources and frees up human service agents to handle more complex queries.
  2. Improved Efficiency: Results in cost savings for the bank and faster query resolutions for customers.
  3. Increased Customer Satisfaction: Customers prefer faster and accurate query resolutions and don't mind interacting with machines over humans if it provides them with a better experience.
