Go Ahead and Make Your AI Recipe: It Won't Be Good Eater

Have you ever tried cooking with artificial intelligence? As someone who loves to cook, I was intrigued by the idea of creating an AI recipe. So, I decided to give it a try.

My first attempt at creating an AI recipe was a disaster. I fed the AI system with all kinds of ingredients, but it couldn't come up with a recipe that made sense. The measurements were off, the flavors were wrong, and the cooking instructions were incomprehensible.

It may seem like a small thing, but cooking is an art, and it requires a certain level of human intuition and creativity. AI simply cannot replicate that.

If you're still skeptical about the limitations of AI in cooking, consider these quantifiable examples:

  • In 2016, IBM's Watson developed a recipe for "Buddha Bowls" that included unappetizing ingredients such as beets and bananas.
  • In 2018, a team of researchers from MIT used AI to create a recipe for "Pumpkin Risotto" that instructed cooks to burn the pumpkin.
  • A Google AI system that attempted to come up with dessert recipes came up with bizarre combinations such as "pasta and parmesan cheesecake."

While these examples may be amusing, they highlight the fact that AI simply cannot replicate the human intuition and expertise needed to create a good recipe.

Why AI Lacks the Creativity Needed for Cooking?

There are several reasons why AI lacks the creativity needed for cooking:

  • AI is trained on existing recipes and data, which limits its ability to think outside the box.
  • AI lacks the human intuition and expertise needed to adjust measurements and flavors on the fly.
  • AI cannot replicate the emotional and cultural connections that humans have with food.

These limitations mean that AI can only replicate existing recipes, which limits its potential to truly innovate and create new culinary experiences.

Conclusion

While AI may have the potential to revolutionize several industries, the culinary world is not one of them. Cooking is an art that requires a certain level of intuition and creativity that AI simply cannot replicate.
