Guardians of the Galaxy Vol. 3's Austin Freeman - Special Interview

Austin Freeman on the Success of the Guardians of the Galaxy Franchise

Austin Freeman is the director of the highly anticipated Marvel film, Guardians of the Galaxy Vol. 3. The franchise has seen immense success over the years with both critics and fans. The films are known for their comedy, action-packed sequences, and the perfect blend of music and visuals.

"I'm incredibly grateful to be a part of this project and I believe that the success of the franchise is a result of a combination of several factors," says Freeman. "One of the key factors is the relatability of the characters. Despite being aliens and living in a completely different universe, the Guardians are just like us. They have flaws, emotions, and past traumas that they deal with on a daily basis."

The other factor that sets the Guardians of the Galaxy apart is the music. The films are known for their eclectic mix of catchy tunes and have given a new life to classic songs from the '70s and '80s. The music not only serves as a backdrop for the film but also adds to the overall experience.

"The music is carefully chosen to reflect the mood of the scene and to help the audience connect with the characters on a deeper level," says Freeman. "I think people have a special connection to music and when you add that to a visually stunning film, you create something truly magical."

With the success of the franchise and the expectations high for the third installment of the series, we sat down with Freeman to talk about what fans can expect from the upcoming film and his journey as a director.

The Making of Guardians of the Galaxy Vol. 3

For Freeman, being a part of the Guardians of the Galaxy franchise has been a dream come true. "As a kid, I was a huge fan of the Marvel comics, so to be able to direct a Marvel film is an absolute dream come true," he says.

However, with great opportunity comes great responsibility. Freeman understands the importance of delivering a film that not only meets the audience's expectations but also stays true to the characters and the story.

"The third film is going to be the biggest and most ambitious Guardians film yet. We have a lot of exciting things planned for the fans, but at the same time, we want to make sure that the story stays true to the characters and the themes that the franchise is known for."

One of the challenges that Freeman faced while making the film was balancing the humor and the emotional elements of the story. "The Guardians films are known for their comedy, but at the same time, we want to make sure that we are telling a story that connects with the audience on an emotional level," he says.

To ensure that the film strikes the right balance, the cast and crew spent a lot of time rehearsing and refining the script. "We wanted to make sure that every line, every joke, every emotion was delivered perfectly. We wanted the audience to laugh, cry, and cheer along with the characters."

Another aspect of the film that Freeman is excited about is the music. "We have some amazing songs lined up for this film that I think fans are going to love," he says. "The music plays a big role in the story and helps to elevate the emotions of the characters."

The Future of the Marvel Franchise

As a filmmaker and a fan of the Marvel franchise, Freeman is excited about the future of the series. With new characters and storylines being introduced in each film, the Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU) continues to grow and evolve.

"I think the success of the MCU comes down to the fact that it's a shared universe. Each film is connected in some way, whether it's through the characters or the story. It's a unique way of storytelling that has never been done before," says Freeman.

He believes that this shared universe format not only allows for great storytelling but also creates a sense of community among the fans. "The fans know that all of these films are connected, so there's a sense of excitement and anticipation for each new release. It's like they're a part of something bigger than just one film."

With the ever-expanding MCU, there's a lot of speculation about what the future holds. However, Freeman is tight-lipped about any future projects he may be involved with. "Right now, my focus is on Guardians of the Galaxy Vol. 3. We're hoping to deliver a film that exceeds expectations and gives the fans something to remember."

Conclusion

The Guardians of the Galaxy franchise has become a fan favorite over the years, and the upcoming third installment is one of the most anticipated films of the year. Austin Freeman, the director of the film, is aware of the expectations and is working hard to deliver a film that not only meets them but exceeds them.

With a perfect blend of comedy, action, and music, Guardians of the Galaxy has become a unique franchise in the Marvel universe. The relatable characters and the music have helped to create an emotional connection with the audience that goes beyond just the visuals on screen.

The success of the franchise has proved that the shared universe format can be a successful way of storytelling. With new characters and storylines being introduced in each film, the MCU continues to grow and evolve, creating a sense of community among the fans.

Overall, the Guardians of the Galaxy franchise is a testament to the power of great storytelling. With Austin Freeman at the helm, fans can be assured that the upcoming film will be a breathtaking experience.
