Recently, WIRED sat down with three up-and-coming stars -- Shameik Moore, Issa Rae, and Hailee Steinfeld -- to answer some of the internet's most searched questions about them. Here are a few highlights:

Shameik Moore

How did Shameik Moore get into acting?

Shameik Moore got his start in the entertainment industry as a dancer and rapper. He was discovered by a talent scout at a party in Atlanta who encouraged him to audition for the part of Malcolm in the film Dope. The rest, as they say, is history.

Is Shameik Moore related to Mary J. Blige?

No, Shameik Moore is not related to Mary J. Blige. Despite some rumors that they might be cousins, there is no familial relationship between the two.

What is Shameik Moore's ethnicity?

Shameik Moore is of Jamaican and African-American descent.

Issa Rae

What is Issa Rae's real name?

Issa Rae's full name is actually Jo-Issa Rae Diop.

What languages can Issa Rae speak?

Issa Rae can speak English, French, and Wolof, a language spoken in Senegal, Gambia, and Mauritania.

How did Issa Rae get famous?

Issa Rae rose to fame thanks to her web series Awkward Black Girl, which she created and starred in. The series gained a large following and led to her being discovered by Hollywood executives.

Hailee Steinfeld

Who is Hailee Steinfeld dating?

As of this writing, Hailee Steinfeld is not publicly dating anyone, though she has been linked to a number of celebrities in the past.

How old was Hailee Steinfeld in True Grit?

Hailee Steinfeld was just 13 years old when she played Mattie Ross in the Coen Brothers' film True Grit.

What is Hailee Steinfeld's net worth?

According to Celebrity Net Worth, Hailee Steinfeld's net worth is approximately $8 million.
