5 L'Oréal Beauty Innovations At Viva Tech

Story

Julie has always struggled with finding the right foundation shade for her skin tone. No matter what brands she tried, she was never satisfied with the result. However, attending the Viva Tech showcase event organized by L'Oréal, she discovered their new AR (Augmented Reality) technology that allowed her to see how different shades would look on her skin before ever trying them on. She was amazed at the precision and ease of use, and finally found her perfect shade.

Examples

  • AR: L'Oréal's AR technology allows customers to try on makeup virtually before making a purchase. This technology is available in stores and also through their app, Makeup Genius.
  • AI: With the help of AI, L'Oréal's Perso can create custom beauty products based on a user's skin tone, preferences, and environmental factors.
  • Web3: L'Oréal's Modiface technology uses Web3 to create virtual makeup looks that can be experienced through the browser.
  • D&I: L'Oréal's D&I (Diversity and Inclusion) strategy is focused on creating a more inclusive beauty industry through fair hiring practices, equitable pay, and increasing representation in their leadership positions.
  • Utility: L'Oréal's My Skin Track UV is a wearable device that tracks a user's UV exposure and provides personalized skincare recommendations based on the data collected.

Conclusion

  1. L'Oréal's use of innovative technologies like AR and AI is revolutionizing the beauty industry and making it more accessible to everyone.
  2. Their commitment to diversity and inclusion is setting a new standard for the industry and promoting positive change.
  3. Their focus on utility and personalization is helping customers take better care of their skin and achieve their beauty goals.

By pushing boundaries and staying ahead of the curve, L'Oréal is paving the way for a more dynamic and inclusive beauty industry.
