The Story

The second season of Bridgerton ended on a cliffhanger, and fans are already itching to see what happens next. Luckily, creators have confirmed that season three is in the works, and the focus will be on Colin and Penelope's love story. We first met Penelope Featherington in season one as the shy and overlooked younger sister of one of the season's main focal points - Marina Thompson. Penelope's love for Colin has been evident since the beginning, but it wasn't until season two that we started to see the beginnings of a relationship. As the third installment of the series focuses on their romance, fans are eager to know what's in store for the couple.

Examples of Romance in Bridgerton Season 2

Fans of the show know that Bridgerton is all about the romance. In the second season, we saw Simon and Daphne's relationship continue to develop. We also met Kate Sharma (played by Simone Ashley), who became the central love interest of Anthony Bridgerton, Simon's friend. As exciting as these love stories were, many fans couldn't help but root for Colin and Penelope's relationship. The two have been friends for years, and it was clear from their interactions that there was something deeper between them. The season ended with Colin's realization of his true feelings for Penelope, and we're eager to see how their relationship develops in the upcoming season.

The Importance of Properly Depicting Relationships on Screen

One of the reasons why viewers are drawn to Bridgerton is the way the show handles its relationships. While the show takes place in a different era, it still focuses on important themes such as consent, communication, and mutual respect. By properly depicting these aspects of relationships on screen, Bridgerton is helping to set a new standard for how love stories are told in Hollywood and beyond.
