What is GameOn?

GameOn is an innovative Web3 gaming platform that uses blockchain technology to offer gamers new opportunities to monetize their skills and creativity. Using the GameOn platform, developers can create, launch and manage Web3 games that provide players with a seamless, secure, and rewarding gaming experience.

Why is this investment important?

Examples of successful Web3 gaming projects

There are several examples of successful Web3 gaming projects in the market today. Here are a few:

  • CryptoKitties: Launched in 2017, CryptoKitties is a game that allows players to breed and trade digital cats on the Ethereum blockchain. It became one of the first mainstream blockchain games and set the stage for future Web3 gaming projects.
  • Decentraland: Decentraland is a virtual world that allows players to buy, sell, and trade virtual land on the Ethereum blockchain. Players can then create and monetize their own virtual experiences and games within the ecosystem.
  • Axie Infinity: Axie Infinity is a game that combines elements of Pokemon and Tamagotchi. Players can collect and battle creatures called "Axies" on the Ethereum blockchain.

Benefits of Web3 gaming

Web3 gaming has several key benefits that set it apart from traditional gaming. Here are a few:

  • Ownership: Unlike traditional games, Web3 games offer players true ownership over their assets. Since blockchain technology is decentralized, players have full control over their in-game items and can freely trade, sell or give them away as they please.
  • Transparency: Blockchain technology provides an unprecedented level of transparency in gaming. Players can track every transaction on the blockchain, ensuring that there is no foul play or cheating.
  • Rewards: Web3 gaming provides players with new opportunities to earn rewards and make money. Players can earn tokens, sell their assets, or even win cash prizes in Web3 games.
