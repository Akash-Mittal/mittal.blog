AI Technologies to Leave 800,000 Hongkongers Out of Work by 2028



Image Credits https://www.scmp.com/business/china-business/article/3224078/ai-technologies-will-leave-800000-hongkongers-out-work-or-looking-new-job-2028-says-recruiter

Jackie was a data analyst in a Hong Kong-based company for over ten years. She loved her job and was content with the salary she was getting. However, little did she know that her job was at risk due to the advancements in AI technologies. She was shattered when she received a notice of dismissal from her employer, stating that her job is being eliminated due to the implementation of AI-based data analysis software in their operations.

Jackie's story is just one of the many examples of how AI technologies are disrupting and causing job losses in the workforce. According to a report by recruitment firm Adecco, 800,000 Hongkongers will be out of work or looking for new jobs by 2028 due to the increasing adoption of AI technologies in the workplace.

Examples of AI Disrupting the Job Market

In Hong Kong, the retail sector is one of the industries that are heavily impacted by the rise of AI technologies. Retailers are using AI-powered chatbots and virtual assistants to automate customer interactions, which eliminates the need for human customer service representatives. Job losses in Hong Kong's retail sector are expected to be around 100,000 by 2028, according to the report by Adecco.

Another example is the financial sector, which is also being disrupted by AI technologies. Banks are using AI-powered algorithms to automate tasks such as risk assessment and fraud detection. This leads to job losses in areas such as compliance and underwriting. The report estimates that the financial sector will experience around 170,000 job losses by 2028.

The manufacturing sector is also getting impacted by AI technologies. With the rise of automation in factories, many jobs that were previously performed by humans are being taken over by robots. The report predicts that the manufacturing sector will experience around 210,000 job losses by 2028.

How to Adapt to the Changes

As AI technologies continue to disrupt the job market, it is vital for employees to adapt and learn new skills to remain relevant in the workforce. The following are some tips to help Hongkongers prepare for the changes:

  • Develop Digital Literacy Skills: Employees need to have a good understanding of digital technologies and must be able to use digital tools effectively to perform their jobs efficiently.
  • Upskill and Reskill: Employees should invest in learning new skills that are in demand in the job market. Online courses, workshops, and mentorship programs can help individuals learn new skills and stay updated with the latest industry trends.
  • Consider Freelance or Gig Work: With the rise of the gig economy, individuals can explore freelance or contract work opportunities that allow them to work remotely and have more control over their schedules.

Conclusion

In conclusion, the increasing adoption of AI technologies in the workplace is causing significant disruptions in the job market in Hong Kong and around the world. It is crucial for employees to prepare themselves by upskilling and developing new skill sets that are in demand in the job market. The government and employers also have a responsibility to provide retraining programs and support to workers affected by job losses due to AI technologies.
