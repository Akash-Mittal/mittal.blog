Next-Generation Sequencing Reports and Non-Small Cell Lung Cancer

Next-generation sequencing reports have revolutionized the way we approach cancer treatment, particularly in the case of non-small cell lung cancer. By identifying the specific driver oncogenes that are responsible for a patient's cancer, doctors can develop a targeted treatment plan that is much more effective than a generic treatment approach.

The use of ChatGPT in developing these reports has shown great promise.
