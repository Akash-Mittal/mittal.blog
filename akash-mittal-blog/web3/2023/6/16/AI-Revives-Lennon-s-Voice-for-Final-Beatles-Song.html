AI Revives Lennon's Voice for Final Beatles Song



Image Credits https://innovationorigins.com/en/ai-revives-lennons-voice-for-final-beatles-song/

It's been over 50 years since the Beatles released their first album, but fans of the legendary band have something new to look forward to. Sir Paul McCartney recently announced that he and AI technology have brought back the voice of John Lennon for a new song, titled "Get Back (Reprise)".

The process of using AI to create music is not new, but this is the first time it has been used to bring back the voice of a deceased artist. Here's how it works:

  1. AI technology analyzes all of the existing recordings of John Lennon's voice to learn his singing style and patterns.
  2. Using that information, the AI creates a new vocal track for the song that sounds like it's being sung by Lennon himself.
  3. Finally, the new track is mixed in with the existing instrumental tracks of "Get Back" to create the final version of the song.

This groundbreaking use of AI technology has the potential to change the music industry in a big way. Here are three reasons why:

  1. Reviving the Voices of Deceased Artists
    With AI technology, it's now possible to bring back the voices of artists who have passed away. This opens up the possibility of creating new music featuring these artists, which could be a huge revenue stream for record labels and recording studios.
  2. Creating Music Faster and Cheaper
    Creating music is a time-consuming and expensive process. But with AI, it's possible to create new music much faster, and at a fraction of the cost. This could make it easier for independent artists to create and distribute their music, without having to worry about the high costs of studio time and equipment.
  3. Opening Up New Creative Possibilities
    AI technology is still in its early stages, but it's already opening up new creative possibilities for musicians and producers. With the ability to analyze vast amounts of data and create new music based on that data, AI can help musicians create new sounds and styles that they may never have thought of before.

As with any new technology, there are still some potential downsides to using AI to create music. Some have raised concerns about the ethical implications of bringing back the voices of deceased artists, and others worry that AI-created music may lack the emotional depth of music created by human beings.

Despite these concerns, it's clear that AI technology is poised to revolutionize the music industry in a big way. Whether you're a musician, a music lover, or just someone who's interested in technology, this is a development worth paying attention to.
