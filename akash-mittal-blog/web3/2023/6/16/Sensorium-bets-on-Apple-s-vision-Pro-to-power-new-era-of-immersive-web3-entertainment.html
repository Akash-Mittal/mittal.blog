Sensorium bets on Apple's vision Pro to power new era of immersive web3 entertainment



Image Credits https://beincrypto.com/sensorium-bets-on-apples-vision-pro-to-power-new-era-of-immersive-web3-entertainment/

The Story

Sensorium Corporation, a global leader in virtual reality and social entertainment founded by legendary tech entrepreneur and music industry veteran Yann Pissenem, is betting on Apple's latest VR product, the Vision Pro, to create a new era of immersive web3 entertainment.

Apple's Vision Pro promises to combine advanced VR technology with the power of the company's ecosystem to create a seamless and immersive gaming and entertainment experience, and Sensorium believes that this product will help it revolutionize the way people interact with each other and experience virtual realities.

"We are very excited for the release of Apple's Vision Pro because it embodies all the features and capabilities that we need to create the next generation of immersive virtual reality experiences," said Pissenem. "Our goal is to bring people together in virtual spaces and allow them to interact with each other and the environment in ways that were previously impossible."

Examples

Some of the ways that Sensorium plans to use Apple's Vision Pro include:

  • Creating fully immersive music festivals where people can attend and interact with each other in virtual reality
  • Enabling gamers to experience games in a whole new way by using VR and other advanced technologies
  • Allowing people to "travel" to different parts of the world and experience different cultures from the comfort of their own homes

"The possibilities are endless, and we are excited to work with Apple to create a new era of immersive web3 entertainment," said Pissenem.
