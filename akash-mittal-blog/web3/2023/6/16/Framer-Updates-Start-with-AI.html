Jack was an aspiring graphic designer who sought to create more intuitive designs using advanced technologies. He had always been fascinated by the way Artificial Intelligence(AI) was revolutionizing the design industry by enabling creatives to build cutting-edge and highly engaging user experiences.

However, Jack had no idea how to integrate AI into his design workflow or which tools to use to bring his design ideas to life. This is where Framer, a design tool that allows designers to bring their designs to life with code, came in handy.

Framer, having realized the potential of AI in design, has recently rolled out several features that enable designers to tap into the power of AI and design more intelligently. In this article, we explore the latest updates from Framer and how they help designers design with AI.

Examples of Framer's AI Updates

Here are some of Framer's latest AI-powered features:

  1. Layouts: Framer's Layouts feature enables designers to design responsive layouts that adapt to different screen sizes, devices, and orientations automatically. With Layouts, designers can quickly create complex UI designs using constraints that align and resize components.
  2. Design Components: Framer's Design Components feature includes prebuilt components, such as text fields, buttons, and sliders, that designers can add to their designs. These components are customizable and can be tailored to match specific design needs and styles.
  3. Autocorrection of Design Elements: Framer's Autocorrection feature helps designers create designs that are more consistent, balanced, and visually appealing. It identifies design elements that are out of place or misaligned and suggests improvements or corrections that designers can implement quickly.

Conclusion: Designing with AI using Framer

In conclusion, Framer is an excellent design tool that supports designers with the latest AI-powered features to design smart, intuitive, and engaging user experiences. With Framer's Layouts, Design Components, and Autocorrection features, designers can create responsive designs, add prebuilt components, and correct design elements easily.

If you are a designer who wants to embrace AI to design better products, Framer is the tool to watch out for.
