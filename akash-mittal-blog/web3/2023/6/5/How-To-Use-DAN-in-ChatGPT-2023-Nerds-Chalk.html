What is the DAN Method?

The DAN method is a communication technique that stands for:

  • Describe: This involves describing the situation or problem in detail. It helps to ensure that everyone is on the same page and understands the issue at hand.
  • Ask: This involves asking questions to clarify any misunderstandings or to better understand the perspective of others. It helps to foster open and honest communication.
  • Note: This involves taking notes to capture important points and to refer back to them later. It helps to ensure that nothing important is missed or forgotten.

The DAN method can be used in any setting, whether it's a professional or personal conversation, a group discussion, or an online chat.
