The Journey and Success of Sushmita Sen in Aarya 3: Wraps up with a Happy Dance

Once upon a time, there was a little girl who dreamt of becoming an actress. Determined and ambitious, she rose to fame after winning the Miss Universe pageant at the age of 18. She then made her way to Bollywood and stunned the audiences with her performance in films such as Biwi No.1, Main Hoon Na, and many others. She is none other than the graceful and beautiful Sushmita Sen.

After a brief hiatus, Sushmita Sen made her comeback as the leading lady in Aarya, a web series by Ram Madhvani. The show left the audiences mesmerized with its amazing storytelling, brilliant performances, and an emotional rollercoaster ride.

The success of Aarya was such that the makers decided to come up with its second season. Recently, Sushmita Sen wrapped up the shooting of Aarya 3, and she celebrated it with a happy dance and by hugging Sikandar Kher, her co-star from the series. The video of Sushmita's happy dance went viral on social media, and fans couldn't be happier for her.
