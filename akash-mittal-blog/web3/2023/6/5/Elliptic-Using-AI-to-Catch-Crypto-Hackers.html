Practical Tips

While Elliptic's technology is impressive, it's important to remember that there are steps you can take to protect yourself against crypto hacks. Here are a few practical tips:

  • Use a reputable exchange: Before investing in cryptocurrency, make sure to do your research and choose a reputable exchange. Look for an exchange that has a good reputation, and that employs strict security measures such as multi-factor authentication and cold storage of funds.
  • Use a hardware wallet: One of the best ways to protect your crypto holdings is by using a hardware wallet. These are physical devices that store your private keys offline, making it almost impossible for hackers to gain access to your funds.
  • Be cautious of phishing scams: Hackers will often use phishing emails to try to gain access to your crypto holdings. Be cautious of any emails that ask for your private keys or other personal information, and never give out this information to anyone.
