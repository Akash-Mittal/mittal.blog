Salesforce doubles Generative AI Fund to $500 million for Startups



Image Credits https://techcrunch.com/2023/06/12/salesforce-pledges-to-invest-500m-in-generative-ai-startups/

Salesforce, the global leader in customer relationship management, has announced that it has doubled its Generative AI Fund for startups from $250 million to $500 million. This move is aligned with Salesforce's focus on developing and expanding its artificial intelligence (AI) offerings.

Story

When it comes to AI, Salesforce has been ahead of the game for quite some time. In fact, in 2018, Salesforce announced that it had developed an AI platform called Einstein that is integrated with its CRM software. With Einstein, Salesforce customers can predict customer behaviors, identify insights, and personalize interactions. But Salesforce's commitment to AI doesn't stop there.

The Generative AI Fund for startups was created to help innovative companies develop AI capabilities and products that could supplement Salesforce's existing offerings. Since its inception, the fund has invested in companies like Automattic, creator of WordPress, and data science platform DataRobot.

Examples

One of the most notable examples of the Generative AI Fund in action is its investment in UiPath, a company that specializes in robotic process automation (RPA). RPA uses AI to automate repetitive tasks, and UiPath's platform has helped companies improve efficiency and reduce costs. UiPath's partnership with Salesforce has already yielded great results, with UiPath's RPA platform being integrated with Salesforce's CRM software.

Another example of the Generative AI Fund's impact on startup growth is its investment in Vidado, a data automation and verification startup. Vidado uses AI to extract unstructured data from images and documents, making it easier for companies to process information. Salesforce's investment in Vidado allowed the company to expand its services and reach new customers.

Conclusion

Salesforce's decision to double its Generative AI Fund is a sign of its commitment to the development and expansion of AI technology. By investing in innovative startups, Salesforce is not only helping to improve AI capabilities but also expanding its own offerings to better serve its customers. The generative AI fund is a powerful tool for AI startups looking for investment and partnership opportunities. It's exciting to see how these partnerships and investments will continue to impact the world of AI and beyond.
