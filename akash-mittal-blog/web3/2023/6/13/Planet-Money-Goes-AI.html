Planet Money Goes AI



Image Credits https://www.vulture.com/article/planet-money-ai-technology.html

Story

The NPR series, Planet Money, has been a go-to resource for business and finance enthusiasts for over a decade. Recently, they tried something different by using artificial intelligence (AI) to produce a nearly passable episode. The experiment proved to be intriguing, and it highlighted the potential of AI in podcast production.

The 29-minute episode featured the show's host, surrounded by machines that had been trained to write and narrate a story on their own. The AI algorithm is fed data, and it analyses it and comes up with a story outline, which is then translated into a script that the host reads. The end result is a human-like narration, but with a surprisingly robotic twist.

Examples

1. AI in Podcast Production

The experiment by Planet Money is just one of the many ways AI is revolutionizing podcast production. The use of AI in podcast creation can free up time for hosts, who can focus on ideas and on creating a narrative structure, instead of worrying about the technicalities of recording and editing.

2. Impact on Business and Finance Podcasts

The use of AI in podcast production is particularly relevant in the business and finance industry. These industries are heavily data-driven, and AI can help in analyzing complex financial information and presenting it in an understandable and appealing way for the audiences.

3. Advantages and Limitations of AI in Narrative Production

While the use of AI in podcast production has shown promise, it still has limitations. For instance, AI may struggle with nuance, irony, and sarcasm. Moreover, it may not be able to give a human touch to a story, which is important for connecting with the listeners. However, it can be useful for tasks such as data analysis and speech transcriptions, increasing the efficiency of podcast production.

Conclusion

The use of AI in podcast production is an exciting development that can revolutionize the industry. It can help with tasks such as data analytics, speech transcriptions, and provide a new level of efficiency. However, it is important to keep in mind that while AI has shown promise, it still has limitations when it comes to narrative production. It can never replace the human touch that is required to build a connection with the listeners.
