Ark Invest's Cathie Wood on Betting Big on AI Stocks: 4 Companies to Watch in 2021



Image Credits https://finance.yahoo.com/news/ark-invests-cathie-wood-betting-162436150.html

Meet Cathie Wood, a super investor who is making big waves in the tech industry with her disruptive innovation funds. Her flagship fund, Ark Innovation ETF, has made some impressive gains over the years, thanks to her bold bets in transformative technologies such as AI (artificial intelligence) and robotics.

When it comes to AI, it's hard to ignore some of the leading companies in this space, such as Nvidia (NASDAQ: NVDA), Alphabet (NASDAQ: GOOG), Amazon (NASDAQ: AMZN), and Baidu (NASDAQ: BIDU). In this article, we will focus on four AI stocks that Cathie Wood and her team at Ark Invest are betting big on in 2021.

1. Nvidia Corporation (NASDAQ: NVDA)

Nvidia is a leading player in the AI space, and the company's shares have soared by 165% this year. The company has built its reputation on developing cutting-edge graphics processing units (GPUs) that are used by gamers, crypto miners, and AI researchers. Nvidia's GPUs are also used in autonomous vehicles and data centers, which are key growth drivers for the company's future growth prospects.

2. Tesla Inc. (NASDAQ: TSLA)

While Tesla is widely known for its electric vehicles, the company is also making significant strides in the AI space. Tesla's Autopilot system uses AI to help its vehicles navigate roads and make decisions in real-time. Tesla is also working on developing a fully autonomous driving system, which could revolutionize the transportation industry. With a market cap of over $650 billion, Tesla is one of the most-watched companies in the world, and Cathie Wood believes that the company's future growth prospects are strong.

3. Square Inc. (NYSE: SQ)

Square is a financial technology company that has been making waves in the payments industry. The company's Cash App has gained significant traction among consumers who are looking for a faster and more convenient way to send and receive money. Square is also investing heavily in AI to improve its fraud detection capabilities and provide personalized financial recommendations to its customers. Cathie Wood believes that Square has significant upside potential, especially as the online payments industry continues to grow.

4. Zoom Video Communications Inc. (NASDAQ: ZM)

Zoom is a video conferencing company that has become a household name during the COVID-19 pandemic. The company's video conferencing software uses AI to improve audio and video quality and to suppress background noise. Zoom has also been investing in AI to develop real-time translation capabilities, which could be a game-changer for the global business community. With a market cap of over $100 billion, Zoom is another high-growth company that Cathie Wood believes has significant upside potential in the coming years.

In conclusion, AI is one of the most transformative technologies of our time, and Cathie Wood is betting big on this space with her Ark Innovation ETF. By investing in companies such as Nvidia, Tesla, Square, and Zoom, she's positioning her fund to benefit significantly from the growth of the AI industry. As always, investing comes with risks, and investors should do their due diligence before making any investment decisions.
