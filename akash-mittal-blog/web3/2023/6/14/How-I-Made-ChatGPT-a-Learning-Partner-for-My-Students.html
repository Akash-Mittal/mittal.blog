How I Made ChatGPT a Learning Partner for My Students

A middle school teacher's opinion on using AI chatbots for enhancing student learning experience

A Personal Story

As a teacher, I always strive to provide the best possible learning experience to my students. I believe that a student-centered approach works best, where the teacher acts as a facilitator rather than a knowledge dispenser. However, it is not always possible to provide individual attention to each student, especially in larger classrooms, and this is where technology can play a crucial role in enhancing the learning experience.

Recently, I stumbled upon an artificial intelligence (AI) chatbot called ChatGPT, and it turned out to be one of the best things that happened to my teaching career. I was initially skeptical about using a chatbot, as I thought it might depersonalize the learning experience, but after experimenting with it, I realized that it can be a highly engaging and effective tool for personalized learning.

Here's how I incorporated ChatGPT into my teaching:

Examples

1. ChatGPT for Instant Feedback: In regular classroom sessions, it can be challenging to provide instant feedback to each student, especially during group activities. I started using ChatGPT as a personalized feedback tool, where students can enter their answers or solutions to a problem, and ChatGPT provides immediate feedback. It not only saves time but also helps me understand where each student is struggling.

2. ChatGPT for Q&A Sessions: As part of the homework, I assign a set of questions for each chapter, which students can attempt at their own pace. I encourage them to ask doubts and questions whenever they face challenges. Instead of holding individual doubt-clearing sessions, I created a ChatGPT channel where students can post their queries. ChatGPT provides instant replies based on the context of the question, which saves me a lot of time, and students can learn from each other's doubts.

3. ChatGPT for Subject-Specific Guidance: Some students need extra guidance on specific topics, and in regular classroom sessions, it might not be possible to provide individual attention to each student. I created a personalized ChatGPT channel for each student, where they can post their doubts and queries related to specific topics. ChatGPT provides context-based replies and also suggests additional resources such as articles, videos, and practice problems.

Conclusion in 3 points

  • AI chatbots can enhance the learning experience by providing personalized feedback, instant replies, and extra guidance.
  • Using ChatGPT as a learning partner can save time and effort for teachers and also encourage students to take ownership of their learning.
  • Integrating AI chatbots into regular classroom sessions can promote a blended learning approach, where both technology and human interactions play a crucial role in the learning process.

and Case Studies

Personal Anecdote: One of my students, who was struggling with a particular topic in Math, started using ChatGPT for extra guidance. With ChatGPT's context-based replies and additional resources, she was able to not only understand the topic better but also score higher marks on the subsequent tests. She even recommended ChatGPT to her peers, who were also struggling with the same topic.

Case Study: A recent study conducted by Stanford University found that incorporating AI chatbots in the classroom increased student engagement and achievement. The study showed that students who interacted with an AI chatbot for personalized feedback and learning reported higher levels of motivation and achievement compared to those who did not use the chatbot.

Tips for Teachers

  • Choose an AI chatbot that is easy to use and integrate with your existing teaching methods.
  • Provide proper guidelines and instructions to students on how to use the chatbot effectively.
  • Monitor the chatbot channels regularly to ensure that students are using it for its intended purpose and also to identify areas where students might need additional guidance.
