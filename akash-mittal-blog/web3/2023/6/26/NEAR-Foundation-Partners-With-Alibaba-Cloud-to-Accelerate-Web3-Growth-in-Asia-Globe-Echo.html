NEAR Foundation Partners With Alibaba Cloud toAccelerate Web3 Growth in Asia

March 1, 2022

A Story of Partnership

NEAR Foundation and Alibaba Cloud have announced a partnership to accelerate the growth of the decentralized web, or Web3, in Asia. The two companies will collaborate on various initiatives, including training programs for developers, joint research, and more.

The announcement comes at a time when there is increasing interest in Web3 as a disruptive technology that has the potential to revolutionize the way we interact with the internet. NEAR Foundation, with its focus on creating an open, decentralized platform for Web3 applications, is well-positioned to take advantage of this trend.

Examples of Web3 Applications

  • Decentralized Finance (DeFi): Allows financial transactions to be conducted without intermediaries like banks. Examples include lending, borrowing, and trading cryptocurrencies.
  • Non-Fungible Tokens (NFTs): Digital assets that can be bought, sold, and traded, enabling creators to monetize their work in new ways.
  • Social Networks: Platforms that give users control over their data and provide incentives for content creators.

Benefits of the Partnership

  1. Increased Access to Resources: By joining forces, NEAR Foundation and Alibaba Cloud can pool their resources and expertise to create a more robust Web3 ecosystem in Asia.
  2. Better Developer Training: The partnership includes plans to develop training programs for developers, which can help to increase the talent pool for Web3 projects.
  3. Greater Visibility: Working with a company as well-known as Alibaba Cloud can help to raise NEAR Foundation's profile and gain recognition among potential users and investors.
