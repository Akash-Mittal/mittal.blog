Velo Labs secures $10m to bolster its "Web3+" FinTech ecosystem



Image Credits https://fintech.global/2023/06/26/velo-labs-secures-10m-to-bolster-its-web3-fintech-ecosystem/

A story of how Velo Labs is revolutionizing the FinTech industry through its "Web3+" ecosystem

Imagine living in a world where financial services are accessible to everyone, free from intermediaries, and customizable to a user's unique needs and preferences. This is the vision of Velo Labs, a Singapore-based FinTech company that recently secured $10 million in funding to bolster its "Web3+" ecosystem.

The "Web3+" ecosystem is a revolutionary platform that combines the benefits of traditional finance with the advantages of decentralized finance (DeFi). This innovative system is made possible by blockchain technology, which Velo Labs has mastered to create a secure and transparent financial system that is accessible to everyone.

One of the key features of the "Web3+" ecosystem is the Velo Protocol, which enables instant and secure transactions in various currencies, including fiat and cryptocurrencies. The Velo Protocol is unique in that it combines the speed and convenience of a centralized system with the security and privacy of a decentralized one.

Examples of the "Web3+" ecosystem in action

To illustrate the potential of the "Web3+" ecosystem, let's consider some real-world examples:

  • A migrant worker wants to send money home to their family but is frustrated by the long wait times and high fees of traditional remittance services. With Velo's "Web3+" ecosystem, the worker can send money instantly and securely, without needing to go through intermediaries.
  • A small business owner needs a loan to expand their operations, but they struggle to get approved by banks due to their lack of collateral or credit history. With Velo's "Web3+" ecosystem, the business owner can access customized lending solutions that take into account their unique circumstances and needs, rather than relying on traditional credit scoring methods.
  • An investor wants to diversify their portfolio and invest in alternative assets, but they are limited by the high entry costs and lack of liquidity of traditional investment options. With Velo's "Web3+" ecosystem, the investor can easily access a wide range of investment opportunities, including tokenized assets and fractional ownership models, without needing to pay high fees or lock up their funds for long periods.
