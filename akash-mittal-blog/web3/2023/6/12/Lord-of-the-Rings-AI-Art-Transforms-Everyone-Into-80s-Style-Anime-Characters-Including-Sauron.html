Examples

The Lord of the Rings AI art has transformed every character from the story. Even Sauron, the iconic villain in the story, has been given the anime-style treatment.

Gandalf, one of the most beloved characters from the story, has also been transformed into an anime-style character. With his long hair, pointed hat, and cloak, he looks like he walked right out of an anime from the '80s.

The hobbits, including Frodo, Sam, Merry, and Pippin, have also been transformed into anime-style characters. With their big eyes and adorable features, they look cute and charming, unlike their rough-and-tumble personalities in the story.
