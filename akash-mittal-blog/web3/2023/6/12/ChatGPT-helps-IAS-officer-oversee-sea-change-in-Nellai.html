The Journey of Gokul S Krishnan

Gokul S Krishnan was just like any other person who aspires to do something in life. He started his journey as a teacher, but his job was never able to give him satisfaction. The Challenges of Nellai

Nellai, a small and beautiful town in Tamil Nadu, had been facing numerous challenges. A lack of necessary infrastructure and limited job opportunities made it difficult for people to thrive. The town was facing major development issues, and the people were left with no option but to migrate to bigger cities to find employment opportunities. Gokul realized that these issues needed to be addressed, and he was determined to find solutions.
