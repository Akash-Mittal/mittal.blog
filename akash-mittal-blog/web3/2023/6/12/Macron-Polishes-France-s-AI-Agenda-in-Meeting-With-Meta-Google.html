A Story to Begin With

France has been a leader in technology and innovation for decades, and President Emmanuel Macron is looking to expand the country's role in the development and regulation of artificial intelligence.

During a recent meeting with experts from Meta Platforms Inc. and Alphabet Inc.'s Google, Macron discussed a range of topics related to AI, including its potential impact on the economy, society, and the environment.

As Macron noted in his remarks, France sees AI as a critical area for investment and innovation. With its thriving startup ecosystem, world-class research institutions, and talented workforce, the country is well-positioned to play a leading role in shaping the future of AI.
