The Rise of Web3 Social Media: A Look at the $56M+ Funding Week



Image Credits https://blockworks.co/news/56m-week-focus-web3-social-media

In the world of crypto, the funding activity never seems to slow down. The past week was no exception, with over $56 million in funding raised for various projects and initiatives. One of the major focuses of the week was on Web3 social media.

The Story: Web3 Social Media Takes Center Stage

Web3 social media platforms, built on blockchain technology, offer a new way for people to connect and interact online. These platforms are decentralized and open, meaning that users have more control over their data and the content they share.

One of the most notable funding rounds of the week was for Lens Protocol, which raised $15 million for its Web3 social media platform. The platform aims to provide a more privacy-focused and transparent alternative to traditional social media platforms.

Other Web3 social media projects that received funding this week include Bit.country, which raised $4 million, and Finoa, which raised $22 million for its digital asset custody platform that includes social features.

Examples: Web3 Social Media in Action

So, what does Web3 social media look like in practice? Here are a few examples:

1. Steemit

Steemit is a social media platform built on the Steem blockchain. It allows users to create and share content, as well as earn cryptocurrency rewards for their contributions. The platform is designed to be censorship-resistant, meaning that content cannot be removed or deleted by a central authority.

2. Rally

Rally is a Web3 social platform that allows creators and communities to launch their own branded social tokens. These tokens can be used to reward community members for their participation and engagement. Rally also includes features like live streaming and community management tools.

3. BitClout

BitClout is a newer Web3 social media platform that is generating a lot of buzz. It allows users to buy and sell creator coins, which are tied to the reputation and influence of famous people on the platform. The more popular a creator becomes, the more valuable their coin becomes. BitClout has been criticized for its lack of transparency and for potentially enabling online harassment and cyberbullying.

Conclusion: The Future of Web3 Social Media

The rise of Web3 social media represents a major shift in the way we interact and share information online. These platforms offer a more decentralized and democratic alternative to traditional social media, where users have more control over their data and the content they consume.

However, there are also concerns about the potential drawbacks of Web3 social media platforms, such as the risk of cyberbullying and online harassment. As these platforms continue to evolve and mature, it will be important to address these issues and ensure that users are safe and protected.

3 Key Takeaways:

  • Web3 social media is on the rise, with several projects receiving millions of dollars in funding this week
  • These platforms offer a new way for people to connect and share information, with a more decentralized and democratic approach
  • As Web3 social media continues to evolve, it will be important to address concerns around safety and security
