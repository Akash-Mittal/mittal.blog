Story

ChatGPT is a popular AI chatbot that has been helping people with various tasks, from answering questions to making appointments. The developers of ChatGPT recently introduced a new feature that allows users to turn it into a Mac app using macOS Sonoma. This new feature is perfect for those who prefer to use desktop apps rather than browser windows. In this article, we will show you how to create your own ChatGPT app for Mac.

How to Create Your Own ChatGPT App for Mac with macOS Sonoma

Before we get started, make sure that you have the latest version of macOS Sonoma installed on your Mac. Here are the steps to create your own ChatGPT app:

  1. Launch Safari and go to the ChatGPT website.
  2. Click on the "Share" button located in the upper-right corner of the Safari window.
  3. Click on the "Add to Home Screen" option.
  4. Customize the name and icon for your ChatGPT app and click on the "Add" button.
  5. Your ChatGPT app will now be available in your Applications folder and Launchpad.

Examples

Here are some examples of how you can use your own ChatGPT app:

  • Ask ChatGPT for the weather forecast before heading out for the day.
  • Use ChatGPT to schedule an appointment with your doctor or dentist.
  • Ask ChatGPT for a recipe when you're not sure what to cook for dinner.

Conclusion

Creating your own ChatGPT app for Mac is easy and convenient. You can use it to get answers to your questions, make appointments, and even entertain yourself. With macOS Sonoma, you can turn any website into a Mac app, making it easier to access and use.
