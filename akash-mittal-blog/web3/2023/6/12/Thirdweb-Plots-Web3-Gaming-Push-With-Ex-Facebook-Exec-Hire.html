Thirdweb Plots Web3 Gaming Push With Ex-Facebook Exec Hire



Image Credits https://decrypt.co/144131/thirdweb-plots-web3-gaming-tools-push-ex-facebook-exec-hire/

Story

Thirdweb, an infrastructure firm that provides tools and services for building blockchain-based decentralized applications, aims to take advantage of the growing trend of Web3 gaming by hiring Atif Khan, a former Facebook executive, to lead its gaming division. The move follows the launch of Thirdweb's first game, Web3 Warriors on Base, which is designed to showcase the potential of Web3 gaming to a wider audience.

Examples

Web3 Gaming

The concept of Web3 gaming is gaining traction as blockchain technology evolves. Web3 gaming incorporates the use of digital assets, tokenization, and decentralization to create a more immersive and interactive gaming experience for players. It also enables the ownership and control of in-game assets, providing more value to players and facilitating economic activities within the game.

Web3 Warriors on Base

Web3 Warriors on Base is a strategic game that allows players to build, manage, and defend their bases, as well as engage in battles against other players. The game runs on Ethereum, a blockchain platform, and uses non-fungible tokens (NFTs) to represent in-game assets such as buildings, units, and resources. It also features a marketplace where players can trade NFTs.

Atif Khan

Atif Khan, an experienced gaming executive, previously worked at Facebook where he helped to launch the company's gaming platform. He also co-founded Playdom, a social gaming company that was later acquired by Disney. At Thirdweb, he will be responsible for driving the company's gaming strategy, developing new games, and building partnerships with other gaming companies.

Conclusion

Thirdweb's move to hire Atif Khan and focus on Web3 gaming represents a significant step towards the mainstream adoption of blockchain-based gaming. With Web3 Warriors on Base, the company aims to demonstrate the potential of Web3 gaming to a wider audience and attract more developers and gamers to its platform. Web3 gaming has the potential to revolutionize the gaming industry by providing more ownership, autonomy, and economic opportunities to players. As blockchain technology continues to evolve, we can expect to see more innovative games and platforms emerge in the near future.

Three Key Takeaways

  1. Web3 gaming is an emerging trend that combines blockchain technology and gaming to create a more immersive and interactive experience for players.
  2. Web3 Warriors on Base is a strategic game that showcases the potential of Web3 gaming and uses Ethereum and NFTs to enable ownership and control of in-game assets.
  3. Hiring Atif Khan, a veteran gaming executive, underscores Thirdweb's commitment to driving its gaming strategy forward and building partnerships with other gaming companies.
