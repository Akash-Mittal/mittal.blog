Is the new coding for kids?



Image Credits https://betakit.com/ai-prompting-is-the-new-coding-for-kids/

Recently, there has been increasing interest in teaching children how to code. This has been fuelled by a belief that coding skills will be essential for future employment in an increasingly technology-driven world. However, there is a new trend emerging - AI prompting. Some experts believe that learning how to prompt an AI engine is the new coding for kids. But is this really the case?

A personal story

My own interest in coding began when I was just eight years old. My father, who was a software engineer, encouraged me to code simple programs using Microsoft Basic. I loved it and knew that I wanted to pursue a career in technology.

However, times have changed since then. Today's children are growing up in a world where artificial intelligence is becoming more and more prevalent. It's no longer enough to simply code - we need to teach children how to interact with AI, to prompt it and to shape it.

Examples of AI prompting

So what exactly is AI prompting? Put simply, it is the process of instructing an AI engine to perform a certain task. This could be anything from creating a chatbot to developing a facial recognition system.

One example of AI prompting is Google's Teachable Machine. This web-based tool enables users to train an AI engine to recognize different objects or actions. For example, you could teach it to recognize a specific type of fruit or to identify when someone is walking.

Another example is Scratch, a visual programming language developed by MIT. Scratch enables children to create interactive stories, games, and animations, while also introducing basic concepts of coding and AI.

Conclusion

So, is AI prompting the new coding for kids? In my opinion, it certainly has a place alongside traditional coding training. AI prompting is likely to become increasingly important in the future, as more and more jobs involve working with AI systems. It's a life skill that could potentially provide many opportunities for those who have mastered it.

That said, we shouldn't forget the importance of traditional coding skills. Coding provides a foundation for learning about technology and for creating new innovations. Children should be encouraged to explore both coding and AI prompting, so that they have a well-rounded understanding of technology and its potential.

Tips for parents and educators

  • Encourage children to explore both coding and AI prompting.
  • Provide access to tools like Teachable Machine and Scratch.
  • Offer guidance and support as children learn new skills.
  • Emphasize the importance of creativity and problem-solving.
