Aberdeen Live: A Strange New Comedy Pilot



Image Credits https://www.aberdeenlive.news/news/aberdeen-news/asked-chatgpt-write-script-aberdeen-8513035

The Story

With the success of sitcoms based in other Scottish towns and cities such as Glasgow and Edinburgh, it was only a matter of time before someone came up with the idea of a sitcom based in Aberdeen. That someone was ChatGPT, and the result was a strange new comedy pilot called Aberdeen Live.

The show follows the lives of a group of people living and working in Aberdeen, from the busy streets of Union Square to the quiet countryside of Drumoak. It's a mix of people from all walks of life, from the oil worker to the student to the retiree.

The strange part comes in with the recurring character of a talking seagull named Seamus. He's a bit of a troublemaker, always causing chaos and getting the characters into ridiculous situations. But somehow, they can't help but love him and the strange dynamic he brings to the show.

Examples

One episode sees the characters attempting to throw a surprise party for their friend's 50th birthday, but Seamus keeps stealing the decorations and hiding them around the city. Another episode sees the characters trying to raise money for a local charity, but Seamus keeps stealing the donations and putting them in his own secret seagull nest.

The show also tackles more serious issues in a lighthearted way, such as the struggles of an oil worker who has been laid off and is now trying to support his family. The characters come together to help him find a new job, all while Seamus creates chaos in the background.

Aberdeen Live is a show that celebrates the quirks and character of the Granite City, while also making you laugh and maybe even shed a tear.

Conclusion

  • Aberdeen Live brings a fresh take to the world of Scottish sitcoms.
  • Talking seagull Seamus brings a unique element to the show.
  • The show strikes a balance between humor and heart, tackling serious issues in a lighthearted way.
