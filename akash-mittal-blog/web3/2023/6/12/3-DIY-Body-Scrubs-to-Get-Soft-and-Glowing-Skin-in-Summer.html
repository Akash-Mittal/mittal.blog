3 DIY Body Scrubs to Get Soft and Glowing Skin in Summer



Image Credits https://www.healthshots.com/beauty/natural-cures/3-diy-body-scrubs-to-get-soft-and-glowing-skin-in-summer/

Summer is just around the corner, and we all want to look and feel our best. One way to achieve this is by using body scrubs, which can exfoliate dead skin cells, unclog pores, and improve circulation. But store-bought body scrubs can be expensive and often contain harsh chemicals that can irritate the skin. That's why we've compiled a list of 3 DIY body scrubs that are easy to make, cost-effective, and will leave your skin feeling soft, smooth, and radiant. So, let's get started!

Recipe 1: Coconut and Sugar Scrub

This scrub combines the moisturizing properties of coconut oil with the exfoliating power of sugar to reveal soft and glowing skin.

  • Ingredients:
    • 1/2 cup coconut oil
    • 1/2 cup white or brown sugar
  • Instructions:
    1. In a small bowl, melt the coconut oil in the microwave or over a double boiler.
    2. Add the sugar to the melted coconut oil and stir until well combined.
    3. Apply the scrub to your body, massaging gently in circular motions.
    4. Rinse off with warm water and pat dry with a towel.

Recipe 2: Lemon and Salt Scrub

This scrub uses the invigorating scent of lemon and the coarse texture of salt to slough off dead skin cells and reveal brighter and smoother skin.

  • Ingredients:
    • 1/2 cup coarse sea salt
    • 1/4 cup olive oil
    • 2 tbsp fresh lemon juice
    • Zest of 1 lemon
  • Instructions:
    1. In a small bowl, mix together the sea salt, olive oil, lemon juice, and zest until well combined.
    2. Apply the scrub to your body, focusing on rough areas like elbows, knees, and heels.
    3. Gently massage in circular motions for 1-2 minutes.
    4. Rinse off with warm water and pat dry with a towel.

Recipe 3: Oatmeal and Honey Scrub

This scrub combines the soothing properties of oatmeal with the antibacterial properties of honey to cleanse and calm sensitive skin.

  • Ingredients:
    • 1/2 cup rolled oats
    • 1/4 cup honey
    • 1 tbsp coconut oil (optional)
  • Instructions:
    1. In a food processor or blender, pulse the rolled oats until they form a coarse powder.
    2. In a small bowl, mix together the oat powder, honey, and coconut oil (if using) until well combined.
    3. Apply the scrub to your body, focusing on areas that need extra exfoliation.
    4. Massage gently in circular motions for 1-2 minutes.
    5. Rinse off with warm water and pat dry with a towel.

Conclusion

DIY body scrubs can be a fun and affordable way to achieve soft and glowing skin. Not only do they exfoliate dead skin cells, but they also nourish and moisturize the skin. Here are three key takeaways from this article:

  1. Coconut oil and sugar make a great combination for moisturizing and exfoliating the skin.
  2. Lemon and sea salt can help brighten and smooth the skin, but be careful not to use it on open wounds or sensitive areas.
  3. Oatmeal and honey are gentle and calming for sensitive skin, and can also help with inflammation and acne.
