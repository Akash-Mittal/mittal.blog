Imagine this: you have a groundbreaking idea for a Web3 project that could bring about significant change in the industry. However, you lack the resources and support needed to make it a reality. This is where the Startup Wise Guys Web3 Accelerator Program comes in.

The program, which is set to launch in 2023, is designed to help Web3 startups grow and succeed. As part of the program, successful applicants will receive up to €150K in investment, as well as access to mentorship, resources, and a network of experts and potential partners.

What is Web3 and Why Does it Matter?

Web3, also known as the decentralized web, is a new and emerging internet infrastructure that is designed to be more secure, transparent, and decentralized than the current Web2 we know today. Through the use of blockchain technology, Web3 enables the creation of decentralized applications (dApps) that can operate without relying on a single centralized authority.

This has significant implications for a wide range of industries and use cases, from finance to healthcare to supply chain management. Web3 has the potential to revolutionize the way we interact with each other online, allowing for more privacy, security, and control over our data.

Why Join the Startup Wise Guys Web3 Accelerator Program?

By joining the Startup Wise Guys Web3 Accelerator Program, you will have access to a wide range of benefits that can help you take your Web3 project to the next level. Here are just a few:

  • Investment: As mentioned, successful applicants can receive up to €150K in investment to help fund their project.
  • Mentorship: You will have access to a network of mentors with expertise in a variety of areas, including Web3, blockchain, entrepreneurship, and more.
  • Resources: The program provides access to a range of resources to help you build and grow your project, including office space, cloud services, and more.
  • Network: You will be connected with a network of industry experts, potential partners, and investors who can help you take your project to the next level.
  • Peer Learning: You will be part of a cohort of like-minded entrepreneurs working on Web3 projects, providing valuable opportunities for peer learning and collaboration.

Success Stories

Still not convinced? Here are a few success stories from previous Startup Wise Guys accelerator programs:

CallPage

CallPage is a sales acceleration platform that enables website visitors to request an instant callback from a sales representative. After participating in the Startup Wise Guys accelerator program, CallPage raised €2.5M in funding and expanded their customer base to over 4,000 companies.

ParkingDoor

ParkingDoor is a platform that allows homeowners to rent out their parking spaces to drivers in need of a spot. Following their participation in the Startup Wise Guys accelerator program, ParkingDoor secured over €400K in funding and expanded their operations to Amsterdam, Rotterdam, and Utrecht.
