Salesforce Dives Deeper into Generative AI with Stock on Sizzling Run this Year



Image Credits https://finance.yahoo.com/news/salesforce-dives-deeper-into-generative-ai-with-stock-on-sizzling-run-this-year-120052003.html

Salesforce, the cloud-based software company, is making significant strides in the world of generative AI, unveiling its new suite of AI technologies to investors and customers alike. This comes after a year of steady stock growth for the company, with a 47% increase in share price since January. As AI becomes increasingly central to the tech industry, Salesforce is positioning itself to be at the forefront of this trend.

Story: Salesforce's journey with AI technology

Salesforce has been incorporating artificial intelligence into its products for several years now, primarily through its Einstein platform, which provides sales, marketing, and customer service teams with AI-powered tools to personalize customer interactions and automate workflows. But the company's interest in AI goes beyond personalized interactions alone.

Recently, Salesforce has begun exploring generative AI, which goes a step further than traditional AI by not just responding to inputs, but also creating new outputs. For example, generative AI can be used to create new fonts, music, or even art. By creating a system that generates outputs based on a set of inputs and rules, it can effectively replace a human in certain creative tasks.

Example: Salesforce's new AI technologies

Salesforce's new suite of AI technologies is centered around two key products: Code Completion and AutoML.

  • Code Completion is an AI assistant that helps developers write code faster and more efficiently. It uses machine learning to suggest code snippets and predict what a developer is going to type next. This can save developers significant time and reduce errors in coding.
  • AutoML is a machine learning platform that automates the creation of machine learning models. It uses generative AI to come up with new algorithms and models, which can be customized to fit specific business needs. This can make machine learning more accessible to businesses that may not have the resources to build their own models from scratch.

Conclusion: Why Salesforce's foray into AI matters

Salesforce's new suite of AI technologies represents a significant step forward for the company and the tech industry as a whole. By investing in generative AI, Salesforce is positioning itself to be a leader in the future of creative technology. The applications for generative AI are wide-ranging, from creating unique customer experiences to automating creative tasks like design and music composition.

Additionally, Salesforce's focus on AI has contributed to the company's strong stock performance this year. As AI becomes an increasingly central part of the tech industry, companies that are investing in the technology are likely to see continued growth and success.
