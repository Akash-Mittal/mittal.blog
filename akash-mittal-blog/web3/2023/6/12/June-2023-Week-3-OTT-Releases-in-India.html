June 2023 Week 3 OTT Releases in India

It's that time of the year again when OTT platforms release a plethora of new movies and web series. The third week of June 2023 is no exception to this trend, with several Hindi, English, Tamil, and Bengali movies and TV shows set to premiere. From Bigg Boss OTT 2 to Black Mirror 6, there is something for everyone. Let's dive into the list and explore what's in store for us.

Bigg Boss OTT 2

Bigg Boss, the popular reality TV series, is coming back with its second OTT season. The show will be available on Voot and promises to be bigger and better than ever before. The contestants will be locked in a house for several weeks, and their every move will be recorded. This show is an excellent example of how OTT platforms are changing the way we consume content. It is a perfect blend of drama, entertainment, and human emotions.

Black Mirror 6

Black Mirror is the epitome of futuristic storytelling. The anthology series showcases a collection of stories that explore the darker side of technology. The show is returning with its sixth season on Netflix, and fans are eagerly waiting for it. From the Nosedive episode, where people rate each other on social media platforms, to the U.S.S. Callister episode, where a video game designer traps his colleagues in a virtual reality game, this show has never failed to impress its audiences.

Extraction 2

Extraction is an action-packed movie that revolves around a black-market mercenary, Tyler Rake, played by Chris Hemsworth. The film was a hit on Netflix, and a sequel is in the works. The second part of the movie promises to be more intense and thrilling than the first. The first part had a gripping storyline, impressive action sequences, and fantastic performances. It will be exciting to see what the sequel has in store for us.

Jee Karda

Jee Karda is a Hindi movie directed by Sanjay Gupta, starring John Abraham, Emraan Hashmi, and Dia Mirza. The film is a crime thriller and promises to keep the audience at the edge of their seats. The trailer has already created a lot of buzz, and fans are eagerly waiting for the release. The movie seems to have a compelling storyline and a fascinating cast, which is sure to grab the viewers' attention.

Rafuchakkar

Rafuchakkar is a Bengali movie that explores the complexities of relationships. The film stars Ritwick Chakraborty, Anubhav Kanjilal, and Ishani Das. The movie revolves around three friends who are trying to find their place in the world. It is a heartfelt and touching story that promises to leave a lasting impression on the audience.

Conclusion

The third week of June 2023 is a busy time for OTT platforms in India, with several exciting releases lined up. From reality TV shows to crime thrillers and anthologies, there is something for everyone. OTT platforms have revolutionized the way we consume content, and it's exciting to see the variety of shows and movies available for us to stream. So, grab some popcorn, sit back, and enjoy these fantastic releases.
