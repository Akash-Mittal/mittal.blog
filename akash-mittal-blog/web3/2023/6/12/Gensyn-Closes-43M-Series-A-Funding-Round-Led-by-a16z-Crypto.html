Gensyn Closes $43M Series A Funding Round Led by a16z Crypto



Image Credits https://www.coindesk.com/business/2023/06/11/blockchain-based-ai-compute-protocol-gensyn-closes-43m-series-a-funding-round-led-by-a16z/

Blockchain technology is gaining a lot of attention, especially in the field of artificial intelligence (AI). Recently, Gensyn announced that it has closed a $43 million Series A funding round led by a16z Crypto to accelerate its AI compute protocol and to expand its workforce. This capital infusion has come at a time when the interest in AI is surging.

A Story of Gensyn

Gensyn was founded in 2019 with the purpose of solving problems related to the high costs and inefficient utilization of computing resources required for AI training and inference. The co-founders, who have extensive experience in the AI industry, noticed that the current centralized model used by cloud service providers to offer AI computing solutions is highly ineffective and expensive.

Gensyn's breakthrough solution, based on blockchain technology, allows for a more decentralized, cost-effective, and secure AI compute protocol. The company's goal is to enable AI developers to access a global network of computing resources, where they can train and test AI models faster and more efficiently.

The Rise of Blockchain-Based AI Compute Protocols

The demand for AI is growing exponentially, and so is the demand for computing resources required to build these complex models.

According to a report by MarketsandMarkets, the global AI market size is expected to grow from USD 58.3 billion in 2021 to USD 309.6 billion by 2026, at a Compound Annual Growth Rate (CAGR) of 39.7% during the forecast period. This growth in the AI market is driving the demand for high-performance, low-cost computing resources.

Cloud providers such as Amazon Web Services (AWS), Google Cloud, and Microsoft Azure are currently the primary providers of AI computing solutions. However, these cloud providers operate on a centralized model, which means all the computing resources are stored in a central location and managed by a single entity. This results in inefficiencies and high costs, which are passed on to the customer.

The rise of blockchain-based AI compute protocols such as Gensyn is disrupting the cloud computing industry by providing a more decentralized, cost-effective, and secure solution.

The Benefits of Gensyn's AI Compute Protocol

Gensyn's AI compute protocol aims to solve the current problems faced by the AI industry by offering the following benefits:

  • Decentralization: The decentralized model used by Gensyn allows for a more efficient distribution of computing resources, which means AI developers can access a global network of computing resources.
  • Cost-Effectiveness: By utilizing idle computing resources available on the network, Gensyn's AI compute protocol can reduce the cost of computing by up to 70%.
  • Security: Gensyn's AI compute protocol uses blockchain technology to secure the network and make it resistant to attack. This ensures that AI developers can work on sensitive data without fear of it being compromised.

Conclusion

The investment by a16z Crypto in Gensyn is evidence of the growing interest in blockchain-based AI compute protocols. As the demand for AI continues to grow, companies like Gensyn will play a crucial role in providing cost-effective, secure, and flexible computing resources for AI developers worldwide.
