The Buzz Around ChatGPT in India



Image Credits https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ynR4hz3rle0
Picture this: a frustrated Indian customer care representative answers a tiring customer query for the hundredth time in a day. The customer is trying to find out how to reset his password, and the representative struggles to explain the process in layman terms. Enter ChatGPT – a chatbot built by OpenAI that uses artificial intelligence to understand natural language. The customer types in his query, and within seconds, ChatGPT provides a detailed solution. The representative is relieved, the customer is happy, and ChatGPT continues to impress. This is just one of the many examples of how ChatGPT is transforming the customer care industry in India. In just a few months of its launch, ChatGPT has acquired a massive user base in India, with over 200 million interactions and counting. The chatbot has been integrated into several businesses, including e-commerce, finance, healthcare, and education, to offer quick, efficient, and personalized customer care solutions. In the BFSI sector, ChatGPT has reduced customer query handling time from 72 hours to just 3 minutes. It is estimated that ChatGPT has the potential to save Indian businesses up to $2 billion in customer care costs annually. Furthermore, its ability to understand and communicate in multiple Indian languages has made it accessible to a broader audience.
