Borda Technology Launches New Product for Healthcare IoT



Image Credits https://www.iotforall.com/press-releases/borda-technology-introduces-new-cmms-with-rlts-chatgpt

It's a typical day at the hospital when a patient is brought in with a critical illness. The doctors and nurses work together to provide the best care possible, but amidst all the chaos, it's easy for things to slip through the cracks. This is where Borda Technology comes in with its new CMMS (Computerized Maintenance Management System) with RLTS (Real-time Location System) and ChatGPT (Chat-based Guidance and Problem-solving Technology).

The new product from Borda Technology is designed to streamline healthcare processes by bringing together all the various components needed for efficient and effective patient care. The CMMS provides real-time data on medical equipment, allowing maintenance staff to quickly identify and fix any issues that may arise. The RLTS tracks the location of patients and staff, enabling quick response times in case of an emergency. Finally, the ChatGPT provides guidance and problem-solving support to all staff members through an easy-to-use chat interface.

The benefits of the new CMMS with RLTS and ChatGPT are measurable. For example:

  • The CMMS reduces maintenance costs by up to 20% by identifying and fixing issues before they become major problems.
  • The RLTS reduces response times in case of an emergency by up to 30%, increasing the likelihood of positive patient outcomes.
  • The ChatGPT improves communication and collaboration between staff members by up to 40%, resulting in smoother workflows and improved patient care.
