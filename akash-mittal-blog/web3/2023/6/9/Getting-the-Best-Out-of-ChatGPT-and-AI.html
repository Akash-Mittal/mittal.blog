ChatGPT and AI seem to be everywhere right now, but how can advisors respond to this change? VettaFi recently held a webcast on this topic, exploring the best ways to leverage these technological advances.

Imagine you are a financial advisor, working late into the night to help a client who is struggling financially. Despite your best efforts, you can't seem to find the perfect solution to their problem. Suddenly, you remember a chatbot you heard about that can answer financial questions and give advice based on a client's specific situation.

You quickly log into ChatGPT and enter your client's information. Within seconds, the bot provides you with several viable options and even alerts you to potential pitfalls to avoid. Thanks to ChatGPT, you are able to offer your client a tailored solution that meets their needs and budget, making you a hero in their eyes.
