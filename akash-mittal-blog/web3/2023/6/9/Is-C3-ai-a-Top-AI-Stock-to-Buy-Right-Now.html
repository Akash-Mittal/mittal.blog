The Truth About C3.ai: Is It Really a Top AI Stock to Buy?

Have you heard of C3.ai? If not, let me tell you a story.

John was a stock market enthusiast. He spent most of his free time researching companies and analyzing their financial statements. One day, he stumbled upon C3.ai, an artificial intelligence (AI) company that claimed to revolutionize the world with its cutting-edge solutions.

John was intrigued. He dug deeper into the company's history, and what he found surprised him.

While C3.ai has received a lot of publicity for its AI solutions, the company has some problems under the hood. In fact, some experts believe that C3.ai may not be as solid of an investment as it seems.

Let's dive into some quantifiable examples to see why C3.ai may not be a top AI stock to buy right now.

  • C3.ai has a trailing twelve-month price-to-sales ratio of 46.43, which is significantly higher than the industry average of 8.12. This suggests that the company's stock is overvalued.
  • The company's revenue growth rate has also been slowing down. While C3.ai's revenue increased by 71% year-over-year in 2019, that growth rate dropped to 27% in 2020.
  • Moreover, the company has yet to turn a profit. C3.ai's net loss was $69.4 million in 2020, compared to a net loss of $33.3 million in 2019.

Is C3.ai a Top AI Stock to Buy?

Based on the above-mentioned examples, it's hard to say that C3.ai is a top AI stock to buy right now. However, there's more to the story.

Point 1: C3.ai Has a Strong Customer Base

C3.ai has some big-name clients, including Shell, the US Air Force, and Engie. These companies have all invested in C3.ai's AI solutions and have reported positive results. This suggests that C3.ai's technology is effective and has the potential to generate significant revenue in the future.

Point 2: The AI Industry Is Expecting Massive Growth

The AI industry is expected to experience massive growth in the coming years. According to ResearchAndMarkets.com, the AI market is projected to reach $266.92 billion by 2027, growing at a CAGR of 33.2% from 2020 to 2027. This suggests that there's a lot of potential for AI companies like C3.ai to grow and capture market share.

Point 3: C3.ai Has a Strong Management Team

C3.ai's management team is composed of industry veterans with a proven track record of success. Founder and CEO Thomas Siebel is a tech industry icon with over a decade of experience in enterprise software. The other members of the team also have impressive resumes, suggesting that C3.ai is well-positioned to execute on its growth strategy.

Conclusion

So, is C3.ai a top AI stock to buy right now? The answer is a bit complicated. While the company has some issues, such as an overvalued stock and slowing revenue growth, it also has a strong customer base, is operating in a growing market, and has a competent management team.

If you're considering investing in C3.ai, it's important to do your due diligence and weigh the company's pros and cons carefully.
