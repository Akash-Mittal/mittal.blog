Mema's Chick'n' & Ribs: Serving Up Southern Hospitality with a Side of Soul Food



Image Credits https://www.wral.com/tar-heel-traveler-mema-s-the-good-down-home-restaurant/20901807/

As I parked my car and walked towards Mema's Chick'n' & Ribs restaurant in Burgaw, North Carolina, the aroma of fried chicken and hickory smoke wafted through the air, tantalizing my taste buds and drawing me towards the entrance. As I stepped inside, I was greeted by a warm, Southern hospitality that made me feel instantly at home. The walls were adorned with pictures of the restaurant's founder, Mary Foy, affectionately known to locals as "Mema". Her beaming smile seemed to radiate from every corner of the room, infusing the space with a sense of love and community.

Mema's Chick'n' & Ribs has been a fixture in Burgaw for over 25 years, and is a beloved institution in the local community. What began as a small, family-owned business has grown into a beloved destination for visitors from all over the world, drawn by the restaurant's reputation for delicious, down-home cooking. Mema's menu features classic Southern comfort food, including fried chicken, BBQ ribs, collard greens, mac and cheese, and cornbread, all made from scratch with fresh, locally sourced ingredients.

Visitors to Mema's come for the delicious food, but they stay for the experience. The restaurant's signature blend of Southern hospitality and soul food creates a unique atmosphere that is both relaxed and energizing. Customers are treated like family, and there is a sense of camaraderie and belonging that permeates the restaurant.

But what exactly sets Mema's Chick'n' & Ribs apart from other Southern-style restaurants? For one, the quality and consistency of the food is unparalleled. In the restaurant industry, where turnover is high and quality can vary from day to day, Mema's has managed to maintain a level of excellence that keeps customers coming back for more. This is evidenced by the many accolades the restaurant has received over the years, including being named one of the "Top 100 Diners in America" by Food Network, and being featured on the Travel Channel's "Man v. Food".

Another factor that sets Mema's apart is the sense of community that the restaurant fosters. Mema's is much more than just a place to eat – it is a hub for socializing, connecting, and sharing stories. This is evidenced by the many local events and fundraisers that are held at the restaurant, as well as by the restaurant's commitment to giving back to the community through charitable donations and volunteer work.

Finally, Mema's is known for its commitment to sustainability and supporting local businesses. The restaurant sources many of its ingredients from local farmers and suppliers, ensuring that the food is fresh, sustainable, and of the highest quality. This commitment to sustainability extends to the restaurant's waste reduction practices, which include composting and recycling, as well as to its eco-friendly business practices, such as using energy-efficient lighting and equipment.

In conclusion, Mema's Chick'n' & Ribs is much more than just a restaurant – it is a cultural institution that embodies the best of Southern hospitality and soul food. Whether you're a local looking for a delicious meal or a visitor seeking an authentic Southern experience, Mema's is the place to be. With its delicious food, warm atmosphere, and commitment to sustainability and community, Mema's Chick'n' & Ribs truly has something for everyone.
