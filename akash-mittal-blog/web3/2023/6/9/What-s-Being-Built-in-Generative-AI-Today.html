What's Being Built in Generative AI Today?



Image Credits https://techcrunch.com/2023/06/09/whats-being-built-in-generative-ai-today/

Story

It was a sunny afternoon when Alice walked into her favorite coffee shop. As she waited for her coffee, she overheard a group discussing the latest advancements in AI. She couldn't help but listen in on their conversation.

One of the men was discussing how his startup was using generative AI to revolutionize the way artists create digital art. They were developing an algorithm that could create original pieces of art that were indistinguishable from those created by humans.

Alice was amazed at the possibilities that generative AI could bring to the world of art. But, she wondered, what else was being built in the field of generative AI?

The startup that Alice overheard in the coffee shop is just one example of the progress being made in the field of generative AI. Here are a few more:

  1. Music Composition: Amper Music, a startup based in New York, has created an AI algorithm that can compose original music tracks.
  2. Fashion Design: H&M partnered with Google to create an AI system that could design and produce a new line of clothing based on customer data.
  3. Meme Creation: A startup called Ctrl Shift Face has developed an AI algorithm that can create deepfake memes of celebrities and politicians.

Conclusion

Generative AI is still in its infancy, but the progress being made by AI startups is remarkable. Here are three key takeaways:

  1. The possibilities of generative AI are limitless. From creating art to designing clothing, there are countless ways that AI can be used.
  2. Generative AI is still largely uncharted territory, which means that there is huge potential for growth and innovation in the field.
  3. As with any emerging technology, there will be ethical considerations that arise as generative AI becomes more widespread. We must be prepared to address these issues as they arise.

As a writer, I am fascinated by the ways that AI can be used to create new forms of storytelling. One startup that I've been keeping an eye on is called Botnik Studios. They have developed a tool called the "Predictive Writer" that uses generative AI to create scripts, poems, and even novels. I tried out the tool myself and was amazed by the results. While the writing wasn't exactly Shakespeare, it was definitely coherent and had a unique voice.

Practical Tips

If you're interested in getting involved in generative AI, there are a few things you can do:

  • Learn as much as you can about the technology. Attend conferences, read articles, and take online courses to deepen your understanding of the field.
  • Join online communities or forums where you can connect with other AI enthusiasts and learn from their experiences.
  • Experiment with AI tools on your own. There are a number of free tools available online that you can use to create your own generative projects.
