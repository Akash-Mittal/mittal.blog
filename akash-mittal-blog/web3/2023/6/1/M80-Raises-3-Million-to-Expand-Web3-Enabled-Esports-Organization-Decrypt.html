M80 Raises 3 Million to Expand Web3 Enabled Esports Organization Decrypt

Esports have come a long way since the days of cramped arcades and strategic button-mashing. Today, the world of competitive gaming is worth billions of dollars, with teams and players vying for fame, fortune, and the thrill of the game.

But as the gaming industry changes, so too do the needs of competitive gamers and their fans. Enter M80, a Web3 enabled Esports organization that has just raised $3 million in funding to help expand its services and bring new opportunities to gamers and fans alike.

The Story of M80

M80 was founded in 2020 by a group of passionate gamers who saw an opportunity to bring blockchain technology into the world of Esports. The organization quickly gained a following by offering players the chance to compete in tournaments and earn cryptocurrency rewards.

But M80 isn't just about tokenization. The organization aims to bring transparency, fairness, and community to the world of competitive gaming. By leveraging the power of the blockchain, M80 is able to create tamper-proof records of player scores and game outcomes, ensuring that everyone plays by the rules and that the best players come out on top.

With their recent $3 million funding round, M80 is looking to expand its offerings and bring even more innovation to the world of Esports. The organization plans to create a Web3 gaming platform that will allow players to earn cryptocurrency rewards for completing tasks and puzzles, as well as participate in tournaments and interact with other gamers from around the world.

The world of Esports is constantly evolving, and M80 is at the forefront of that evolution. With its innovative use of Web3 technology and commitment to transparency and fairness, M80 is setting the standard for how competitive gaming should be done.
