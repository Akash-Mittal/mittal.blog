Story

Marvel has recently announced that it will be using AI to create the credits for its upcoming Secret Invasion series. While some may be surprised by this move, it is not really shocking given the increasing use of AI in various industries.

Examples

There are numerous examples of how AI is being used in the entertainment industry. Netflix has been using AI to recommend content to its viewers based on their viewing history. Amazon Prime is using AI to personalize its content offerings. Disney has also been using AI to create personalized experiences for its theme park visitors.

In the film industry, AI is being used to automate tasks such as color correction and editing. Blade Runner 2049, for example, used AI to create its visual effects. AI is also being used to create realistic facial expressions and emotions for digital characters, such as in The Mandalorian.

In the music industry, AI is being used to create new songs, such as Taryn Southern's "Break Free", which was entirely composed with the help of AI. AI is also being used to analyze songs and predict which ones will become hits.

Conclusion

  1. AI is increasingly being used in various industries, including entertainment.
  2. AI can automate tasks and personalize experiences for consumers.
  3. While some may be surprised by the use of AI in the entertainment industry, it is not really shocking given its widespread use in other industries.
