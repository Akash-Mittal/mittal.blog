The Partnership

Forta and QuickNode had recently announced their partnership to provide better monitoring and protection for all assets in Web3.

Forta is a blockchain security company that offers real-time monitoring, detection, and response to smart contract attacks and exploits. QuickNode, on the other hand, is a blockchain infrastructure provider that offers fast, reliable, and scalable node services.

Together, they aim to provide a comprehensive solution for developers, enterprises, and investors who want to secure their assets in the decentralized world.

How it Works

Forta and QuickNode's joint solution works by integrating Forta's smart contract security platform with QuickNode's blockchain node infrastructure.

Forta's platform continuously scans the blockchain for any suspicious activities or vulnerabilities in smart contracts. If it detects any potential threats, it sends alerts to the developers or administrators, who can take immediate action to prevent any damage.

QuickNode's infrastructure provides the necessary computing power and bandwidth to support Forta's platform. It ensures that Forta's scanning and monitoring activities do not slow down the network or affect the user experience.

Together, Forta and QuickNode offer a powerful and reliable solution for securing assets in Web3.

Benefits

The benefits of Forta and QuickNode's partnership are numerous:

  • Real-time monitoring: Forta's platform provides real-time monitoring and detection of smart contract attacks and exploits. This allows developers and administrators to respond quickly and prevent any damage.
  • Scalable infrastructure: QuickNode's infrastructure offers fast and scalable node services that can support Forta's platform and other blockchain applications. This ensures that the network can handle the increasing demand and complexity of Web3.
  • Comprehensive security: Forta and QuickNode's joint solution provides a comprehensive security solution for all assets in the decentralized world. It covers everything from smart contracts to nodes to wallets.
  • Peace of mind: With Forta and QuickNode, developers and investors can rest assured that their assets are secure and protected from any potential threats or attacks.
