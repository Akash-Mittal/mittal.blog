Velo Labs Raises $10 Million to Accelerate Growth of its Revolutionary "Web3+" Financial Ecosystem



Image Credits https://www.einnews.com/pr_news/641368630/velo-labs-raises-10-million-to-accelerate-growth-of-its-revolutionary-web3-financial-ecosystem

Once upon a time, there was a financial system that was limited by geographical boundaries and traditional banking systems. People had to go to a physical bank branch to open an account, transfer money, or apply for a loan. Everything was paper-based, slow, and cumbersome. Then came the digital age, and the internet revolutionized the financial industry. People could now access their accounts anytime, anywhere, and transfer money instantly. But even the digital financial system had limitations. It was still centralized, controlled by big banks and corporations, and vulnerable to cyber attacks and frauds. Velo Labs was created to address these limitations and create a revolutionary "Web3+" financial ecosystem.

What is Velo Labs?

Velo Labs is a blockchain-based company that was founded in 2020 with a mission to create a borderless and decentralized financial ecosystem. The founders of Velo Labs believed in the power of blockchain technology to enable secure, transparent, and fast financial transactions, without the need for intermediaries. They wanted to create a financial system that was inclusive, accessible, and affordable for everyone, regardless of their location, background, or financial status.

To achieve this mission, Velo Labs created a two-tiered architecture: Velo Protocol and Velo Labs. Velo Protocol is a decentralized settlement network that enables instant and low-cost transactions between businesses, institutions, and individuals. It is scalable, interoperable, and secure, thanks to its use of the Stellar blockchain and the Velo smart contract. Velo Labs, on the other hand, is the commercial arm of Velo Protocol, responsible for marketing, business development, and partnerships.

What Problems does Velo Labs Solve?

Velo Labs solves several problems in the traditional financial system and the current digital financial system. Here are some examples:

  • High Transaction Fees: When you send money from one country to another, you may have to pay high transaction fees, currency conversion fees, and intermediaries fees. With Velo Protocol, you can send money instantly and with low transaction fees, thanks to its direct settlement network.
  • Slow Transactions: Traditional banking systems may take days or even weeks to process transactions, especially if they involve cross-border payments. With Velo Protocol, you can send money instantly, without delays or waiting times.
  • Limited Access: Many people around the world do not have access to banking systems, either because of their location, their income, or their identity. With Velo Protocol, anyone can open a wallet and start sending and receiving money, without the need for a bank account or a credit check.
  • Fraud and Cyber Attacks: Traditional banking systems are vulnerable to frauds and cyber attacks, which can result in financial loss and damage to reputation. With Velo Protocol, all transactions are secured by the Stellar blockchain, which ensures transparency, immutability, and authenticity.

What is the significance of the recent funding round?

In March 2021, Velo Labs announced that it had raised $10 million in a funding round led by VCs, including NGC Ventures and Bitmain Technologies. The funding will be used to accelerate the growth of Velo Protocol and expand its reach to more businesses and individuals around the world. The funding will also be used to develop new products and services, such as a stablecoin, a decentralized exchange, and a digital identity platform.

The funding round is significant for several reasons:

  • Validation: The funding round is a validation of Velo Labs' vision, mission, and strategy. It shows that investors believe in the potential of Velo Protocol to disrupt the financial industry and create value for businesses and individuals.
  • Growth: The funding will enable Velo Labs to accelerate its growth and expand its network of partners and users. This will create a virtuous cycle of adoption, as more businesses and individuals join the network, the more valuable it becomes.
  • Innovation: The funding will enable Velo Labs to develop new products and services that address the needs of its users. This will enable Velo Protocol to stay ahead of its competitors and maintain its position as a leader in the blockchain financial ecosystem.

Conclusion

In conclusion, Velo Labs is a revolutionary company that is transforming the financial industry by leveraging the power of blockchain technology. Its two-tiered architecture, Velo Protocol, and Velo Labs, offer a decentralized, secure, and affordable alternative to traditional banking systems and the current digital financial systems. The recent funding round of $10 million is a validation of Velo Labs' vision and a significant milestone in its journey towards creating a borderless and inclusive financial ecosystem.
