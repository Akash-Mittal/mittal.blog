ChatGPT on iOS: Now with Bing Built-In

Story

As a digital nomad, I always look for innovative ways to connect with my clients and colleagues. When I heard about ChatGPT, a chatbot service that uses AI to find answers, I was thrilled. As soon as I downloaded the iOS app, I found a new feature that made my work even easier: Bing search was built right into the chatbot. Now, I can get answers to my questions without even leaving the app. This has not only saved me time but has also improved my productivity.

Examples

Here are a few examples of how ChatGPT's Bing integration has helped me:

  • When a client asked me for a list of good coworking spaces in Bali, I simply asked ChatGPT and it gave me a list of options, complete with addresses and websites, from Bing's search engine.
  • When I needed to find the latest statistics on remote work, I asked ChatGPT and it pulled up a page from Bing with all the information I needed.
  • When I was working on a project and needed to find an image to use in a presentation, I asked ChatGPT to find a photo and it brought me to Bing's image search, where I could easily browse and download images.

Conclusion

Here are three reasons why ChatGPT on iOS with Bing built-in is a game-changer for digital nomads and professionals:

  1. Increased productivity: With Bing built right into the chatbot, you don't need to switch between apps to search for information. This saves time and improves your workflow.
  2. Improved accuracy: Since Bing is one of the most popular search engines, you can be confident that the information you receive is accurate and up-to-date.
  3. Enhanced user experience: By having Bing search integrated into ChatGPT, the user experience is seamless and smooth, making it easier to get answers to your questions without any hassle.

If you're a digital nomad or professional looking for an innovative way to stay connected and productive, try ChatGPT on iOS with Bing built-in today!
