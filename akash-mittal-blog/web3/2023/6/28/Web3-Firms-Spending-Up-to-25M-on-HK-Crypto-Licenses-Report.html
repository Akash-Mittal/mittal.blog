Web3 Firms Spending Up to $25M on HK Crypto Licenses: Report

It was a hot summer day in Hong Kong when John, the CEO of a Web3 firm, received a call from the Chief Legal Officer of the company. "John, we need to secure a crypto license in Hong Kong to continue our operations", said the CLO.

John knew that it would be a challenging task, but he also knew that it was crucial for the future of the company. He immediately called a meeting with his team to discuss the requirements and costs of acquiring a crypto license in Hong Kong.

Examples of Web3 firms acquiring crypto licenses in Hong Kong

  • Alchemy Pay: The Singapore-based Web3 payments company spent $30 million to acquire a digital payment license in Hong Kong in June 2021.
  • Unstoppable Domains: The blockchain-based domain name provider acquired a Money Service Operator (MSO) license in Hong Kong to offer crypto payments on its platform in May 2021.
  • Bitmain Technologies: The cryptocurrency mining company secured a license from the Securities and Futures Commission of Hong Kong in October 2020 to offer digital asset management services.

Conclusion

  1. Web3 firms are spending millions of dollars to acquire crypto licenses in Hong Kong to comply with regulatory requirements and expand their operations.
  2. The process of acquiring a crypto license in Hong Kong can be complex and expensive, but it is essential for the future growth of Web3 companies.
  3. With increasing regulatory scrutiny of cryptocurrencies, it is vital for Web3 firms to proactively engage with regulators and comply with regulatory requirements to build trust and legitimacy in the industry.
