Microsoft Outlook: Here's how to change the theme on Web, Mac, Windows

Do you find it boring to use the same old theme every day while checking your mails on Microsoft Outlook? You are not alone. Many people feel the same way and prefer to change the themes to keep it refreshing and lively.

However, not everyone knows how to do it. In this article, we will discuss the steps involved in changing the Microsoft Outlook theme on Web, Mac, and Windows. Let's get started.

Steps to Change the Theme on Microsoft Outlook

Changing the theme of Microsoft Outlook is a simple and straightforward process. We have provided the steps for each platform below.

Web Platform

For changing the theme on the web version of Microsoft Outlook, follow the steps below:

  1. Log in to your Microsoft Outlook account.
  2. Click on the gear icon in the top right corner of the screen and choose 'Personalization' from the drop-down menu.
  3. Click on 'Theme' and choose the desired theme from the list of available options.
  4. Click on 'Save' to apply the changes.

Mac Platform

To change the theme on the Mac version of Microsoft Outlook, follow the steps below:

  1. Open Microsoft Outlook on your Mac.
  2. Click on 'Outlook' in the top left corner of the screen and choose 'Preferences' from the drop-down menu.
  3. Click on 'General' and choose the desired theme from the drop-down menu under 'Personalize your copy of Microsoft Office'.
  4. Close the Preferences dialog box to apply the changes.

Windows Platform

For changing the theme on the Windows version of Microsoft Outlook, follow the steps below:

  1. Open Microsoft Outlook on your Windows device.
  2. Click on 'File' in the top left corner of the screen and choose 'Options' from the drop-down menu.
  3. Click on 'General' and choose the desired theme from the drop-down menu under 'Personalize your copy of Microsoft Office'.
  4. Click on 'OK' to apply the changes.

Why Should You Change the Theme?

You may be wondering, why bother changing the theme when you can easily use the default one? Well, there are several reasons why you should consider changing the theme.

  • It spices things up: Using the same old theme every day can get boring. Changing the theme can bring a refreshing change to your daily routine.
  • It reflects your personality: Your choice of the theme reflects your personality. You can choose a theme that matches your style and preferences.
  • It improves productivity: Using a visually pleasing theme can improve your mood and make you more productive. It can also reduce eye strain and fatigue.
