Web Werks - Iron Mountain JV Expands Mumbai Data Center Campus



Image Credits https://www.cxotoday.com/press-release/web-werks-iron-mountain-jv-to-expand-its-mumbai-data-center-campus-by-building-a-third-facility-to-support-growing-enterprise-and-hyperscale-customer-demand/

Story

Web Werks - Iron Mountain JV, a leading provider of data center services in Mumbai, India, has announced the expansion of its Mumbai data center campus by building a third facility to support the growing demand from both enterprise and hyperscale customers.

The new facility will provide additional capacity of 20 MW, expanding its total IT load capacity to 50 MW. The facility will be designed to meet the highest standards of security, safety, and connectivity. The expansion will further reinforce Web Werks - Iron Mountain JV's position as a major regional player in the data center market.

Examples

The demand for data center services in India has been growing rapidly in recent years. According to a report by JLL, India's data center market is expected to reach $4.5 billion by 2025, growing at a CAGR of 10-12%. The growth is driven by factors such as the increasing adoption of cloud services, the government's push towards digitization, and the rise in data consumption by businesses and consumers.

Web Werks - Iron Mountain JV has been at the forefront of this growth, providing high-quality data center services to customers across industries such as banking, financial services, and insurance, healthcare, and e-commerce. The company's existing facilities in Mumbai have been operating at near-full capacity, highlighting the need for additional capacity to support the growing customer demand.

Moreover, the COVID-19 pandemic has accelerated the shift towards remote working and digitalization, further increasing the demand for data center services. As more businesses rely on data and digital infrastructure to operate, the need for secure, reliable, and scalable data center services will continue to grow.

Conclusion

The expansion of Web Werks - Iron Mountain JV's Mumbai data center campus is a strategic move to support the growing demand for data center services in India. The new facility will provide additional capacity and reinforce the company's position as a leading player in the data center market. As the digital transformation of businesses and the economy accelerates, reliable and scalable data center services will become increasingly important, and companies like Web Werks - Iron Mountain JV will be well-positioned to meet the growing demand.

For businesses looking to leverage data center services, it is essential to choose a provider that meets their specific needs and requirements. Some key factors to consider include the location, connectivity, security, and scalability of the data center facilities, as well as the provider's track record and expertise in the industry.
