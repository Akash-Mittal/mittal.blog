How to Change the Microsoft Outlook Theme in Web, Mac, & Windows



Image Credits https://m.economictimes.com/news/international/us/microsoft-outlook-heres-how-to-change-the-theme-on-web-mac-windows/articleshow/100884300.cms

Do you use Microsoft Outlook for email but wish it had more personality? Good news! You can totally change the email client's default theme on web, Mac, and Windows devices. Here's how to customize your email experience and make it your own.

Changing the Theme

Before we get started, let me share with you a personal story. For years, I used the default blue theme on my Outlook account. It was functional, but boring. One day, I scrolled through the settings and discovered the theme options. I was thrilled to learn I could choose from a variety of colors and designs. I selected the green theme and felt a sense of joy every time I opened an email. It may seem like a small change, but it truly makes a difference in my Outlook experience.

Now, let's get to the how-to part. The steps to change the theme may vary slightly depending on whether you're using Outlook on the web, Mac, or Windows. Here are the general guidelines for each platform:

  • Outlook on the Web: Go to Settings > Theme and select your desired theme.
  • Outlook for Mac: Go to Preferences > General > Personalize and select your desired theme.
  • Outlook for Windows: Go to File > Options > General > Office Theme and select your desired theme.

Once you've made your selection, save your changes and voila! You now have a customized version of Outlook that reflects your personal style.

Examples of Themes

Not sure which theme to choose? Here are some examples of the themes available:

  • Colorful: A vivid, multi-colored theme that adds a pop of personality to your inbox.
  • Dark Gray: A sleek, modern theme that reduces eye strain and is perfect for working in dimly lit spaces.
  • White: A classic, clean theme that is easy on the eyes and perfect for those who prefer simplicity.

Of course, these are just a few examples. Feel free to explore the full range of Outlook themes and find the one that best suits your needs.

Conclusion

Customizing the theme of Microsoft Outlook is a simple but effective way to make the email client more engaging and enjoyable to use. By selecting a theme that aligns with your personality and preferences, you can personalize your email experience and make it your own.

  1. Choose a theme that reflects your style and personality.
  2. Experiment with different themes until you find the one that feels right.
  3. Enjoy your customized Outlook experience and feel good every time you open an email.

With these steps, you're well on your way to turning a mundane email task into a more visually appealing and satisfying experience.
