Enhancing Data Accessibility in Industry 4.0



Image Credits https://www.datanami.com/this-just-in/sekai-and-dragonlabz-power-parso-enhancing-data-accessibility-in-industry-4-0/

BASEL, Switzerland, June 8, 2023 -- SEKAI, known for its patented AI technology used by Fortune 500 companies, and Dragonlabz, a Swiss-based venture, have partnered to address an important issue in Industry 4.0: data accessibility. As companies increase their reliance on data-driven decisions, it is crucial that they have access to the right information at the right time. However, this can be a challenge in a world where data is dispersed across various sources and formats.

The Challenge of Data Accessibility

Imagine a manufacturing plant that relies on data analytics to optimize its production processes. The plant has sensors that collect real-time data on machine performance, inventory levels, and worker productivity. However, this data is spread across multiple systems and databases, making it difficult to analyze and gain insights from. Without a way to easily access and consolidate this data, the plant may miss opportunities for process improvements and cost savings.

The Solution: Power Parso

To address this challenge, SEKAI and Dragonlabz have developed Power Parso, a platform that enables easy access to data from multiple sources. Power Parso aggregates data from various databases and systems and provides a central repository for analysis. With its user-friendly interface, anyone can access and analyze the data, regardless of technical expertise. Additionally, Power Parso uses AI algorithms to help identify patterns and correlations in the data, providing valuable insights for decision-making.

Real-World Applications

Power Parso has already shown its value in a number of real-world applications. For example, a global pharmaceutical company uses Power Parso to analyze data from clinical trials and drug development studies. By aggregating data from various sources, the company is able to identify potential drug candidates faster and more efficiently. In another case, a shipping company uses Power Parso to optimize its supply chain by analyzing data from its vessels, ports, and logistics partners. By identifying bottlenecks and inefficiencies, the company has been able to reduce costs and improve delivery times.

Conclusion

As Industry 4.0 continues to evolve, data will only become more important. However, access to data will remain a challenge for many companies. Power Parso provides an effective solution to this challenge by enabling easy access to data from multiple sources. Its user-friendly interface and AI algorithms make it accessible to anyone, regardless of technical expertise. With Power Parso, companies can make data-driven decisions faster and more efficiently, leading to greater business success.
