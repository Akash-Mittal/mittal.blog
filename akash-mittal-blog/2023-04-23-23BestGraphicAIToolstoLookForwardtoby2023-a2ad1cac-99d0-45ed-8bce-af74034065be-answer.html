Concrete Examples

Here are some of the best graphic AI tools that Sarah found:

  • Canva - This user-friendly platform allows anyone to create beautiful designs even without a background in design.
  • Designhill - This tool uses AI to generate professional-looking logos for businesses.
  • Adobe Sensei - This AI-powered tool enhances images and automates repetitive tasks.
  • Convertio - This online tool converts images and performs other image-related functions.
  • Fixel - This AI plugin helps to automate the process of color grading images.
  • MockUPhone - This tool allows you to generate mockups of your apps on different devices.
  • Skylum AI - This AI tool helps to enhance and edit images with ease.
  • Stencil - This platform provides social media templates that are customizable and easy to use.
  • Unscreen - This tool helps to remove backgrounds from images quickly and efficiently.
