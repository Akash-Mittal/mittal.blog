Imagine waking up to the sound of birds chirping and stepping out onto your balcony to see the lush greenery of a golf course stretching out before you. This is the reality for residents of the new luxury apartments at Cecil Golf Course in Pittsburgh.

Living on the greens not only provides stunning views, but also has tangible benefits. According to a study by the National Association of Home Builders, homes on or near golf courses have a higher resale value than those that are not. In fact, they found that homes on golf courses typically sell for 8-12% more than their counterparts.

Additionally, living on a golf course often comes with amenities such as access to a clubhouse, pool, and fitness center. All of these amenities can lead to a healthier and more social lifestyle, which can improve overall well-being.

Practical Tips

If you're considering living on the greens, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, research the golf course and make sure it has all the amenities and features you're looking for. You may also want to consider the location and how it fits into your lifestyle and commute.

It's also important to consider the cost. While living on a golf course can lead to a higher resale value, it may also come with a higher price tag. Make sure to factor in all expenses and consider your budget carefully.
