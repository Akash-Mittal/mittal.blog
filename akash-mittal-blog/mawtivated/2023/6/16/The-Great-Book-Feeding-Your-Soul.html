What Makes a Book Great?

In his famous quote, Robertson Davies states that the great book for you is the book that has the most to say to you at the moment when you are reading. Davies emphasizes that the book that feeds your spirit doesn't necessarily have to be instructive. It should cater to your psychological and spiritual need, and that depends on your age and experience.

In other words, a great book is one that resonates with you and speaks to you on a deep, personal level. It could be a novel, a memoir, a self-help book, a religious text, or any other book that sparks something within you.

  • It makes you reflect on your life and values
  • It offers new insights and perspectives
  • It inspires you to be a better person
"The only way to do great work is to love what you do." -Steve Jobs

One of the greatest books ever written, Harper Lee's "To Kill a Mockingbird," is a prime example of how a book can feed your soul. The story of the struggle for justice in America's Deep South in the 1930s touches upon themes such as racism, poverty, and inequality. But what makes the book great is how it shows the power of empathy, compassion, and courage, and how even the smallest act of kindness can make a difference.

Another example of a book that feeds your soul is Viktor Frankl's "Man's Search for Meaning." Frankl, a Holocaust survivor, shares his experience and insights on how to find meaning in life in the face of extreme suffering. The book offers a unique perspective on the human condition and inspires readers to find purpose and hope even in the darkest of times.

Conclusion: How to Find a Great Book

Reading a book that feeds your soul is not only enjoyable but also beneficial for your well-being. In order to find the right book, you need to look within yourself and consider your personal needs and interests. Here are some tips to help you:

  1. Reflect on your values, beliefs, and goals
  2. Explore different genres and authors
  3. Ask for recommendations from friends, family, or book clubs
  4. Read reviews and summaries online

Remember that a great book is not just a source of entertainment or knowledge but also a way to connect with your inner self and the world around you.
