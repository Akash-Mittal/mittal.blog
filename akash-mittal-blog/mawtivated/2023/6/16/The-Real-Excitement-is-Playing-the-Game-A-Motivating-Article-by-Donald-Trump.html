The Real Excitement is Playing the Game: A Motivating Article by Donald Trump



It was a cold winter night in New York. Donald Trump, the famous American businessman, was walking towards his luxurious car after attending an important meeting. Suddenly, a homeless man approached him and begged for some money. Trump could have ignored him, but instead, he took out his wallet and gave him a hundred-dollar bill.

The homeless man was overwhelmed with gratitude and asked Trump, "Why did you do that?". Trump simply replied, "Money was never a big motivation for me, except as a way to keep score. The real excitement is playing the game."

Donald Trump is a well-known personality in the business world. He is the Chairman and President of The Trump Organization, a US-based real estate developer and television personality. Born in 1946 in Queens, New York City, Trump has been a successful businessman for decades.

Examples

  • Steve Jobs, the co-founder of Apple Inc., was motivated by his passion for technology and design rather than money.
  • JK Rowling, the author of the Harry Potter series, was initially rejected by several publishers but never gave up. She continued to play the game, and her persistence paid off in the end.
  • Elon Musk, the CEO of SpaceX and Tesla, is driven by his mission to make the world a better place, rather than just making profits.

When I first started my business, I was focused on making money. However, I quickly realized that money can only provide temporary happiness. It was the thrill of the game that kept me going. I remember the times when I faced challenges and obstacles in my business, but I never gave up. I was inspired by my passion and perseverance, rather than just the profits.

Conclusion

  1. Money can only provide temporary happiness. It is the passion and excitement of the game that fuels us in the long run.
  2. Playing the game involves facing challenges and obstacles, but it is important to never give up and stay motivated.
  3. Success is not just about making profits. It is about making a positive impact and leaving a legacy.
