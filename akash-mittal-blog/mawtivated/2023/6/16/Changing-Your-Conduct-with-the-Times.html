The Power of Changing Your Conduct with the Times



When you look at successful people, you may think that they became successful overnight, but that is never the case. Success requires constant effort and adaptation to changes in the world. It's the ability to keep reinventing yourself that allows you to stay relevant in an ever-changing world. In the words of Niccolo Machiavelli, "Whosoever desires constant success must change his conduct with the times."

The Story of Michael Jordan

One great example of the importance of changing your conduct is Michael Jordan. We all know Michael Jordan as one of the greatest basketball players in history. He won six championships between 1991 and 1998 and is considered the greatest player of all time. However, what most people do not know is that he failed many times before finding success.

When Michael Jordan was in high school, he did not make the varsity team. Instead, he played in the junior varsity team. That failure did not deter him. He improved his game tirelessly, and his work paid off. He eventually made the varsity team and went on to have a successful college career.

But his success did not end there. He continued to work hard and adapt to changes in the game. When he entered the NBA, he was not the great player we know today. He struggled to make a name for himself in the league. However, his tireless work ethic paid off, and he won his first championship in 1991.

But he didn't stop there. He continued to adapt to changes in the game and became even better. With six championships under his belt, Michael Jordan is a great example of the importance of changing your conduct with the times.

Author - Niccolo Machiavelli Story

Niccolo Machiavelli was an Italian diplomat, philosopher, and writer who lived during the Renaissance. He was born in 1469 in Florence, Italy. He was a political adviser to the rulers of Florence and wrote extensively about politics and power. His most famous work is The Prince, in which he discusses how rulers should gain and maintain power.

The quote "Whosoever desires constant success must change his conduct with the times" is just one of the many pearls of wisdom that can be found in Machiavelli's works. His ideas about the importance of adaptation and change are more relevant today than they ever were.

Examples of Changing Conduct with the Times

  • Netflix started as a DVD-by-mail rental service but has since transformed itself into a streaming service that has revolutionized the way we watch TV.
  • Apple started as a computer company but has since expanded into the smartphone, tablet, and smartwatch markets.
  • Blockbuster failed to adapt to the rise of streaming services and went bankrupt in 2010.

Practical Tips for Changing Conduct with the Times

  1. Stay up-to-date with trends and changes in your industry.
  2. Be open-minded and willing to learn new things.
  3. Don't be afraid to take risks and try new things.
  4. Be prepared to adapt to changes quickly and efficiently.

Conclusion: Why Changing Your Conduct with the Times is Important

To succeed in today's world, it is crucial to be adaptable and willing to change. The world is constantly evolving, and those who stay stuck in the past will be left behind. To stay relevant and successful, you must keep reinventing yourself and your business. Learning from the successes and failures of others can help you make informed decisions and change your conduct with the times.

Remember, the journey to success is not a straight line. It requires resilience, effort, and the ability to adapt to changes in the world. Follow Machiavelli's advice, and you will be on your way to constant success.
