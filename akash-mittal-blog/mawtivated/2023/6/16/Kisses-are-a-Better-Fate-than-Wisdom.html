Kisses are a Better Fate than Wisdom



An Inspiring Story

There was once a man who was highly intelligent. He had a degree from a prestigious university and had read countless books. He was always quick to offer his opinion on any subject, and he prided himself on his vast knowledge. One day, he met a woman who was not particularly intelligent, but she was warm and kind. They began to spend time together, and something amazing happened - he found that he loved her more with every passing day. He began to realize that kisses from her were a better fate than all the wisdom he had ever acquired. He discovered that life was not about how much you knew, but about how much you loved.

Author - e. e. cummings

It was e. e. cummings who said that "kisses are a better fate than wisdom". E. E. Cummings was a famous American poet and writer who is best known for his unconventional use of grammar and punctuation. His writing was often characterized by its whimsical and playful tone, as well as its philosophical depth. In many of his works, cummings explored the themes of love, nature, and the human experience. He believed that the most important things in life were not intellectual pursuits, but emotional ones.
