In oneself lies the whole world



Let me share a story with you. Once upon a time, there was a young boy named John who was born and raised in a small town. John was an average student but always had big dreams. He wanted to become a successful businessman and make his parents proud.

One day, John attended a seminar on self-improvement, and the speaker quoted Jiddu Krishnamurti: "In oneself lies the whole world and if you know how to look and learn, the door is there and the key is in your hand. Nobody on earth can give you either the key or the door to open, except yourself."

This quote deeply resonated with John and changed his life forever. He realized that he had the power within himself to achieve his dreams and open the door to success. From that day on, John started taking responsibility for his life and worked hard to make his dreams come true. And eventually, he did become a successful businessman, just as he had always imagined.

About Jiddu Krishnamurti

Jiddu Krishnamurti was an Indian philosopher, speaker, and writer. He is regarded as one of the greatest thinkers of the 20th century and his teachings have influenced millions of people around the world. Krishnamurti believed that each person has the power and responsibility to understand their own mind and transform their lives. He encouraged people to question all authority and conventions, and to find their own truth through self-inquiry.

How to unlock the door to success

  • Believe in yourself: The first step towards unlocking the door to success is to believe that you have the power within yourself to achieve your goals. Don't let anyone else tell you otherwise.
  • Take action: Success doesn't come to those who wait. You need to take action towards your goals every day, even if it's just a small step. Consistent effort will eventually lead to significant results.
  • Learn from your failures: Failure is not the opposite of success; it's a part of the journey. Embrace your failures and use them as opportunities to learn and grow. Remember, every successful person has failed many times before finally succeeding.

Conclusion

  1. Believe in yourself
  2. Take action
  3. Learn from your failures

The key to unlocking the door to success lies within you. Take responsibility for your life, believe in yourself, take action, and learn from your failures. With consistent effort and self-inquiry, you can achieve anything you set your mind to.
