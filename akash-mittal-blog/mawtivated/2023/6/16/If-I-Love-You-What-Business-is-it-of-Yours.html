If I Love You, What Business is it of Yours?



A story about love, its complexities, and the importance of personal choices

Once upon a time...

There lived a girl named Mia who had just graduated from college. She was an ambitious young woman with a bright future ahead of her, until she fell in love with a boy named Luke.

Luke was not the kind of guy that Mia's parents approved of. He played in a band, had a few tattoos, and didn't have a steady job. Despite all of this, Mia was head over heels in love with him.

Her parents warned her about getting involved with someone like Luke, but Mia didn't listen. She felt alive and happy when she was with him, and that was all that mattered to her. She didn't care about his flaws or what others thought of him. She loved him for who he was.

One day, Mia's parents gave her an ultimatum - leave Luke or get disowned. Mia was devastated. She loved her parents, but she also loved Luke. She didn't know what to do.

That's when she remembered a famous quote by Johann Wolfgang von Goethe - "If I love you, what business is it of yours?" She realized that her love for Luke was her own personal choice, and it was not up to anyone else to dictate who she should love.

Background on Johann Wolfgang von Goethe

Johann Wolfgang von Goethe was a German writer and statesman who lived in the late 18th and early 19th centuries. He was widely regarded as one of the greatest literary figures in Western history, known for his plays, poetry, and novels.

Goethe was a firm believer in personal choice and individual will. He felt that it was important for people to make their own decisions, even if those decisions went against societal conventions.

His quote "If I love you, what business is it of yours?" is often used to express the idea that love is a personal choice, and that others should not interfere with whom we choose to love.

Examples of Love as a Personal Choice

  • An interracial couple who faces discrimination from their families and society, but still chooses to be together because of their love.
  • A person who chooses to come out as LGBTQ+ despite the risks and consequences, because they want to live authentically and love who they love.
  • A person who falls in love with someone who is not their religion, and decides to pursue the relationship despite objections from their community.

Practical Tips for Making Personal Choices

  1. Reflect on your values and beliefs. What is truly important to you, and what are you willing to stand up for?
  2. Consider the consequences of your actions. Will your choices negatively impact others, or will they bring happiness and fulfillment?
  3. Don't be afraid to seek support and advice from trusted friends and family, but ultimately make the decision that feels right for you.
