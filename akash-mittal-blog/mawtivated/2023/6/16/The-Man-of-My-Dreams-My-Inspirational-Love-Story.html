The Man of My Dreams: My Inspirational Love Story



For many of us, finding the love of our life is one of the greatest joys we can experience. It can be a journey full of twists and turns, and sometimes it can feel like the search will never end. But when you finally find that special someone, everything else fades away and you know that this is the person you were meant to spend your life with. That's exactly how I felt when I met my husband, the man of my dreams.

The Beginning of My Journey

My name is Cindy Margolis and I have been called the "Queen of the Internet" and "America's Most Downloaded Woman". But before all of that, I was just a young girl with big dreams. One of those dreams was to find true love. I grew up in a small town in California and went to college at Cal State Northridge. That's where I met my ex-husband, but unfortunately, things didn't work out as I had hoped.

After the divorce, I took some time to heal and focus on myself. I worked hard and built my career, but I never gave up on the hope of finding love again. I went on countless dates, but nobody seemed to click with me in the way I had hoped.

Meeting the Man of My Dreams

It wasn't until 1997, when I was on a photoshoot in Lake Tahoe, that fate finally brought me face to face with the man of my dreams. His name was Guy Starkman and he worked in advertising. We hit it off right away and after a few months of dating, I knew this was the man I wanted to spend the rest of my life with.

On July 17th, 1998, we got married in a beautiful ceremony in Maui. It was a magical day and I knew that I had found my happily ever after.

Lessons Learned on My Journey

Looking back on my journey to find love, there are a few important lessons that I learned along the way:

  • Know what you want: Before you can find the person of your dreams, you need to know what you are looking for. Take some time to figure out what qualities are important to you in a partner and don't settle for anything less.
  • Be patient: Finding love takes time. It's important not to rush into anything and to give yourself time to really get to know someone before committing to a relationship.
  • Believe in yourself: It's easy to get discouraged when you feel like you aren't making any progress in your search for love. But it's important to remember that there is someone out there who is perfect for you. Keep the faith and believe in yourself.

A Final Thought

My journey to finding the man of my dreams wasn't easy, but it was worth it. Guy and I have been married for over 20 years now and our love only grows stronger with each passing day. I truly believe that there is someone out there for everyone and that if you keep an open heart and mind, you will find your happily ever after too.
