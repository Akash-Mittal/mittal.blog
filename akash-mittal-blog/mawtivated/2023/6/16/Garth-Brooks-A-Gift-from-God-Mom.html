Garth Brooks: A Gift from God & Mom



As a child growing up in Oklahoma, Garth Brooks loved music. He listened to his mom's Capitol Records and practiced guitar in his bedroom. He dreamed of becoming a country singer one day. That dream became a reality when he signed with Capitol Records himself and released his first album in 1989. Since then, Garth Brooks has become one of the most successful artists in country music history. But he knows he didn't get there on his own.

"If I have any talent at all it's from God, and my mom, who was on Capitol Records also," Brooks once said. He understands that his gift and his success are not entirely his own doing. He recognizes that he has been blessed with talent and opportunity. But unlike some artists who take all the credit for their success, Brooks gives credit where credit is due. He attributes his talent to a higher power and to his mother, who was also a musician.
