Designing with Technology: An Exciting Journey Ahead



Imagine a world without colors, a world without textures and a world without fabrics! How dull and lifeless our world would have been! But luckily, with the advent of technology, everything around us has become more colorful, vibrant and cheerful. And this is not just restricted to our attire or surroundings but has made its way into designing as well. With the help of technology, the world of design has become more creative, innovative and fun.

David Bromstad, an award-winning designer, shares his story on how technology has transformed his designing experience. David was born in Minnesota and always had a passion for art and design. He graduated from Ringling College of Art and Design and started his career as a Disney illustrator. He rose to fame after winning the first season of HGTV Design Star in 2006.

"Technology is making design more exciting, with color, wallpaper, textures, fabrics that could never have been created without the technology." - David Bromstad

The Journey of Designing with Technology

The world of design has come a long way since David's early days. This is mainly because of the technology intertwined with design. It has unlocked boundless opportunities for designers who can now experiment with new ideas, colors, fabrics and textures without any constraints.

Colors

Colors have been an integral part of designing since its inception. However, with the aid of technology, these have undergone significant transformation. Designers now have access to color combinations which were otherwise unimaginable. They can experiment with bold and vibrant hues as well as soft and pastel shades effortlessly.

Wallpapers

The use of wallpapers in designing interiors has been prevalent for a while. But, technology has made it possible for designers to create custom wallpapers that complement the decor and break the monotony of solid colored walls. These wallpapers come in various patterns, textures, and designs that add an element of intrigue to space.

Textures

The use of textures in designing has been a game-changer, from rough wood finish to glossy metallic. Designers can now create textures that mimic any surface, even exotic ones, giving a new level of depth to their designs.

Fabrics

Fabrics form an integral part of any design, be it fashion, home decor, or even furniture. The rise of technology has brought a paradigm shift in the textile industry, making it possible to create bespoke fabrics with intricate patterns, designs and textures that could have never been created before. New textile printing and manufacturing methods have made it possible for designers to customize their designs and create unique pieces.

Conclusion

Designing with technology has opened up a vast array of possibilities, new materials, and methods that designers can use to create innovative and unique designs. This has revolutionized the world of design, from creating bespoke fabrics, textures and colors to creating virtual design prototypes and satisfying customer's needs. Designers are free to experiment and create exactly what they want without being limited by their technical abilities. With technology ever-evolving and improving, we can expect more inspiring and dynamic designs in the future!
