Designing Your Dreams: The Story of Jonathan Ive



An Inspiring Story:

Jonathan Ive's journey to become one of the greatest designers of all time is nothing less than inspiring. Born in London, he grew up passionate about design and originally intended to become an architect. However, after studying industrial design, he became increasingly interested in technology and, as a result, landed a job at Apple in 1992 as a designer. For the next two decades, he was responsible for designing some of the most iconic products that we still use todayâ€“the iPod, iPhone, and iPad. He recently left Apple in 2019 to start his own firm called LoveFrom, where he continues to work on innovative design projects.

Becoming a Designer:

Jonathan Ive didn't become a renowned designer overnight. It took years of hard work, experimentation, and persistence to make his dreams a reality. He learned design skills by studying industrial design at Newcastle Polytechnic and the Royal College of Art in London, where he received his Master of Arts degree in 1989. During his time in school, he honed his craft and developed his design philosophy, which he still uses to this day.

The Power of Collaboration:

One of the most important lessons we can learn from Ive's story is the power of collaboration. Although he was a brilliant designer himself, he recognized that he couldn't create Apple's iconic products on his own. He spent countless hours working with engineers, marketers, and other designers to come up with the designs that we know and love today. By working together, they were able to create products that were not just aesthetically pleasing, but also functional and intuitive.

Designing Your Dreams:

If you're interested in pursuing a career in design, there are a few things you can learn from Ive's story:

  • Develop a strong work ethic: Becoming a great designer takes a lot of hard work. You need to be willing to put in the time and effort to hone your craft and develop your skills.
  • Find your design philosophy: What do you believe is important in design? Spend some time thinking about your values and how you want to approach your work.
  • Collaborate with others: Don't be afraid to work with others and bounce ideas off of each other. Collaboration can be one of the most powerful tools for achieving great design.
