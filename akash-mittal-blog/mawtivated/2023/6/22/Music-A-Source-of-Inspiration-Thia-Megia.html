Music: A Source of Inspiration - Thia Megia



Author - Thia Megia is a Filipino-American singer and songwriter who gained national and international recognition after participating on the tenth season of American Idol. She works to inspire others through her music and touching lyrics that connect to emotions.

A Source of Inspiration

Music has been a way for humans to connect, feel, remember, and express themselves for centuries. It has the power to move us emotionally, physically, and mentally. Whether it's a catchy melody or heart-touching lyrics, music can inspire us to do amazing things in our lives.

One such inspiring story comes from John Ondrasik, a singer-songwriter who found out that his song "Superman (It's Not Easy)" had a huge impact on people's lives. Ondrasik received letters from soldiers fighting in Iraq who said that the song helped them through tough times, and he also heard from parents who had lost children in the 9/11 attacks who said that the song gave them comfort. This story is a testament to the power that music can have in our lives, in ways that even the artist never imagined.

My Inspiring Journey

As a singer-songwriter, I have personally experienced the power of music to inspire others. Just knowing that through my music I actually inspire people is amazing for me and I find it very heartwarming. I remember one time when I was performing at a local gig, a woman from the audience came up to me and said that my music had helped her cope with her recent divorce. She said that my lyrics spoke directly to her heart and made her feel like she wasn't alone. That moment filled me with gratitude and inspiration to continue to write meaningful music that resonates with people's emotions.

Examples of Inspiring Music

  • "I Will Survive" by Gloria Gaynor - A song about resilience and overcoming adversity that has become a staple of empowering playlists.
  • "What a Wonderful World" by Louis Armstrong - A timeless and uplifting song that reminds us of the beauty in life even in times of hardship.
  • "Man in the Mirror" by Michael Jackson - A call to action for making positive change in the world, starting with ourselves.
  • "Brave" by Sara Bareilles - An anthem for standing up for oneself and being brave enough to be yourself.
"Music expresses that which cannot be said and on which it is impossible to be silent." - Victor Hugo

Tips for Finding Inspiring Music

With so much music out there, it can be overwhelming to find something that moves you and inspires you. Here are some tips to help you find inspiring music:

  • Listen to different genres
  • Read lyrics and research the meaning behind the song
  • Pay attention to your emotional response
  • Look for artists who are authentic and passionate about their music

Conclusion

  1. Music has the power to inspire us and connect us emotionally with others.
  2. Personal experiences like John Ondrasik's and my own have shown that music can have a profound impact on people's lives.
  3. When looking for inspiring music, consider different genres, research the meaning behind the songs, and pay attention to your emotional response to the music.
