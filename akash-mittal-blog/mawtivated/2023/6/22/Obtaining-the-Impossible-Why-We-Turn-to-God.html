Obtaining the Impossible: Why We Turn to God



An Inspirational Story of Faith and Hope

By Albert Camus

There was a man named John who had lost everything. His wife had left him, his business had gone bankrupt, and he had developed serious health problems. He was at the lowest point in his life and felt like there was no hope left. But one day, he heard a sermon at church about the power of God and how He can do the impossible. John was skeptical at first, but as he continued to attend church and read the Bible, he began to see a glimmer of hope.

John started praying to God and asking for help. At first, he didn't see any changes in his circumstances, but he didn't give up. He continued to have faith and trust in God, even when things seemed hopeless. One day, he received a call from an old friend who offered him a job. It wasn't a great job, but it was a start. And from there, things slowly began to improve for him.

John's story is just one example of why people turn toward God, especially when they feel like they have hit rock bottom. In times of despair, it's natural to seek out something greater than ourselves and to hope for a miracle.

The Impossible Dreams We Pursue

Why do we turn toward God only to obtain the impossible? The answer lies in the dreams and aspirations we all have. These dreams may seem impossible to achieve on our own, but through faith and prayer, we believe that God can make them a reality.

  • A young woman dreams of becoming a doctor, but comes from a family of limited means.
  • A man wants to start his own business, but lacks the necessary resources and connections.
  • A couple hopes to conceive a child, but has struggled with infertility for years.

These are just a few examples of the dreams that seem impossible to achieve on our own. But with God's help, we believe that anything is possible.

The Power of Faith and Prayer

When we turn toward God, we are acknowledging that we need help beyond what we can do for ourselves. We are admitting that we don't have all the answers, and that we need guidance and support. By putting our faith in God and praying for our dreams to come true, we are opening ourselves up to a greater power.

"Faith is to believe what you do not see; the reward of this faith is to see what you believe." - Saint Augustine

The power of faith and prayer is not just about asking for things and hoping that they magically happen. It's about trusting that there is a greater plan in place, and that everything happens for a reason. We may not always understand why things happen the way they do, but we can trust that God has a purpose for our lives.

Conclusion: How to Hold on to Faith When Things are Tough

So why do we turn toward God only to obtain the impossible? It's because we believe that with God, all things are possible. When we are faced with impossible dreams, we turn to a greater power and put our faith in Him to make those dreams come true.

Here are three practical tips for holding on to faith when things are tough:

  1. Pray daily, even if it's just for a few minutes.
  2. Read the Bible for inspiration and guidance.
  3. Seek out a supportive community of faith.

By doing these things, we can hold on to our faith and trust that God will make the impossible possible.
