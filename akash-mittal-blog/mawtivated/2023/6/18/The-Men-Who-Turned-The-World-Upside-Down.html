The Men Who Turned The World Upside Down



Billy Graham, a renowned evangelist, once told a story about an old man who lived in a remote village. He was known to possess a special gift of healing. People would travel from afar just to see him. One day, a journalist met the old man and asked him about his secret. The old man replied, "I don't heal anyone. I just give them hope." Billy Graham himself was a man who gave hope to many. He was known for his powerful sermons that had the ability to transform lives. Graham's background was humble, but he became one of the most influential evangelists in history. But Graham was not alone in his mission. The men who followed Jesus two thousand years ago were unique in their generation. They were simple fishermen, tax collectors, and tradesmen. Yet, they had something that set them apart from the rest. They had encountered Jesus, and their lives were never the same again. Jesus had turned their hearts right side up. He had given them a new perspective on life, and they were passionate about sharing it with others. They turned the world upside down because their hearts had been turned right side up. These men were not just talkers; they were doers. They lived out their message with boldness and courage. They faced opposition, persecution, and even death, yet they refused to be silenced. They performed miracles, healed the sick, and preached the gospel to all who would listen. They were living examples of what it means to follow Jesus. One example is the story of Paul, formerly known as Saul. He was a persecutor of Christians until he encountered Jesus on the road to Damascus. His transformed life had a powerful impact on those around him. He became a prolific writer, and his letters to various churches continue to inspire and challenge readers to this day. The world has never been the same since these men walked the earth. Their message of hope, love, and salvation has transcended time and culture. They continue to influence millions of lives today. So, how can we apply their message to our own lives? Here are a few practical tips:

  • Encounter Jesus: The first step is to encounter Jesus for ourselves. This is not just an intellectual exercise, but a personal experience that transforms our hearts.
  • Live out our message: We need to live out our message with boldness and courage. Our lives should reflect the love and compassion of Jesus.
  • Share our message: Finally, we need to share our message with others. We can do this through our words, deeds, and lifestyle.
In conclusion, the men who followed Jesus turned the world upside down because their hearts had been turned right side up. They continue to inspire and challenge us today. Let us follow their example and live out the message of hope, love, and salvation.
