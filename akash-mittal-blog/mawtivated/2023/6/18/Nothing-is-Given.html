Nothing is Given - Struggle and Conflict in Pursuit of Values



An Inspiring Story of Perseverance

John, a young man from a small town, dreamed of becoming a doctor. But his family couldn't afford to pay for his education, and he had to work odd jobs to support himself. Despite the odds stacked against him, John never gave up on his dreams. He studied tirelessly, even using books borrowed from a library, and eventually managed to secure a scholarship. After years of struggle and hard work, John became a successful doctor, proving that nothing is given on earth - everything worth having requires perseverance and persistence.

The Nature of Life

Nothing worth having comes easy. Life is full of struggles and obstacles that must be overcome. From the moment we are born, we are faced with challenges. We must learn to crawl, walk, talk, and communicate. As we grow older, we encounter new obstacles, such as making friends, passing exams, and finding a job. These challenges can be daunting, and many people give up before they have even begun. But the hero is the person who perseveres, no matter what obstacles come their way.

  • Life is full of struggles and obstacles
  • Challenges can be daunting, but must be overcome
  • The hero is the person who perseveres
"I know of no more encouraging fact than the unquestionable ability of man to elevate his life by conscious endeavor." - Henry David Thoreau

The Heroic Journey

The hero's journey is a common motif in literature and mythology. It is the story of a hero who faces challenges and obstacles on their quest for a goal or purpose. The hero must overcome these obstacles in order to achieve their goal, and in doing so, they become stronger, wiser, and more resilient. This journey is not just for mythical heroes - it is a journey that we can all undertake in pursuit of our own values and goals.

  • The hero's journey is the story of overcoming obstacles
  • By facing challenges, the hero becomes stronger and wiser
  • We can all undertake the hero's journey in pursuit of our own values and goals
"A hero is an ordinary individual who finds the strength to persevere and endure in spite of overwhelming obstacles." - Christopher Reeve

The Power of Persistence

Persistence is the key to success. It is the ability to keep going, even in the face of adversity. Persistence is what sets apart the successful from the unsuccessful. A person who is persistent never gives up on their dreams, even when the going gets tough. They keep pushing forward, one step at a time, until they reach their goal. Persistence is a skill that can be developed through practice and hard work, and it is essential for anyone who wants to achieve great things.

  • Persistence is the key to success
  • A person who is persistent never gives up on their dreams
  • Persistence is a skill that can be developed through practice and hard work
"Perseverance is failing nineteen times and succeeding the twentieth." - Julie Andrews

Conclusion

  1. Life is full of struggles and obstacles, but the hero is the person who perseveres.
  2. The hero's journey is a journey we can all undertake in pursuit of our own values and goals.
  3. Persistence is the key to success and essential for anyone who wants to achieve great things.

Remember, nothing is given to us on earth - we must all struggle and fight for what we want. But if we persevere, if we undertake the hero's journey, and if we are persistent in our pursuit of our values and goals, we can achieve greatness.
