"I am Happy as Happiness Goes" - A Story of Lillie Langtry



From Rags to Riches - Lillie's Story

Lillie Langtry started her life in poverty, born to a family of 13 children in the small island of Jersey in the British Isles. But she always had a dream, a dream to escape the island life and become someone important. She began her journey as a teenager, first as a schoolteacher on the mainland, and then as a governess for a wealthy family where she met her future husband, Edward Langtry.

But despite her marriage, Lillie was restless. She wanted to be noticed, to be famous. And she saw her chance when she was asked to model for a portrait that would be displayed at the Royal Academy. The painting caught the attention of many, including the Prince of Wales, and thus, Lillie's rise to fame began.

She became the Prince's mistress, a daring move in a time when such relationships were frowned upon. But Lillie was not the type to be held back by conventions. She became a stage actress, playing to packed audiences in London and New York. She even went on to start her own successful production company.

But fame and fortune came at a price. Lillie's personal life was filled with scandals and heartbreak. She divorced Edward Langtry and went on to have several tumultuous relationships. Her only son died in his 20s, and she struggled with alcoholism in her later years.

Yet despite the highs and lows of her life, Lillie always had a positive outlook. As she once said, "I am happy as happiness goes, for a woman who has so many memories and who lives the lonely life of an actress."

What We Can Learn from Lillie

Lillie Langtry's story is a reminder that success can come from humble beginnings with hard work and determination. She never let anyone or anything hold her back from achieving her dreams. But more importantly, Lillie taught us to embrace life with a positive attitude, despite its ups and downs.

Here are a few practical tips we can learn from Lillie:

  • Believe in yourself. Lillie had a dream to become someone important, and she never gave up on that dream, even when others doubted her.
  • Take risks. Lillie's decision to become the Prince's mistress and pursue a career in acting were both risks. But without taking risks, she wouldn't have achieved the success she did.
  • Keep a positive attitude. Lillie faced many challenges in her personal life, but she never let them get her down. She found happiness in her memories and the life she had chosen for herself.

A Final Thought

"I am happy as happiness goes, for a woman who has so many memories and who lives the lonely life of an actress."

Lillie Langtry's life was a testament to the power of believing in oneself and embracing life with a positive attitude, even in the face of adversity. Let us take inspiration from her story and try to live our own lives with the same courage and optimism.
