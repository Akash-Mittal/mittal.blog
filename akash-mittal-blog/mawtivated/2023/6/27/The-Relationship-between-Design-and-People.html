The Relationship between Design and People



Author: Genevieve Gorder

Design is not just about aesthetics, it's about understanding the needs of people. As a designer, I learned this lesson early on in my career when I took on a challenging project for a family with quadruplets.

The couple had just moved into a new home and were struggling to make it work for their family of six. They needed help creating a space that could accommodate their children's unique needs without sacrificing style. After spending time with the family, observing how they interacted, and listening to their concerns, I was able to design a space that met both their functional and aesthetic needs.

This experience taught me that design is not an ambush, it's a relationship. You have to understand the people you are designing for to create spaces that work for them.

The Importance of Understanding People in Design

Design is not just about making things look pretty. It's about solving problems and meeting the needs of people. To do this effectively, designers must have a deep understanding of the people they are designing for. Here are a few reasons why:

  • Design solutions that meet the needs of people are more likely to be successful.
  • Designing for people's needs can improve their quality of life.
  • Designing with people in mind can help build better relationships between designers and clients.

Examples of Designing for People

One example of designing for people is the work of architect Michael Murphy, who designed a school in Rwanda with the needs of the students in mind. The building is designed to provide natural light and ventilation, making for a comfortable and healthy learning environment. Murphy's design also includes spaces for community gatherings, which strengthen relationships between students, teachers, and the surrounding community.

Another example comes from the company Airbnb, which designs its properties with the needs of travelers in mind. The company hires local designers to create spaces that feel like home for travelers. This approach has been successful for Airbnb, as it has grown into a multi-billion dollar company.

Conclusion: Designing for People

Designing for people is essential to creating successful, sustainable, and enjoyable spaces. To design for people, designers must understand their needs and how they move, live, and work. The design process should involve observation, listening, and communication to ensure that the final product meets the needs of people.

  1. Design solutions that meet the needs of people are more likely to be successful.
  2. Designing for people's needs can improve their quality of life.
  3. Designing with people in mind can help build better relationships between designers and clients.
