The Total Poetry of Family



When Karl Wilhelm Friedrich Schlegel said, "If you want to see mankind fully, look at a family. Within the family minds become organically one, and for this reason the family is total poetry," it became clear that family is an essential component of human nature.

Author - Karl Wilhelm Friedrich Schlegel

Karl Wilhelm Friedrich Schlegel was a German poet, philosopher, literary critic, and a major figure in German Romanticism. He believed that the family, as the basic unit of society, displays the nature of mankind in its most organic form. His statement about the family being "total poetry" emphasizes the family's intrinsic value.

Examples of Total Poetry in Families

1. Shared Experiences

A family that spends time together forms a bond that cannot be broken easily. Shared experiences like hiking, camping, or traveling together create memories that stay with the family for life.

2. Support and Encouragement

Family members who support and encourage each other enable growth and development. A supportive family contributes to the success of each individual member.

3. Love and Affection

Unconditional love and affection within a family creates a sense of security and trust. A family that expresses love openly creates an environment that promotes emotional well-being.

Conclusion: Three Points to Take Away

  • The family reflects the nature of mankind in its most organic form
  • Shared experiences, support and encouragement, and love and affection are essential to a strong family bond
  • The family provides a foundation for personal and emotional growth

It is essential to remember that the family is an important part of our lives. We must prioritize spending time with our loved ones, support and encourage each other, and show love and affection. In doing so, we create a strong and supportive family unit that can weather any challenge.
