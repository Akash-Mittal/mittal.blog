The Lily Pad Journey of Faith



An Inspiring Story of Anne Lamott

Anne Lamott, a renowned American novelist and non-fiction writer, once said, "My coming to faith did not start with a leap but rather a series of staggers from what seemed like one safe place to another. Like lily pads, round and green, these places summoned and then held me up while I grew. Each prepared me for the next leaf on which I would land, and in this way I moved across the swamp of doubt and fear."

This metaphor of the lily pad journey of faith beautifully captures the essence of Anne Lamott's personal experience with faith. Her narrative not only inspires but also provides a practical framework for anyone who is on a journey of faith to navigate their way through the swamp of doubts and fears.

The Journey

  1. Staggers from Safe Places: Anne Lamott's journey to faith was not a sudden leap but a series of staggers from one safe place to another. She had to leave her comfort zone to explore the unknown. This staggers from a safe place indicates that one needs to take a step forward to embark on this journey of faith.
  2. Summoned to Safe Places: The round and green lily pad is a metaphor describing the safe places where Anne Lamott found herself throughout her journey. She calls them summoned because each place pulled her towards it, like a calling from a divine force. These safe places are like stepping stones, and each step leads to the next leaf on which one lands.
  3. Holding Up While You Grow: These safe places are not only for protection but also for nurturing growth. These places provide the necessary support required to grow and navigate through the swamp of doubts and fears. They help to develop an individual's character and strengthen their faith. Essential in this process is a willingness to grow, change, and let go of prior beliefs for new ones.
  4. Preparing for the Next Leaf: Each safe place prepares an individual for the next leaf and the challenges of the unknown. It helps to instill confidence and trust in oneself for the journey ahead.
  5. Moving across the Swamp of Doubt and Fear: The ultimate goal of the lily pad journey of faith is to move across the swamp of doubt and fear. These doubts and fears are like the murky and perilous waters that hinder our progress in the journey. However, with trust and confidence in oneself, one can cross the swamp towards the truth and clarity.

Practical Tips

  • Start with small steps: Like Anne Lamott, take small steps from one safe place to another rather than a sudden leap.
  • Be open to new beliefs: Let go of preconceived notions and be open to exploring new perspectives and beliefs.
  • Embrace growth: Use each safe place to grow and learn new things about oneself and the journey of faith.
  • Trust the journey: Believe that every safe place is leading you towards the ultimate goal of crossing the swamp of doubt and fear.
  • Seek guidance: Seek guidance and wisdom from trusted mentors or spiritual leaders to navigate through the journey.

Conclusion

In conclusion, like the lily pad journey of faith, the path towards self-discovery and spirituality is never a straight line. It is a journey filled with twists and turns, but with each step, one gains wisdom and grows closer to truth. Anne Lamott's metaphor of the lily pad journey of faith provides a practical framework for all those seeking to navigate their way through this journey. Embrace growth, trust the journey, and be open to new beliefs, and you will ultimately cross the swamp of doubt and fear.
