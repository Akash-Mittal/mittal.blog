My Mom's Strictness: Lessons Learned



It was a hot summer day, and I was on my way to the park with my friends. We planned to play basketball for hours until the sun started to set. As I was leaving the house, my mom called out to me from the porch. "Don't forget your sunscreen and water, and be back before 7 pm," she said with a stern voice. I rolled my eyes and waved goodbye, feeling annoyed at her care and rules that seemed unnecessary to me at the time.

Little did I know that my mom's strictness and discipline would shape me into the person I am today. Her love and guidance were always there, even if I didn't understand it at the time. Looking back, I realize how fortunate I was to have a mom who cared for my wellbeing and future.

The Power of a Strict Parenting Style

As children, we often rebel against rules and boundaries, seeking independence and freedom. However, research shows that a strict parenting style can have positive effects on children's development and success.

"The most successful children have parents who are both demanding and responsive. Demanding means setting high standards and expecting achievement from children. Responsive means providing warmth, support, and openness to their children's needs. Put the two together and you get the authoritative style of parenting."
-Ellen Galinsky, President of Families and Work Institute
  • A strict parenting style teaches children responsibility, discipline, and self-control.
  • It creates a sense of security and stability for the child, knowing there are clear expectations and consequences.
  • It sets a foundation for success, as children learn to prioritize their goals and work hard to achieve them.

Gloria Estefan: A Success Story of Strict Parenting

Gloria Estefan, a Cuban-American singer, actress, and entrepreneur, is a prime example of how strict parenting can lead to greatness. Growing up, her mom would wake her up at 5 am to practice piano before school, and her dad would give her tough love during track and field practice.

Despite the strictness, Gloria's parents instilled in her a strong work ethic, determination, and love for music. She began singing and playing guitar with her family's band and eventually formed the Miami Sound Machine. Today, Gloria is a seven-time Grammy award winner and one of the most successful Latin crossover artists in music history.

Applying Strict Parenting in Real Life

Here are some practical tips on how to incorporate strict parenting in everyday life:

  1. Set clear expectations and consequences with your child. Be consistent and follow through with what you say.
  2. Don't be afraid to say no. Children need to learn that the world won't always cater to their wants and needs.
  3. Practice tough love. Encourage and support your child while also pushing them out of their comfort zone and challenging them to be their best.

Conclusion:

  1. A strict parenting style can have positive effects on children's development and success.
  2. Parents who are both demanding and responsive create an authoritative parenting style.
  3. Strict parenting can lead to greatness, as seen in the success story of Gloria Estefan.

Overall, strict parenting is not easy, but it can make all the difference in your child's life. Remember to balance rules and love, and trust that your child will thank you later for the lessons learned.
