The Greatest Man I Ever Knew: A Tribute to My Father



Angelina Jolie has made a career out of acting in some of the most iconic films of our time, but she is the first to admit that her greatest hero is her father. Famous actor Jon Voight may be a familiar face on the big screen, but it is his role as a father that has left a lasting impact on his daughter. In this tribute, Angelina Jolie tells the world why her dad is not only an incredible actor but an even better father.

An Inspiring Story

One of the most inspiring stories that Angelina shares about her father is the way he supported her decision to take on her first role in a leading film. At the time, Angelina was a struggling actress trying to make a name for herself in Hollywood. While she was trying to break through, she was offered the leading role in the movie, "Hackers."

"My dad wanted me to pursue my dreams," Angelina reminisces. "When I told him about the role in 'Hackers,' he was so supportive. He read the script and knew it was a great opportunity for me. Even though he was on set filming another movie, he made sure to call and check in with me every night to see how it was going."

Angelina credits this experience as one of the turning points in her career, and it was all thanks to her dad's unwavering support.

Lessons Learned from My Father

Through the years, Angelina has learned many valuable lessons from her father. Here are just a few:

  • Believe in Yourself. "My dad always told me that if I believed in myself, I could achieve anything," says Angelina. "He instilled this confidence in me from a young age, and it's carried me through some of the toughest times in my life."
  • Family Comes First. "Even though my dad is a famous actor, he never put his career ahead of his family," Angelina explains. "He always made time for us and showed us that we were the most important thing in his life."
  • Be Grateful. "My dad has taught me to be grateful for everything I have in life," says Angelina. "Even when times were tough growing up, he reminded us that there was always something to be thankful for."

These are just a few of the many lessons that Angelina has learned from her father. She is grateful for his guidance and support through the years, and she hopes to pass on these lessons to her own children.
