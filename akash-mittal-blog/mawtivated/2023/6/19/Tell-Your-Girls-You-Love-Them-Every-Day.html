Tell Your Girls You Love Them Every Day



An Inspiring Story

There was a little girl, let's call her Emma, who lived in a small house with her parents and two elder brothers. Emma's parents were always busy with work and household chores, and the brothers were too old to play with her. So, Emma often found herself alone, craving for attention and love.

One day, Emma's mother took her to a family gathering, where Emma met her cousin Katie. Katie's father, unlike Emma's, was a very loving and affectionate man. He hugged and kissed Katie, called her his princess, and praised her every little achievement. Emma was in awe of Katie and her father's relationship, and wished her own father could be like that.

Unfortunately, Emma's wish never came true. Growing up, Emma felt unloved and invisible, and the lack of affection affected her self-esteem and relationships with other men. It wasn't until she met someone who showed her the love and attention she craved for, that she realized the importance of fathers loving their daughters, and telling them so every day.

As comedian Chris Rock once said, "I've seen women who don't have great relationships with their dads, and it all comes down to this: You have to tell girls you love them every day."

Why It's Important

According to research, a father's love and affection, or lack thereof, can greatly affect a daughter's personality, behavior, and emotions. Girls who grow up with affectionate fathers tend to develop healthy self-esteem, confidence, and better relationships with men. They also tend to perform better academically and have lower rates of depression and anxiety.

On the other hand, girls who grow up with absent, neglectful, or abusive fathers, or those who fail to show love and affection, often develop low self-esteem, trust issues, and engage in risky behavior such as drug abuse, early sexual activity, and unhealthy relationships with men.

That's why it's important for fathers to show their love and affection towards their daughters, not just through material gifts or financial support, but through the words they say, the time they spend, and the attention they give.

How To Do It

Here are some practical tips on how fathers can express their love and affection towards their daughters:

  • Tell them "I love you" every day
  • Give them hugs and kisses regularly
  • Ask them about their feelings and listen to them without judgment
  • Encourage and support their interests and pursuits
  • Be present and engaged in their lives
  • Apologize and make amends when you make mistakes
  • Show them respect and treat them as equals
  • Teach them life skills and values

It's never too late to start showing your daughter your love and affection. Even if your relationship has been strained or distant, you can still make things right by reaching out and expressing your feelings.

Conclusion

To sum up, fathers play a crucial role in their daughter's emotional and psychological development. By showing love and affection towards their daughters, fathers can empower them, boost their self-esteem, and shape their future relationships with men. Therefore, every father should make it a habit to tell their girls they love them every day, and to show it through their words and actions.
