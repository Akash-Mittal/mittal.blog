From Mailroom to Director: The Journey of Famous Directors



An inspiring story on how starting in the mailroom can pave the way to becoming a successful director

The Mailroom: The Gateway to a Dream Career

Starting from the bottom and working your way up the ranks is a familiar concept in most career paths, and the film industry is no exception. Many famous directors that we admire today started their journey in the mailroom of major studios. It may seem like a lowly position, but getting your foot in the door is a crucial step in achieving your dream career in the film industry.

A Director's Story: Kenneth Anger

"I started out in the mailroom at Warner Bros. and then worked at the RKO research library. They fired me after they found my book of poems, 'Turn Off the Sunshine.'" - Kenneth Anger

Kenneth Anger is a filmmaker known for his avant-garde style, and his story is proof that starting in the mailroom can lead to great things. Despite being fired from RKO Pictures, Anger continued to pursue his passion for filmmaking and went on to create iconic films like "Scorpio Rising" and "Inauguration of the Pleasure Dome."

Examples of Famous Directors Who Started in the Mailroom

  • Francis Ford Coppola
  • James Cameron
  • David Lynch
  • Spike Lee
  • Ron Howard

These directors, along with many others, started from humble beginnings in the mailroom and worked their way up to becoming global household names in the film industry.

Conclusion: Steps to Success

To achieve success in the film industry, here are three steps you can take:

  1. Get your foot in the door, even if it means starting in the mailroom.
  2. Stay committed to your craft and continue to develop your skills.
  3. Network and build relationships with people in the industry.
