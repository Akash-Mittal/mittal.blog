The Secret of Success: Never, Never Give Up



An Inspiring Story of Wilma Mankiller

Wilma Mankiller was the first female Principal Chief of the Cherokee Nation and a staunch advocate for Indigenous rights. She grew up in Oklahoma, where her family was forced to move after the federal government forcibly removed them from their ancestral lands in the southeastern United States. Despite facing obstacles such as poverty and discrimination, Mankiller never gave up on her dreams.

Mankiller's perseverance and determination are the qualities that characterized her successful leadership of the Cherokee Nation. She once said, "The secret of our success is that we never, never give up." This statement encapsulates the mindset that is essential to achieving success.

Why Giving Up is Not an Option

The road to success is rarely smooth. There are going to be obstacles, setbacks, and failures. But giving up should never be an option. Here's why:

  • Giving up means never achieving your goals.
  • Giving up means accepting defeat.
  • Giving up means settling for less than what you're capable of.
  • Giving up means never knowing what could have been.

Examples of Never Giving Up

There are countless examples of people who have achieved success because they never gave up. Here are just a few:

"I have not failed. I've just found 10,000 ways that won't work." – Thomas Edison

Thomas Edison, inventor of the light bulb, failed numerous times before finally succeeding. But he never gave up. Instead, he viewed every failure as a stepping stone to success.

"I have missed more than 9,000 shots in my career. I have lost almost 300 games. On 26 occasions I have been entrusted to take the game-winning shot... and I missed. I have failed over and over and over again in my life. And that is why I succeed." – Michael Jordan

Michael Jordan, widely regarded as one of the greatest basketball players of all time, faced numerous setbacks and failures throughout his career. But he never gave up. Instead, he used each failure as motivation to work harder and improve his skills.

How to Never Give Up

Here are some practical tips for developing the "never give up" mindset:

  1. Set realistic goals and develop a plan for achieving them.
  2. Surround yourself with supportive people who believe in you.
  3. Get up every time you fall down.
  4. Remember your reasons for pursuing your goals and let those reasons motivate you.
  5. Accept that failure is a part of the journey to success and learn from your mistakes.
  6. Celebrate small successes along the way to keep yourself motivated.

Conclusion

In conclusion, the secret of success is simple: never, never give up. Developing a mindset of perseverance and determination is essential to achieving your goals. Remember Wilma Mankiller's words and let them inspire you to keep going, no matter what obstacles you may face.

  1. Never give up on your dreams.
  2. Remember that failure is not the end, but a learning opportunity.
  3. Believe in yourself and your ability to overcome challenges.
