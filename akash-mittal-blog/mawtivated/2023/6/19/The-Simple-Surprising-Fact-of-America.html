The Simple Surprising Fact of America



An Inspiring Story

It was a bright sunny day, and I was in the park enjoying the fresh air when something caught my attention. There was a young boy, no more than eight years old, playing with his toy airplane. As he ran around with his toy, he suddenly stopped and looked up at the sky. I followed his gaze and saw, for the first time, an airplane flying above us. The boy was captivated, and so was I. In that moment, I realized the simple surprising fact of America: we have been part of some of the world's greatest achievements and have helped prepare mankind for all its later surprises.

Author - Daniel J. Boorstin Story or Background

Daniel J. Boorstin was an American historian, professor, and writer. He served as the 12th Librarian of the United States Congress from 1975 to 1987, and was lauded for his efforts to increase public access to the Library's holdings. Boorstin authored numerous books, including "The Americans: The National Experience," which explored the idea of American exceptionalism and the country's unique role in shaping the world.
