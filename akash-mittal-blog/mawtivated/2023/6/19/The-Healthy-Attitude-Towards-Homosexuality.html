The Healthy Attitude Towards Homosexuality



When I was a child, I had a classmate who always stood out to me. He had two dads, something I had never encountered before. But the way his family carried themselves, with so much love and respect, made me realize that a family is a family, no matter what.

That lesson stayed with me, but unfortunately, not everyone has had the opportunity to learn it. Many of us grew up in societies where homosexuality is still not accepted, or even worse, is demonized and punished. But as fashion legend Karl Lagerfeld's mother said many years ago, it's nothing. It's like hair color. It's not a subject.

Who is Karl Lagerfeld?

Before we dive into the importance of a healthy attitude towards homosexuality, let's take a brief look at the person who inspired this article. Karl Lagerfeld was a German fashion designer, who passed away in 2019 at the age of 85. He was known for his work as the creative director of Chanel, Fendi, and his eponymous fashion label.

While Lagerfeld's legacy in the fashion industry cannot be denied, he was also known for his outspoken and often controversial opinions. However, when it came to the subject of homosexuality, Lagerfeld was clear in his support.

"I have known homosexuals who are perfectly well-balanced and feel no anxiety whatsoever. Some of them are geniuses. But nature plays these tricks on us, and it's just as well they do. Without homosexuality, there would be no Picasso."

Lagerfeld understood that homosexuality is not a choice or a behavior, but simply a part of someone's identity. And just like any other part of ourselves, it should be accepted and celebrated.

Why a Healthy Attitude Towards Homosexuality Matters

Now, let's explore why the attitude we have towards people who identify as LGBTQ+ is so important.

It affects mental health

Being rejected or ostracized by the people around us for something we cannot change is one of the most harmful experiences a person can go through. Study after study has shown that LGBTQ+ people are at a higher risk of mental health issues like depression, anxiety, and suicide. When we have a healthy attitude towards homosexuality, we create safe spaces for LGBTQ+ individuals to be themselves and feel accepted, which can be life-saving.

It affects physical health

Prejudice and discrimination doesn't just affect mental health. LGBTQ+ individuals also face significant barriers to accessing healthcare. They may avoid going to doctors or clinics because of past negative experiences, or because they fear being judged or mistreated. This can lead to chronic health issues going untreated, and even premature death if illnesses aren't caught early enough.

It affects society as a whole

Finally, the way we treat LGBTQ+ individuals is a reflection of our society's values. Do we want to be known for promoting love and acceptance, or hatred and fear? When we have a healthy attitude towards homosexuality, we send a message that we believe in equality and fairness for all, regardless of who they love.

Examples of a Healthy Attitude Towards Homosexuality

  • Celebrating Pride Month and attending parades or events
  • Listening to and accepting stories of LGBTQ+ individuals
  • Supporting LGBTQ+ businesses and organizations
  • Being an Ally to the community

Conclusion

In conclusion, a healthy attitude towards homosexuality is not simply a matter of political correctness. It stands to benefit everyone in society, from the individual to the collective. To summarize, here are three main takeaways:

  1. Being accepting of LGBTQ+ individuals is crucial for their mental and physical health
  2. The way we treat minority groups reflects our values as a society
  3. Examples of a healthy attitude include visible support, active listening, and allyship
