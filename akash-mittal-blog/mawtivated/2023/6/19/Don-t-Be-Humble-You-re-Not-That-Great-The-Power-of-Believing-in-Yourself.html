Don't Be Humble, You're Not That Great! - The Power of Believing in Yourself



When we think of humility, we often think of it in a positive light. We're taught to be humble, to downplay our achievements, to not be boastful about our successes. But, what happens when we take it too far? What happens when we become so humble that we lose sight of our own greatness?

An Inspiring Story

Golda Meir, the fourth Prime Minister of Israel, was a woman who knew the power of believing in herself. Born in Kiev, Russia in 1898, she immigrated to the United States as a child and later moved to Palestine where she played a key role in the founding of the state of Israel.

Meir once said, "Trust yourself. Create the kind of self that you will be happy to live with all your life. Make the most of yourself by fanning the tiny, inner sparks of possibility into flames of achievement."

Meir knew the importance of not being too humble. She knew she had something valuable to offer the world and she wasn't afraid to share it.

Personally, I have struggled with feeling like I'm not good enough or that my achievements aren't worth celebrating. It wasn't until I started to believe in myself and my abilities that I was able to truly start succeeding.

I once took on a project at work that I thought was too big for me. I was hesitant to take it on because I didn't want to fail and I didn't want to let my team down. But, I decided to push past my fear and give it my all. The project ended up being a huge success and I was praised for my hard work and dedication. It was then that I realized the power of believing in myself and not being too humble.

The Danger of Being Too Humble

Being too humble can be dangerous. It can hold you back from achieving your full potential and it can make you feel unworthy of success. If you constantly downplay your achievements and abilities, you may start to believe that you're not good enough and you may miss out on opportunities to grow and learn.

On the other hand, if you have too much confidence, you may be seen as arrogant and not approachable. Finding the balance between humility and confidence is key.

Examples of the Power of Believing in Yourself

  • Oprah Winfrey - Oprah grew up in poverty and faced numerous hardships throughout her life. Despite this, she believed in herself and her abilities and became one of the most successful and influential women in the world.
  • Michael Jordan - Jordan was cut from his high school basketball team but didn't let it hold him back. He continued to work hard and eventually became one of the greatest basketball players of all time.

Conclusion

  1. Believe in yourself - You have something valuable to offer the world. Don't be afraid to share it.
  2. Find the balance - Don't be too humble or too confident. Learn to find the balance between humility and confidence.
  3. Celebrate your achievements - Don't downplay your achievements or feel unworthy of success. Celebrate your successes and use them as motivation to continue growing and learning.
