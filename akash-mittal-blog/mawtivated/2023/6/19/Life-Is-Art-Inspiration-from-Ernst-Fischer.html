Life Is Art: Inspiration from Ernst Fischer



How to Live an Artful Life and Make Every Day a Masterpiece

An Inspiring Story

Many years ago, there was a young artist named Ernst Fischer. He was poor, but he had a dream: to create a painting that would capture the essence of his soul and touch the hearts of everyone who beheld it. For years, he painted and painted, but he was never satisfied. Finally, in a moment of desperation, he threw down his brushes and cried out to the universe, "Why can't I create something beautiful?"

As if in answer, a voice spoke to him: "Ernst, you are art. Your life is the canvas on which your masterpiece is painted. Live each day with passion and purpose, and your art will flow from you like a river. Don't worry about imitating life or art; simply be the art you were created to be."

From that day forth, Ernst Fischer lived his life like a work of art. Every moment was filled with creativity, wonder, and beauty. And when he died, he left behind a legacy of inspiration that still touches countless lives to this day.

About the Author

Ernst Fischer is a German-born writer, artist, and philosopher who lived from 1899 to 1972. He is best known for his book The Necessity of Art, which argues that art is not a luxury or a frivolity, but a vital part of human existence that nourishes our souls and brings us closer to the divine. Through his life and his work, Fischer embodied the idea that creativity is not just a talent, but a way of life.

What is Art?

When we think of art, we often picture paintings, sculptures, and other forms of visual art. But according to Fischer, art is much more than that. It is a way of seeing and being in the world. It is a way of expressing our deepest emotions, thoughts, and dreams. And it is a way of connecting with others and transcendence.

How to Live an Artful Life

So how can we live an artful life like Ernest Fischer? Here are a few practical tips:

  • Find Your Passion: What is it that makes your heart sing? What fills you with energy and enthusiasm? Whatever it is, make it a part of your daily life.
  • Create Something Every Day: It doesn't have to be a masterpiece. It can be a sketch, a poem, a song, or even a meal. The important thing is to let your creativity flow.
  • Appreciate the Beauty around You: Take time to notice the colors, shapes, and textures of the world. Marvel at the sunrise and the sunset. Listen to the birds and the wind. The more you appreciate the world around you, the more you will see how extraordinary it is.
  • Connect with Others: Art is not a solo act. It is a way of sharing our humanity with others. Connect with others who share your passion for art, or simply connect with others in your community. The more you connect with others, the more you will see how much you have in common.

Conclusion in Three Points

  1. Art is not just a talent; it is a way of life.
  2. We can all live more artfully by finding our passion, creating something every day, appreciating the beauty around us, and connecting with others.
  3. When we live our lives like a work of art, we create a legacy that will inspire others for generations to come.
