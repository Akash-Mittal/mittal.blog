Creating the Perfect Love: Stop Searching for the Perfect Lover



"We waste time looking for the perfect lover, instead of creating the perfect love." - Tom Robbins

Once upon a time, a man and a woman fell in love. They were young and foolish, thinking that their love would be easy and perfect. But as time went on, they discovered that love is not always easy. They argued and fought and sometimes went to bed angry. But they also laughed and danced and cried tears of joy together. In the end, they realized that love is not about finding the perfect partner, but about creating the perfect love.

"We waste time looking for the perfect lover, instead of creating the perfect love." - Tom Robbins

What Does it Mean to Create the Perfect Love?

Creating the perfect love means taking a proactive approach to your relationship. It means putting in the effort to communicate effectively, to show your partner that you care, and to work through the ups and downs of life together. It means being vulnerable and honest, and accepting each other for who you truly are.

Why Do We Look for the Perfect Lover?

Many of us have been conditioned to believe that there is a perfect partner out there for us. We grow up watching romantic movies and reading fairy tales, which depict love as a magical and effortless experience. We may even compare our own relationships to those portrayed in the media, and feel like we are missing out on something if our relationships are not "perfect."

But the truth is that there is no such thing as a perfect partner or a perfect relationship. Every person has flaws, and relationships take work. It is only when we accept this reality and focus on creating the perfect love that we can truly experience happiness and fulfillment in our relationships.

How Can We Create the Perfect Love?

Creating the perfect love is not easy, but it is possible. Here are some tips to get you started:

  • Communicate openly and honestly with your partner
  • Show your partner that you care through small gestures of affection and appreciation
  • Work through conflicts and challenges together, rather than avoiding them
  • Practice forgiveness and let go of grudges
  • Focus on building a strong foundation of trust and respect in your relationship

Remember that creating the perfect love is a journey, not a destination. It requires ongoing effort and commitment from both partners. But if you are willing to put in the work and prioritize your relationship, you can create a love that is truly perfect for you.

Conclusion

  1. Stop looking for the perfect lover and start focusing on creating the perfect love
  2. Accept that relationships take work and be willing to put in the effort to make yours thrive
  3. Communicate openly, show your partner that you care, and prioritize building a strong foundation of trust and respect
