Striving and Struggle Precede Success



Have you ever heard the story of Sarah Ban Breathnach, the author of Simple Abundance? She had hit rock bottom, living in a small garage with her daughter after going through a divorce and bankruptcy. But instead of giving up, she worked hard and eventually became a bestselling author with her book selling millions of copies worldwide. Sarah's story is a reminder that striving and struggle are necessary on the road to success, even in the dictionary.

Success doesn't come easy, it takes hard work, dedication, and perseverance. There are no shortcuts to success, and the road to achieving your goals can be rocky and full of obstacles. However, it's important to keep going and never give up on your dreams. Every effort counts, and each step you take brings you closer to achieving your objectives.

Why Striving and Struggle are Important

Striving and struggle are important because they help you grow and learn. When you work hard towards your goals, you develop new skills, gain knowledge, and become more confident. Struggle also teaches you resilience and how to bounce back after facing setbacks and failures.

Without striving and struggle, life would be uneventful and predictable, and you wouldn't have any opportunities to challenge yourself, take risks, and push your boundaries. When you overcome obstacles and achieve your goals, you feel a sense of accomplishment and pride that can boost your self-esteem and motivation.

Examples of Striving and Struggle

  • Entrepreneurs who start their own business and work tirelessly to make it successful
  • Athletes who practice and train every day to become the best in their sport
  • Students who study hard and sacrifice their leisure time to get good grades
  • Parents who work hard to provide for their children and give them a better future

Practical Tips for Striving and Struggle

If you want to achieve your goals and succeed in life, here are some practical tips:

  1. Set goals: Define what you want to achieve and create a plan of action to get there. Set realistic and achievable goals that motivate you and keep you focused.
  2. Work hard: Success requires hard work, dedication, and perseverance. Be willing to put in the effort and work tirelessly towards your goals.
  3. Stay positive: Believe in yourself and your abilities, and stay motivated no matter what obstacles you face. Surround yourself with positive people who support and encourage you.
  4. Learn from failures: Don't be afraid to fail, as failure is a natural part of the learning process. Use your failures as opportunities to learn, grow, and improve.

Conclusion

In conclusion, striving and struggle precede success, and are essential ingredients to achieving your goals and aspirations. Remember to set goals, work hard, stay positive, and learn from your failures. Never give up on your dreams, and keep pushing forward no matter how difficult the journey may be. As Sarah Ban Breathnach once said: "When we choose not to focus on what is missing from our lives but are grateful for the abundance that's present, we experience heaven on earth."
