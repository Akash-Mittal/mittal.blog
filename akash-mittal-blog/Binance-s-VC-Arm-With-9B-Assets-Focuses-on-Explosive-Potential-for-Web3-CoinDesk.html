Binance's VC Arm With $9B Assets Focuses on Explosive Potential for Web3

An Exciting Journey to the Future of the Internet

Imagine being able to browse the internet without worrying about privacy and security. Imagine being able to buy and sell goods and services without intermediaries taking a cut. Imagine being able to build and run decentralized applications that can't be shut down by anyone. This is the future of the internet, and it's called Web3.

Web3 is the next evolution of the internet, where users own and control their data, where transactions are faster and cheaper, and where trust is built on cryptography and consensus. It's a paradigm shift from the current web, where users are at the mercy of tech giants and their business models.

Many companies and investors have already realized the potential of Web3, and Binance's venture capital arm is one of them. With $9 billion in assets under management, Binance VC is one of the largest players in the space, and its focus on Web3 is a testament to its vision for the future.

Web3's Potential

Web3 is still at a nascent stage, but there are already some quantifiable examples of its potential. For instance:

  • The global market size of blockchain technology is expected to reach $39.7 billion by 2025, according to a report by Markets and Markets. This includes everything from cryptocurrencies to smart contracts to decentralized finance.
  • Decentralized finance (DeFi) has exploded in popularity in the past year, with the total value locked in DeFi protocols reaching $154 billion in May 2021, according to DeFi Pulse. This represents a 2,000% increase from the previous year.
  • The NFT (non-fungible token) market has also taken off, with the total sales volume of NFTs reaching $2.5 billion in the first half of 2021, according to DappRadar. This represents a 2,100% increase from the same period in 2020.
