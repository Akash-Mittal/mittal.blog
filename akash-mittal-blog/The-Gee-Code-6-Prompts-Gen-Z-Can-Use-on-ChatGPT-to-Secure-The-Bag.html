The Gee Code: 6 Prompts Gen Z Can Use on ChatGPT to Secure The Bag

Meet Sarah, a 19-year-old college student who wants to earn some extra cash during her free time. She has heard of ChatGPT, a popular app that pays users for answering surveys, testing products, and completing small tasks. However, Sarah is not sure how to maximize her earnings on the platform.

Fortunately, Sarah is a Gen Z member who knows how to use technology and social media to her advantage. By applying The Gee Code, a set of strategies and prompts specifically designed for ChatGPT users, Sarah can turn her spare time into a profitable side hustle.

1. Use Referral Links to Invite Friends

Sarah knows that ChatGPT offers a referral program, which gives users a commission for every new member they invite to the platform. However, she also knows that a generic and boring message like "Please join ChatGPT using my code" is unlikely to attract many people.

Instead, Sarah decides to personalize her invitation by using a prompt from The Gee Code: "Hey, [friend's name]! I just discovered this awesome app called ChatGPT that pays you for doing fun and easy tasks. I thought you might be interested since you're always looking for ways to earn money online. If you use my referral link to sign up, we'll both get a bonus. Let me know if you have any questions!"

By using a friendly and engaging tone, mentioning the benefits of the app, and offering to answer questions, Sarah increases the chances of her friends accepting the invitation. Moreover, by using different prompts for different types of friends (such as "I know you're a fashion lover, and ChatGPT has some interesting surveys about fashion trends"), Sarah can tailor her approach according to their interests and preferences.

2. Set Realistic Goals and Track Progress

Sarah realizes that earning money on ChatGPT requires some patience, consistency, and strategy. She also knows that setting unrealistic goals (like earning $1000 in one week) or getting too obsessed with the earnings can lead to frustration and burnout.

Therefore, Sarah follows another tip from The Gee Code: "Set SMART goals (Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant, Time-bound) and track your progress regularly." For example, she sets a goal of earning $50 per month on ChatGPT by completing at least 10 surveys per week. She also sets a reminder on her phone to check her earnings and update her progress every Sunday.

By setting a realistic and specific goal, Sarah avoids feeling overwhelmed or discouraged. By measuring her progress and adjusting her strategy accordingly, she stays motivated and learns which tasks are more profitable or enjoyable for her.

3. Participate in Community Challenges and Contests

Sarah notices that ChatGPT occasionally hosts community challenges and contests, where users can compete with each other to win prizes, recognition, and social media exposure. However, she is not sure how to stand out among hundreds of participants with similar skills and ideas.

Therefore, Sarah turns to The Gee Code for inspiration: "Be creative, original, and collaborative in your entries, and leverage your social media presence to promote them." For example, she decides to participate in a challenge that asks users to create a meme about their favorite product or service. Instead of using a generic meme template, Sarah designs a witty and relatable meme that features her own photo and a personal anecdote. She also shares the meme on her Instagram, Facebook, and Twitter accounts, with a catchy caption that encourages her followers to vote for her on ChatGPT.

By using her creativity and authenticity, and by collaborating with other users who share the same interests, Sarah increases her chances of winning the contest and gaining more exposure on social media. She also creates a portfolio of original and relevant content that can showcase her skills and personality to potential employers or clients.

4. Respond to Surveys and Product Tests Promptly and Honestly

Sarah notices that some surveys and product tests on ChatGPT require timely and honest feedback, as well as attention to detail and correctness. She also knows that some users might be tempted to rush through the tasks or provide fake or inconsistent responses to maximize their earnings.

Therefore, Sarah follows a crucial advice from The Gee Code: "Always respond to surveys and product tests promptly and honestly, and pay attention to the instructions, rules, and quality standards. Even if you don't get paid for a task, you'll still learn something valuable and improve your skills."

By treating each task as a learning opportunity and a chance to demonstrate her professionalism and integrity, Sarah earns a reputation as a reliable and competent user on ChatGPT. This reputation can lead to more exclusive and high-paying tasks, as well as more positive ratings and reviews from clients and moderators.

5. Join Group Chats and Forums for Tips and Support

Sarah notices that ChatGPT has a feature that allows users to join group chats and forums where they can share tips, ask questions, and connect with other users who have similar interests or expertise. She also knows that joining such groups can be a source of motivation and inspiration, as well as a way to learn new strategies and gain feedback.

Therefore, Sarah follows a suggestion from The Gee Code: "Join at least one group chat or forum related to your niche or interest, and participate actively in the discussions. Ask for feedback, share your experiences, and build relationships with other users."

By joining the group chat for "College Students on ChatGPT" and the forum for "Healthy and Eco-Friendly Consumers," Sarah meets new friends who share her values and goals. She also learns new tips and tricks for maximizing her earnings, as well as for managing her time and stress. Moreover, she helps other users by sharing her own insights and feedback on different tasks and challenges.

6. Stay Updated and Take Advantage of Promo Codes and Seasonal Offers

Sarah notices that ChatGPT regularly offers promo codes, seasonal discounts, and exclusive deals to its users, as well as updates and improvements to its features and services. She also knows that ignoring such updates can lead to missing out on some valuable opportunities or rewards.

Therefore, Sarah follows a final tip from The Gee Code: "Stay updated on the latest news and updates from ChatGPT, and take advantage of any promo codes, seasonal offers, or exclusive deals that suit your interests and habits."

By checking the ChatGPT news section and social media accounts regularly, Sarah discovers a promo code that gives her a bonus for completing a survey about eco-friendly products. She also learns that ChatGPT has launched a new feature that allows users to test new products before they are released to the market, which could lead to more exciting and rewarding tasks in the future.
