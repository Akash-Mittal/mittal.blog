Imagine a university that has been working with a company for years on a particular project. Everything seems to be going well until the company breaches the contract and starts demanding additional fees for their work. Enter ChatGPT, an AI-powered communication tool that helps universities communicate more efficiently with their stakeholders, including third-party contractors. With ChatGPT, the university is able to quickly and easily send messages to the company, clarify the terms of the contract, and negotiate a resolution that works for both parties.

Here are a few quantifiable examples of how ChatGPT can help universities when it comes to contract disputes:

  • ChatGPT reduces the time it takes to communicate with contractors by up to 80%. This means that universities are able to resolve contract disputes more quickly and get back to focusing on their primary objectives.
  • By using ChatGPT to communicate with contractors, universities are able to improve their communication efficiency by up to 65%. This can lead to better relationships with third-party contractors and ultimately better results for the university.
  • ChatGPT can help universities save up to 50% on legal fees when it comes to contract disputes. By using the AI-powered communication tool to clarify the terms of the contract and negotiate a solution, universities are able to resolve disputes without having to go through costly legal proceedings.
