The Power of Public Speaking: How to Stand Out at TC Disrupt

It was a hot summer day, and I was set to deliver my first ever talk at a tech conference. At that time, I was just a young entrepreneur trying to make it in the industry. And honestly, I was nervous as hell.

As I stood in front of the crowd, I could feel the sweat trickling down my back. My palms were sweaty, and I could barely remember the words I had rehearsed countless times. But as soon as I started speaking, something miraculous happened.

The people in the audience perked up. They were listening to every word I said, nodding their heads in agreement, and even chuckling at my jokes. That is when I realised that public speaking is a powerful tool that can make or break your career.

Example of Memorable Speakers

If you take a look at some of the most successful people in the startup world, you will notice that they all have one thing in common—they are great public speakers. Here are some examples:

  • Elon Musk: During Tesla's launch event, Elon Musk mesmerised the audience with his storytelling skills and passion for clean energy.
  • Sheryl Sandberg: In her TEDtalk, Sheryl Sandberg talked about the importance of women in leadership roles and motivated her audience with her inspiring story.
  • Steve Jobs: Steve Jobs was known for his captivating product launches that were more like performances than presentations.

How to Stand Out at TC Disrupt

If you want to make a mark at TC Disrupt, you need to be a memorable speaker. Here are some tips:

  1. Start with a catchy opening line: Begin your talk with a bold statement, a story or a question that piques the interest of your audience.
  2. Use visuals: People are more likely to remember what they see than what they hear. So, use visuals such as pictures, videos or infographics to supplement your speech.
  3. Speak with conviction: The audience is more likely to believe in what you say if you are passionate about it. So, practice your speech until you feel confident enough to deliver it with conviction.

Conclusion

By incorporating these tips into your speech, you can become a memorable speaker at TC Disrupt. Remember to start with a catchy opening line, use visuals to supplement your speech, and speak with conviction. By doing so, you will leave a lasting impression on your audience and gain recognition in your industry.
