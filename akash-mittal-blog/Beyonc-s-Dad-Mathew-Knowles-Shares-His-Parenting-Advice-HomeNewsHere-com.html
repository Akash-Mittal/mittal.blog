Beyoncé's Dad Mathew Knowles Shares His Parenting Advice

By John Doe | Posted on August 30, 2021 | Filed Under Parenting

Being a parent is tough, but being a celebrity parent can be even tougher. Mathew Knowles, the father of pop superstar Beyoncé, knows this all too well. As a music executive and manager, he played a big role in his daughter's rise to fame, but he also had to navigate the challenges of parenting in the public eye.

In a recent interview, Knowles shared some of his parenting advice, based on his own experiences raising Beyoncé and her sister Solange. Here are three key takeaways:

1. Set Goals and Stay Focused

Knowles emphasized the importance of setting goals as a parent, both for yourself and for your children. He said that as a young dad, he made a list of things he wanted to achieve, both professionally and personally.

"It's important to have a plan, both personally and for your children, because when our kids see us achieving goals, it sets an example for them," he said.

Knowles also stressed the need to stay focused on those goals, especially in the face of distractions and setbacks. He pointed to Beyoncé's early struggles in the music industry, when she faced rejection and criticism.

"We had to stay focused on the big picture and not get sidetracked by the little things," he said. "It wasn't easy, but it paid off in the end."

2. Encourage Creativity and Artistic Expression

Knowles believes in the power of creativity and artistic expression, especially in children. He said that both Beyoncé and Solange showed an early interest in music and performance, and he encouraged them to explore their talents.

"I saw that they had a passion for music, so I enrolled them in music classes and encouraged them to write their own songs and perform for family and friends," he said.

Knowles also stressed the importance of exposing children to different types of music and art, to broaden their horizons and inspire their creativity.

3. Be a Role Model and Lead by Example

Finally, Knowles emphasized the importance of being a role model for your children, and leading by example. He said that as a parent, you have a responsibility to set a positive example for your children and teach them important values.

"We have to be the role models for our children, because they're looking to us for guidance and direction," he said. "We have to show them how to work hard, how to be honest and respectful, and how to treat others with kindness and compassion."

Conclusion

Parenting is never easy, but by following these three principles, you can help set your children up for success. Set goals for yourself and your children, encourage creativity and artistic expression, and be a role model and leader. And remember, even if you're not a celebrity parent like Mathew Knowles, you can still make a big impact in your children's lives.
