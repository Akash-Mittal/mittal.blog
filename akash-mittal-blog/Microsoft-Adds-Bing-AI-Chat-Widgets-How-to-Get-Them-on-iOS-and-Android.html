Microsoft Adds Bing AI Chat Widgets: How to Get Them on iOS and Android

Have you ever been in the middle of something on your smartphone and wished for the ability to multitask? Microsoft has answered that request with its new Bing AI chat widgets that allow you to perform other tasks while still keeping your conversation active. Now you can use Bing to search the web and figure out the answer to a question in the middle of a conversation, all without having to navigate away from the chat screen. The integration of Bing with the chat feature is seamless and provides the ability to stay informed without any extra hassle.

How it Works

The Bing AI chat widget is available for iOS and Android users in the US, UK, Canada, Australia, India, and New Zealand. To get started, you simply need to open up your chat app (which supports the Bing AI chat widget) and start a conversation. Once you are chatting, you can tap on the Bing icon to start a search or a question. You can then select one of the suggested results or continue browsing and search until you find the information you need. Once you have found the answer that you were looking for, you can then share it with your chat partner by tapping on the share icon.

The Bing AI chat widget incorporates not just web search, but also image search and video search, which gives users a wide range of options for finding the information they need. And with the chat window still active, you can quickly switch back to the conversation and share your findings with your chat partner. Additionally, Microsoft's chat widgets are designed to work seamlessly on the lock screen, which allows users to access the chat feature without having to unlock their device or navigate away from their current task.

Quantifiable Examples

Microsoft's Bing AI chat widgets have made it easier for users to multitask and stay informed while still being able to maintain an active conversation. Here are some quantifiable examples of how this feature has helped users:

  • Users have been able to search for information without having to navigate away from the chat screen, which has saved them time and hassle.
  • Users have been able to conduct research and share their findings in real-time with their chat partner, which has made conversations more engaging and informative.
  • Users have been able to stay up-to-date with the latest news and trends by using the Bing AI chat widget to search for articles and videos without having to leave the chat screen.

Conclusion

In conclusion, Microsoft's Bing AI chat widgets have provided users with a new and innovative way to stay informed and multitask while engaging in a chat conversation. With the ability to seamlessly integrate web, image, and video search with the chat feature, users can now access a wealth of information without any extra hassle. So if you are an iOS or Android user in the US, UK, Canada, Australia, India, or New Zealand, be sure to give the Bing AI chat widget a try and see how it can enhance your chat experience.
