Apple Hiring for AI-Powered Products

Apple Inc., a multinational technology company, is hiring experts to work on its own AI-powered products. Apple has been exploring the field of artificial intelligence for years, and now they are taking a major step in the direction of developing AI-powered products.

According to a recent report by International Data Corporation (IDC), the artificial intelligence market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 23.1% from 2017 to 2022. The report also states that AI-related hardware, software, and services revenues will increase from $8.0 billion in 2016 to $47.0 billion by 2020.

Apple is aware of the growth potential of AI and has hired a team of experts to develop AI-powered products. Apple is looking for engineers, data scientists, and machine learning experts who can work on projects involving Siri, HomePod, and other Apple products. They are also planning to create new products that can compete with Amazon's Alexa and Google's Assistant.

Apple's recent acquisition of Silk Labs, which specializes in advanced visual and audio recognition technology for smart home devices, shows Apple's commitment to AI and their plan to develop new products in this field. They have also released a machine learning framework called Core ML for app developers to integrate AI into their apps.

Additionally, Apple's Siri has improved significantly since its launch in 2011 and now boasts a greater than 90% accuracy rate. Apple is also investing in machine learning research and has hired well-known AI researchers and developers such as Russ Salakhutdinov and John Giannandrea.
