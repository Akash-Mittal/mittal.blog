Japan AI Startup Launches Robot for Home Use

Have you ever wished for a robot to do your house chores for you? Well, a Japan AI startup has just released a robot that can do just that!

The startup, called RoboHouse, has spent the last two years developing a robot that can clean floors, do laundry, and even make meals. The robot, called RoboHome, is set to revolutionize the way we do housework.

According to RoboHouse, the RoboHome can clean a room in half the time it would take a human to do so. It can also do laundry for a family of four in just one hour, and cook a meal in under 20 minutes.

Based on these examples, it's clear to see how the RoboHome can save people a lot of time and effort when it comes to household chores.
