Nick Cannon's Most Time-Spent Kid

Find out which of Nick Cannon's kids gets the most of his time.

Nick Cannon, the American rapper and TV personality, recently revealed on the "The Ellen DeGeneres Show" which of his kids he spends the most time with. But before we get to that, let's take a look at Cannon's family.

Cannon is the father of seven children with four different mothers. He shares three kids with his ex-wife Mariah Carey: Moroccan and Monroe (fraternal twins) and Golden. The 40-year-old TV star also has a son, Sagon, with Brittany Bell and a daughter, Powerful Queen, with Abby De La Rosa. He became a father again in June 2021 when his ex-girlfriend, model Alyssa Scott, gave birth to their son, Zen.

So, which of these kids does Cannon spend the most time with? It's none other than...

The Quantifiable Examples

Drumroll, please! The kid Nick Cannon spends the most time with is his son Golden, who is 10 years old. Cannon said on "The Ellen DeGeneres Show" that he and Golden have a special bond, and that the boy loves music and performing, just like his father.

"My youngest son, Golden, he's always with me," Cannon said. "He's always in the studio with me, always trying to make beats and make music."

Cannon also shared that he loves spending time with his other six kids, but that they are spread out across the country.

Golden has also been spotted on The Masked Singer set, where he his father hosts. The young boy made a special appearance on the show in the season 5 finale.

It's not just about spending time with his kids, though. Cannon is also a great dad when it comes to co-parenting. He often shares pictures of his kids on social media, and even co-parents with his ex-wife Mariah Carey. The two have been spotted spending time together with their kids, and even celebrated Halloween together in 2020.
