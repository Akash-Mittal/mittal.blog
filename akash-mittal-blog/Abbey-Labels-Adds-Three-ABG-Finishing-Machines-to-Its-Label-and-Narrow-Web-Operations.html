Abbey Labels Adds Three ABG Finishing Machines to Its Label and Narrow Web Operations

Starting a business is not easy. The competition is high, and the challenges are endless. However, if you are passionate about what you do, put in the work, and make smart investments, success can be achieved. This is the story of Abbey Labels, a UK-based label and narrow web printing company that has recently invested in three ABG finishing machines.

Abbey Labels is known for its exceptional printing quality and excellent customer service. Founded in 1982, the company has built a reputation for delivering consistent results and meeting tight deadlines. However, as the demand for labels and flexible packaging products continues to grow, the company looked to upgrade its finishing capabilities to stay competitive.

The ABG machines that Abbey Labels invested in are fully automated and can handle a wide range of finishing processes, including die-cutting, slitting, and rewinding. This investment has enabled the company to increase its production capacity while maintaining its reputation for quality and reliability.

The investment in ABG finishing machines has enabled Abbey Labels to:

  • Reduce lead times by up to 50%
  • Increase production capacity by 30%
  • Deliver products with higher accuracy and consistency
  • Reduce waste and increase efficiency

The Benefits of Investing in ABG Finishing Machines

The benefits of investing in ABG finishing machines go beyond productivity and efficiency. Here are few:

Easier and Faster Setup

The ABG machines have automated setup features that reduce the time it takes to prepare for a job. This feature eliminates the need for manual adjustments and makes it easier to switch between different jobs.

Improved Quality Control

The ABG machines are equipped with advanced sensors and cameras that monitor the production process and ensure consistent quality across all products. This technology eliminates the risk of human error and helps Abbey Labels deliver products with higher accuracy and consistency.

Lower Cost of Production

The ABG machines have reduced the cost of labor, as fewer operators are needed to oversee the production process. Additionally, the machines have reduced material waste, as they are designed to use the minimum amount of material required for each job.

Conclusion

The investment in ABG finishing machines has allowed Abbey Labels to increase its production capacity, improve quality control, and reduce costs. In the highly competitive label and narrow web printing industry, investing in advanced technology is crucial for staying ahead of the game.

  1. Investing in automation technology can increase production capacity and efficiency.
  2. Automated finishing machines can improve quality control and reduce waste.
  3. For companies seeking to stay competitive, investing in advanced technology is crucial.
