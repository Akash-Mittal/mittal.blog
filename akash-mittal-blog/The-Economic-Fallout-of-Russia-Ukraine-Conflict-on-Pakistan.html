The Economic Fallout of Russia-Ukraine Conflict on Pakistan

Once upon a time, there was a small but growing Pakistani business that had just secured a customer in Ukraine. Everything was going smoothly until the conflict between Russia and Ukraine erupted and the Pakistani business suddenly found itself at risk of losing the customer due to the economic fallout of the conflict. This story is just one small example of the impact that the conflict in Eastern Europe has had on Pakistan.

The conflict between Russia and Ukraine has had a number of quantifiable impacts on Pakistan, including:

  • Decreased exports - Pakistan's exports to Ukraine have decreased by 66% since 2013, largely due to the economic impact of the conflict.
  • Decreased remittances - Around 60% of Pakistanis working in Ukraine have returned home due to the conflict, resulting in a significant decrease in remittances from that country.
  • Increased energy costs - Pakistan has had to look to other countries for energy imports, which has led to increased costs for businesses and consumers.

The Economic Fallout of the Russia-Ukraine Conflict: How Pakistan is Feeling the Effects

  1. The conflict between Russia and Ukraine has had a significant impact on Pakistan's economy, particularly in terms of decreased exports and remittances from Ukrainian workers.
