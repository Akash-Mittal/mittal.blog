The Top 10 List

Without further ado, here are the 10 best video games of all time, according to ChatGPT:

  1. "Tetris" - This classic arcade game has been around since 1984 and has become a cultural phenomenon. It's a puzzle game that challenges players to clear lines by fitting different-shaped blocks into a grid.
  2. "Super Mario Bros." - This game needs no introduction. It was released in 1985 and is considered one of the most important games in video game history. The game follows Mario as he tries to save Princess Toadstool from the evil Bowser.
  3. "The Legend of Zelda: Ocarina of Time" - This game was released in 1998 and is still considered one of the best games ever made. It's an action-adventure game that follows Link as he tries to stop Ganondorf from obtaining the Triforce.
  4. "Minecraft" - Released in 2011, this game has become a cultural phenomenon. It's an open-world game that allows players to create and build their own worlds using blocks.
  5. "Grand Theft Auto V" - This game was released in 2013 and is still popular. It's an open-world game that allows players to explore a fictional city while completing missions and causing mayhem.
  6. "World of Warcraft" - Released in 2004, this game is a massively multiplayer online role-playing game (MMORPG) that is still popular today. The game takes place in the world of Azeroth and allows players to choose from different races and classes.
  7. "Metal Gear Solid 2: Sons of Liberty" - This game was released in 2001 and is still considered one of the best games ever made. It's a stealth-action game that follows Solid Snake as he tries to stop the terrorist group Sons of Liberty.
  8. "Half-Life 2" - This game was released in 2004 and is still considered one of the best games ever made. It's a first-person shooter game that follows physicist Gordon Freeman as he tries to fight against an alien invasion.
  9. "Final Fantasy VII" - This game was released in 1997 and is still considered one of the best games ever made. It's a role-playing game that follows Cloud Strife as he tries to stop the evil Sephiroth from destroying the planet.
  10. "Portal 2" - This game was released in 2011 and is still popular. It's a puzzle-platform game that follows a robot named Wheatley as he helps the player character Chell escape from the Aperture Science facility.
