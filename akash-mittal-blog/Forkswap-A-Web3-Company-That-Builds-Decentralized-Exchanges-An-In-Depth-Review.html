What Makes Forkswap Unique?

Forkswap is unique because of its decentralized nature. With centralized exchanges, customers have to trust a third party with their funds. This means that customers' funds and private data can be hacked or stolen. Furthermore, centralized exchanges have a single point of failure. If the system is hacked or goes down, the entire exchange will be unavailable for trading.

Forkswap uses a decentralized exchange (DEX) model that eliminates the need for centralization. The DEX model allows customers to trade peer-to-peer without an intermediary. Transactions are performed on-chain, and customers' funds remain in their wallets. As such, customers have complete control over their funds and data.
