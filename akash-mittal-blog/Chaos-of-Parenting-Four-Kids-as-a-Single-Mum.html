Chaos of Parenting Four Kids as a Single Mum

Kim Kardashian opens up about the challenges and struggles of raising four kids as a single mother.

The Start of it All

It all started in 2013 when Kim Kardashian and Kanye West welcomed their first child, North. Since then, they have expanded their family with the additions of Saint, Chicago, and Psalm. But what happens when you have four kids and your partner is not around to help?

The Reality of Being a Single Mother

According to Kim, "It's a lot, but when you have four kids, you just have to be really organized. You have to be super planned out and strategic." Being a single mother means she has to handle everything on her own from school drop-offs and pick-ups to meal planning and bedtime routines. It can be overwhelming, but Kim has learned to manage it with a few key strategies:

  • Having a schedule - Kim plans out every hour of the day to make sure nothing slips through the cracks.
  • A support system - Kim relies on her family and assistants to help her with the day-to-day tasks.
  • Taking breaks - Kim understands the importance of self-care and makes sure to schedule time for herself.

The Chaos of Parenting Four Kids

With four kids comes a lot of chaos. Kim admits that she often feels like she's "running a daycare." But despite the challenges, she says that being a mother is "the most rewarding job." Here are some quantifiable examples of the chaos that comes with parenting four kids:

  • Laundry - With four kids, the amount of laundry can be overwhelming. Kim says that she does at least one load of laundry every day.
  • Noise - Four kids means a lot of noise. Kim says that her house is always loud, with kids running around and playing.
  • Mess - Kids are messy, and with four of them, there's always something to clean up. Kim says that she's constantly picking up toys and cleaning up spills.

Practical Tips for Managing Chaos

Kim has learned a lot about managing chaos over the years. Here are some practical tips that she recommends:

  • Keep a to-do list - Writing down tasks can help you feel more organized and in control.
  • Delegate tasks - Don't be afraid to ask for help from family members or hire a babysitter.
  • Take time for yourself - Self-care is important for mental and emotional well-being.
  • Stay positive - Reframing negative situations can help you stay positive and focused.

In Conclusion

Parenting four kids as a single mother is not easy, but as Kim Kardashian has shown, it can be done with the right strategies in place. By being organized, having a support system, and taking breaks when needed, she has been able to manage the chaos that comes with parenting four kids. While it may be challenging at times, the rewards are worth it. Being a mother is one of the most rewarding jobs out there.
