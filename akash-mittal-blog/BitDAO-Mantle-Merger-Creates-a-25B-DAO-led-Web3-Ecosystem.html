From the CryptoSlate Newsroom: BitDAO Mantle Merger Creates a $25B DAO-led Web3 Ecosystem

On September 20, 2021, BitDAO and Mantle announced a merger that resulted in a $25 billion decentralized autonomous organization (DAO)-led web3 ecosystem. The merger aims to combine BitDAO's liquidity and Mantle's technology to create a highly efficient, user-friendly decentralized exchange (DEX). The BitDAO Mantle merger is indicative of the growing interest in decentralized finance (DeFi) and Web3 ecosystems.

An Interesting Story of DAOs and Web3 Ecosystems

The story of DAOs and Web3 ecosystems is one of the most exciting and disruptive stories in modern finance. DAOs are decentralized organizations that are governed by community members, where decisions are made by consensus rather than by centralized authorities. These organizations are powered by smart contract protocols that minimize the need for intermediaries and ensure transparency and fairness.

Web3 ecosystems, on the other hand, are decentralized networks that allow users to interact with blockchain technology in a more user-friendly way. Web3 ecosystems provide a more seamless and efficient user experience, and they enable users to participate in various decentralized applications (dApps) and services.

The BitDAO Mantle merger is a significant milestone in the development of DAOs and Web3 ecosystems. The merger brings together two of the most significant players in the crypto space to create a powerful, user-friendly decentralized exchange.
