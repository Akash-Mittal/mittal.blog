Practical Tips

  • Spend quality time with your children every day, even if it's just for a few minutes.
  • Praise your children for their efforts and achievements, not just their results.
  • Listen to your children's concerns and feelings without judgment or criticism.
  • Set clear boundaries and consequences for behaviour, and be consistent in enforcing them.
  • Take care of your own well-being and mental health, and seek support when needed.
