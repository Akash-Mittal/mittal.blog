The Impact on Kim Kardashian's Philanthropic Work

Kim Kardashian has always been known for her humanitarian efforts. From donating money to various charities to using her platform to raise awareness about important issues, she's been a powerful force for good.

This has led to a significant drop in donations for some organizations. The Armenian Educational Foundation, which Kim has been a vocal supporter of in the past, recently reported a 50% decrease in donations since the scandals broke.

  • Example: Before the scandals, Kim would use her platform to raise awareness about issues like gun violence and police brutality. Since the scandals, she hasn't posted anything about these topics.
