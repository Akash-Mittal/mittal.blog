What is ChatGPT?

ChatGPT is an AI-powered chatbot created by OpenAI, a research organization co-founded by Elon Musk in 2015. The app uses the latest advances in natural language processing (NLP) to generate responses to user queries, allowing for more human-like conversations than ever before.

Unlike traditional chatbots, which often rely on predetermined scripts or keyword-based responses, ChatGPT uses a technique called "generative pre-training transformer" to learn from vast amounts of written text and generate its own responses. This means that the app can understand and respond to a wide range of questions and topics, making it an incredibly versatile and powerful tool for users.
