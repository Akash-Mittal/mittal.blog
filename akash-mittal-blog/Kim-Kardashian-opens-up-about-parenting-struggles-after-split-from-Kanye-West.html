Kim Kardashian reveals parenting struggles after split from Kanye West

Kim Kardashian has always been a hands-on mom to her four children, North, Saint, Chicago, and Psalm. But, the reality star has recently opened up about the challenges she's faced as a single mother after her split from rapper, Kanye West.

"Co-parenting is hard," said Kardashian in an interview with Vogue. "It's all about finding the right balance and communication. It's been a learning experience for me, but I'm taking it day by day."

The 40-year-old celebrity admitted that while she's been embracing the single-mom gig, there are times when she feels overwhelmed and exhausted. Kardashian revealed that West's busy schedule and erratic behavior often made it difficult for her to manage the household and parenting duties.

"Kanye is a great father, and we've always been a team when it comes to raising our children. But, with our split, it's been a little challenging," she said.

Despite the struggles, Kardashian is determined to make it work and create a co-parenting plan that works best for her kids.

Parenting Struggles

Kim Kardashian's parenting struggles are not unique to her situation. Many single parents, especially those going through a separation or divorce, face similar challenges. Here are some quantifiable examples of the struggles that single parents face:

  • According to the U.S. Census Bureau, 23% of custodial parents are fathers, and 77% are mothers.
  • Single-parent households are more likely to experience poverty, with 42% of single-mother households living in poverty compared to 21% of married-couple households.
  • Single parents often struggle with finding affordable childcare. According to Child Care Aware of America, the average cost of full-time care for an infant in a childcare center is $9,589 per year.
  • Single parents often feel overwhelmed and burnt out from trying to balance work and parenting responsibilities. A study by Pew Research Center found that 56% of working single parents find it difficult to balance work and family responsibilities.

Kim Kardashian's Tips for Co-Parenting

Despite the hurdles, Kim Kardashian has some tips for other co-parents who may be struggling. Here are some tips Kim has shared:

  1. Stay focused on the kids: Kardashian has emphasized the importance of putting the children's needs first and focusing on their well-being. Communication is key to make sure both parents are on the same page when it comes to parenting decisions.
  2. Be flexible and patient: Co-parenting requires flexibility and patience. Kardashian recommends being open-minded and willing to compromise in order to create a plan that works best for the children.
  3. Take care of yourself: Kardashian has stressed the importance of self-care for single parents. It's essential to prioritize your own physical and mental health in order to be the best parent possible.

Conclusion

Kim Kardashian's candid interview about her parenting struggles highlights the reality that many single parents face. Co-parenting is a difficult journey that requires patience, flexibility, and communication. By putting the children's needs first, being patient and flexible, and prioritizing self-care, co-parents can create a plan that works best for everyone involved.
