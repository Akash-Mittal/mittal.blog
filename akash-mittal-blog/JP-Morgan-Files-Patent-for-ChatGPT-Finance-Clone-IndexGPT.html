JP Morgan Files Patent for ChatGPT Finance Clone IndexGPT

JP Morgan is taking the world by storm with its new technology, ChatGPT. The company has now filed a patent for its finance-focused chatbot, IndexGPT. IndexGPT is an AI chatbot that helps investors to track their investments. The bot is designed to provide real-time updates on a user's portfolio and also to answer any finance-related questions.
