What is Copilot?

Copilot is an AI-powered chatbot that's built into Windows 11. It provides users with personalized assistance, support, and recommendations in real-time. Copilot acts like a virtual assistant that's always ready to help users navigate the operating system and solve any issues that may arise.

What sets Copilot apart from other virtual assistants is its ability to learn from every interaction. Over time, Copilot will adapt to the user's preferences, habits, and needs, providing an even more customized experience. Copilot will become more than just a chatbot, but rather a trusted companion that makes using Windows 11 more enjoyable and efficient.
