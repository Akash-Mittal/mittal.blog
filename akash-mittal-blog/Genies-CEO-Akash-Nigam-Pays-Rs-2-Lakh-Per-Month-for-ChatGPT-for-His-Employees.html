Most of us are familiar with the concept of genie, the magical creature that fulfils wishes. But have you heard of Genies, the digital company that creates personalized avatars for users to use online?

But Nigam isn't content with just that. He knows that his team of employees is what makes his company successful, and he wants to ensure that they have every tool at their disposal to continue making Genies a leader in the industry.

That's where ChatGPT comes in.

ChatGPT is an AI-powered chatbot that uses advanced natural language processing algorithms to provide real-time support to employees. With ChatGPT, the Genies team can get answers to their questions and find the resources they need in an instant.
