Founded in 2017, the oddity data platform is an AI-powered beauty technology that uses advanced data analysis to provide a personalized beauty experience. Their proprietary technology analyzes a user's skin and creates a customized skincare routine based on their unique needs and goals.

But how exactly does it work? The oddity data platform uses machine learning algorithms to analyze data from a user's skin.
