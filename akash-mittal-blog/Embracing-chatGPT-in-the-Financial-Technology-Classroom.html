Practical Tips

If you're a financial technology student or instructor, here are a few practical tips for incorporating chatGPT into your studies:

  • Start small: Don't overwhelm yourself by trying to use chatGPT for every aspect of your coursework. Start with one or two areas where you think it will be most useful.
  • Be specific: ChatGPT works best when you give it specific questions to answer. Don't just ask broad questions like "What is financial technology?" Instead, ask more targeted questions like "What are the major challenges facing financial technology today?"
  • Collaborate: If you're having trouble with a question, ask your classmates or instructor for help. You can also ask chatGPT to recommend resources or connect you with other students who are studying the same topics.
