Nick Cannon Talks About Mariah Carey's Opinion On His 12 Kids

Nick Cannon, the popular American comedian, and television host recently opened up about his family life and shared Mariah Carey's opinion on his 12 kids with her. Cannon was married to Carey, the famous singer, and songwriter for 6 years and has two children together.

Cannon, who is now the father of 12 children from different relationships, revealed that Carey joked about his large family size but was supportive nonetheless.

"She always says, 'I don't know how you do it. You're like a super dad.' She's funny about it. She's always supportive," Cannon said in an interview with Marc Lamont Hill on Black News Channel.

Cannon's family consists of children from three different mothers, including Brittany Bell, a former Miss Arizona USA whom he shares two kids with, Abby De La Rosa, with whom he recently welcomed twins, and other women who haven't been identified.

Despite having a large family, Cannon said that he always puts his children first and spends quality time with each of them.

Quantifiable Examples

Cannon's dedication to his kids is evident from his social media accounts, where he shares pictures and videos of his children regularly. He also hosts "Wild 'N Out," a comedy show that he produces and stars in, which provides jobs for many young comedians.

Cannon's commitment to fatherhood has also led him to start the "Ncredible" foundation, a non-profit organization that focuses on empowering youth through education, entrepreneurship, and entertainment. The foundation has provided scholarships, mentorship, and other resources to underprivileged children and families.

Personal Anecdotes

Cannon's experience with raising a large family has taught him the importance of setting boundaries and balancing his personal and professional life. He has previously spoken about how he manages to prioritize his children while also pursuing his career.

"It's just about time management, man. It's about being present where you're at. When I'm at work, I'm at work. When I'm with my kids, I'm with my kids. When I have me time, I have me time," Cannon said in an interview with DJ Vlad.

Cannon also revealed that his children inspire him to be a better person and help him stay grounded. He said that he wants to be a positive role model for his kids and teach them valuable life skills.

Conclusion

In conclusion, Nick Cannon's journey as a father of 12 kids is an inspiring story of dedication, hard work, and commitment to family. Despite having a busy schedule and a large family, Cannon always makes time for his children and strives to provide them with the best possible upbringing.

His work with the "Ncredible" foundation and "Wild 'N Out" also showcases his commitment to empowering young people and promoting positive change in the community.

If there's one thing that we can learn from Nick Cannon's story, it's that family always comes first, and with the right balance of work and personal life, one can achieve their goals and be a great parent at the same time.
