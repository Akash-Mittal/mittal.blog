The Rise of Web3 Development: A Crypto Funding Focus on the $151M Week

Imagine you are in a city made up of thousands of buildings, each connected to one another by narrow, congested streets. You are tasked with delivering a package to a building on the other side of the city. Do you take the most straightforward route, following the tangled web of streets, or do you build a tunnel, connecting directly to your destination? This analogy is similar to the current state of the internet.

The internet we know today is built on a foundation of Web2â€” a system of interconnected websites and applications that rely on complex networks to fulfil user requests. But, as we continue to build atop this foundation, the internet has become increasingly cluttered, and many believe it's time for a new approach. This is where Web3 comes in.

Web3 is a new protocol that aims to improve upon the limitations of Web2 by leveraging blockchain technology and decentralization principles to create a more fluid and interconnected web. In recent months, the development of Web3 has surged, and investors have taken notice. The week of September 27th saw over $151M in funding raised for Web3 focused startups, marking a significant milestone in the industry's growth.

The surge in Web3 funding is due, in part, to the growing interest in Decentralized Finance (DeFi) and Non-Fungible Tokens (NFTs). These sectors have been primary drivers of Web3 adoption and have attracted significant investment in recent months. Here are some quantifiable examples:

  • Osiris raised $20M in a funding round led by Peter Thiel and others to develop decentralized AI infrastructure.
  • Polygon (MATIC) raised $152M in a funding round led by billionaire Mark Cuban to continue their expansion in scaling solutions for DeFi and NFTs on Ethereum.
  • Alchemy raised $250M Series C funding, making it the second-most successful blockchain company after Coinbase.

These are just a few examples of the booming Web3 industry. With more projects emerging, and blockchain technology becoming more advanced, we can only expect the trend of Web3 funding to continue.

The Eye-catching Tittle: Web3 is Here, and It's Changing Everything

As we continue to move towards a more decentralized and interconnected world, Web3 is poised to become the foundation on which the next generation of the internet will be built. It has the potential to change everything, from how we interact with one another to how we conduct business and exchange value.

The surge in Web3 funding is a testament to the significant role blockchain technology will continue to play in shaping our future. While the technology is still in its early stages, the potential implications of Web3 are vast and groundbreaking.

The rise of Web3 is significant, and we can expect three key things as the industry continues to evolve:

  1. The creation of a more decentralized and interconnected web;
  2. The continued growth of DeFi and NFTs will further drive Web3 adoption;
  3. The potential for widespread innovation and disruption across industries.
