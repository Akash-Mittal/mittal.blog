The Story

It all started with a conversation between the executives of Cronos and the City of Seoul. They were discussing ways to promote the city's technology sector and encourage innovation. The topic eventually came around to blockchain and Web 3.0, and the idea for the Seoul Web 3.0 Festival 2023 was born.

The festival will be a gathering of industry leaders, entrepreneurs, and technologists who are working in the field of Web 3.0. It will be a chance for people to learn about the latest developments and to collaborate on new projects.

"This is an exciting opportunity for us to showcase the innovation that is happening in Seoul and to connect with people from all over the world who are passionate about Web 3.0," said the Mayor of Seoul.

The Seoul Web 3.0 Festival 2023 is expected to draw more than 10,000 attendees from around the world. It will feature more than 100 speakers and 50 exhibitors, showcasing the latest in blockchain technology, smart contracts, and decentralized applications. The festival will also offer opportunities for networking, collaboration, and investment.

Several renowned companies in the blockchain industry have already expressed interest in participating in the event, including Ethereum, Ripple, and Stellar.
