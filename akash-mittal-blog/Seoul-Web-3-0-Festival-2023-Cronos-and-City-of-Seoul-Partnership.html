Seoul Web 3.0 Festival 2023: Cronos and City of Seoul Partnership

It was a normal day for the Cronos team when they received a call from the City of Seoul. The officials were interested in partnering with Cronos to launch a grand event that would showcase the innovative potential of the web 3.0 technology. After a few meetings and discussions, the idea of the Seoul Web 3.0 Festival 2023 was born.

It was a bold move by the City of Seoul to host such a huge event, but they were convinced that it could be a game-changer for the web industry. And Cronos, with its expertise in blockchain and decentralized applications, was the perfect partner for this venture.

What is the Seoul Web 3.0 Festival?

The Seoul Web 3.0 Festival is a one-of-a-kind event that will bring together developers, innovators, entrepreneurs, policymakers, and investors from around the world to explore the possibilities of web 3.0 technology. The festival will be a mix of exhibitions, workshops, panel discussions, hackathons, and networking sessions.

The objective of the festival is to showcase the potential of web 3.0 and its decentralized applications, and to spur innovation in this field. The festival will also help create awareness about the benefits of web 3.0 and how it can improve our lives in various ways. By hosting this event, the City of Seoul aims to position itself as a global hub for web 3.0 technology and innovation.

Some Quantifiable Examples

  • The festival is expected to attract more than 10,000 attendees from around the world.
  • Over 100 companies are expected to showcase their products and services related to web 3.0 technology.
  • More than 50 world-renowned speakers and experts will give talks and participate in panel discussions.
  • More than 30 workshops and hackathons will be conducted for developers and entrepreneurs.
  • Over $1 million in prizes will be given away to the winners of hackathons and innovation challenges.
  • The festival is expected to generate over $20 million in economic impact for the City of Seoul.
