10 Lives Animated Feature Starring Zayn Malik Lands Chinese Deal

Have you heard of the upcoming animated movie, 10 Lives? Well, if you haven't, you're in for a treat! The movie stars none other than Zayn Malik, the former One Direction heartthrob turned solo artist. Fans all over the world are excited to see him take on a new role in this highly anticipated film.

The movie is set to be released in the near future, and it has already made headlines as it just landed a massive deal in China. According to Deadline, the movie has been picked up by Beijing Culture, one of China's biggest animation companies, for a seven-figure deal. This is huge news not only for the movie but for the entire animation industry as well.

  • The movie has already gained a massive following on social media, with over 3 million followers on Instagram.
  • The Chinese movie market is currently the second-largest in the world, with sales reaching over $9 billion in 2019.
  • The movie is being produced by the same team who worked on the highly successful The Lion King and The Jungle Book.

What Does This Mean for the Animation Industry?

The fact that 10 Lives has landed such a massive deal in China is a clear indication of the growing importance of the Chinese movie market. The animation industry, in particular, has seen tremendous growth in China in recent years, and this deal is just further proof of that.

This deal also means that the team behind 10 Lives will have more resources and support to put towards the film's production, which could result in an even better final product for audiences all over the world to enjoy.

Finally, this deal also highlights the reach and power of social media in marketing and promoting movies. With over 3 million followers on Instagram alone, the producers of 10 Lives have been able to create a massive buzz and anticipation for the movie before it's even been released.

Final Thoughts

It's clear that 10 Lives has a lot going for it, from its star-studded cast to its experienced production team. This latest deal with Beijing Culture is just further proof that this movie is going to be a hit when it's finally released. As moviegoers, we can only hope that it lives up to the hype!
