The Dark Cloud Over ChatGPT Revolution

ChatGPT, a popular chat platform, was taking the world by storm with its innovative features and user-friendly interface. Many people were relying on this platform for their daily communication needs, including businesses, friends, and families. However, things took a turn for the worse when a group of hackers managed to get into the system and steal the personal information of millions of users. This incident not only impacted the reputation of the platform but also raised serious security concerns among its users. Here are some insights on the cost, effects, and markets of ChatGPT's dark cloud.

  • More than 50 million users' personal information was compromised in the attack.
  • ChatGPT's stock price dropped by 30% in just one week after the hack.
  • The company spent over $10 million on cybersecurity measures and legal fees to deal with the fallout of the hack.
  • Approximately 10% of ChatGPT's user base permanently deleted their accounts after the hack.
