XQc Denies Claims of Moving to Kick After ChatGPT Conversations Surface

Streamer xQc has denied claims that he is leaving Twitch to move to rival platform KICK. The rumors emerged after a series of conversations were leaked between xQc and ChatGPT, a popular online community.

An interesting story

According to the leaked conversations, ChatGPT approached xQc with an offer to move to their platform in exchange for a lucrative contract. xQc initially seemed interested, asking for more details about the offer and the terms of the contract.

However, as the conversation continued, xQc expressed doubts about ChatGPT's ability to deliver on their promises. He raised concerns about the stability of the platform, the quality of the user base, and the potential for conflict with Twitch.

Ultimately, xQc decided not to pursue the offer and to remain on Twitch. He informed ChatGPT of his decision and thanked them for their interest, but made it clear that he had no intention of leaving the platform that had made him famous.
