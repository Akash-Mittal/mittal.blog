The concept is simple: take a small handful of ground beef, smash it down into a thin patty, and cook it on a flat top grill. Then, add your favorite taco toppings, fold the patty in half, and serve. The result? A delicious and satisfying combination of burger and taco that can be made in just a few minutes.
