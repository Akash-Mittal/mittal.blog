Pak Netizens Laud India and PM Modi: New Parliament Inauguration

In a surprising turn of events, Pakistani netizens have been praising Indian Prime Minister Narendra Modi for the inauguration of the new Indian Parliament building. Social media platforms like Twitter and Facebook have been flooded with positive comments from Pakistanis expressing their admiration for the new structure and PM Modi's leadership.

The hashtag #NewIndianParliament was trending in Pakistan with over 50,000 tweets supporting the new building in just 24 hours. One tweet read, "I must say, India is way ahead of us in building the infrastructure of its country and making it beautiful." Another user wrote, "This new Indian Parliament is absolutely stunning and I hope it is a sign of a more prosperous future for our two nations."

In the current state of heightened tensions between India and Pakistan, the surprising show of support from Pakistani netizens has caught the attention of the world. The praise for PM Modi and India's leadership has left many wondering if this could be a turning point for the two nations, who have had a tumultuous relationship.
