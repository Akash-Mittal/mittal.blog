What is Yuma AI?

Yuma AI is an AI-based ticket assistant that works alongside customer service agents to help manage support tickets. It can handle a variety of tasks, including:

  • Prioritizing tickets based on urgency
  • Assigning tickets to the appropriate agent
  • Suggesting solutions based on previous ticket history
  • Providing customers with automated responses
  • And more!

By automating these tasks, Yuma AI frees up customer service agents to focus on more complex tasks, such as resolving unique customer issues and improving overall customer satisfaction.

Why Integrate Yuma AI with Zendesk Support?

Zendesk Support is a popular customer support platform used by businesses of all sizes. It allows agents to manage support tickets from multiple sources, such as email, chat, and social media, in one centralized location. By integrating Yuma AI with Zendesk Support, businesses can take advantage of the powerful features of both systems.

By automating many of the repetitive tasks involved with managing support tickets, Yuma AI can save customer service agents countless hours of work. It can prioritize tickets based on urgency, assign them to the appropriate agent, and even suggest solutions based on previous ticket history. This frees up agents to focus on more complex tasks, such as resolving unique customer issues and improving overall customer satisfaction.

Accuracy

Yuma AI is powered by artificial intelligence, which means it can analyze large amounts of data to identify patterns and trends. By providing customers with accurate and effective solutions, businesses can improve customer satisfaction and loyalty.

Speed

Yuma AI is designed to respond to customer support requests quickly and efficiently. It can provide customers with automated responses to simple queries, such as password resets or account balance inquiries.
