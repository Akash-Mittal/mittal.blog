It was a chilly evening in Denver, but the heat was on at Cervantes Other Side on November 3 as ALO took the stage. The California rock band brought their unique blend of reggae, funk, and jam band music to an excited and enthusiastic crowd.

As the band started blasting out their hits, the room erupted in a frenzy of dancing and cheering. The energy was palpable as ALO worked their way through a setlist that included fan favorites like "Maria," "The Ticket," and "BBQ."

The chemistry among the band members was electric, and it was obvious that they were having just as much fun on stage as the audience was having on the dancefloor. Even as the night wore on and the crowd thinned out, ALO kept up the energy, never letting up until they had given their all.
