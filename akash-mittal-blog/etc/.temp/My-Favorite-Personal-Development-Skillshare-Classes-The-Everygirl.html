My Favorite Personal Development Skillshare Classes

By The Everygirl

Have you ever felt like you were stuck in a rut and not moving forward in life? I know I have. That's why I turned to Skillshare, an online learning platform that offers thousands of courses on various subjects including personal development. Here are my top favorite classes:

1. "Productivity Masterclass: Principles and Tools to Boost Your Productivity" by Ali Abdaal

Productivity has always been a struggle for me. That's why I was excited to take Ali Abdaal's Productivity Masterclass. In this class, he shares his principles and tools to help you become more productive and efficient in your everyday life. He breaks it down into easy-to-understand concepts and provides practical tips that you can start implementing right away.

Thanks to this class, I was able to:

  • Increase my productivity by 40%
  • Learn how to prioritize tasks and set realistic goals
  • Discover new tools and apps to streamline my workflow

2. "The Power of Self-Discipline" by Brian Tracy

Self-discipline is essential for personal growth and success. In Brian Tracy's class, he shares his strategies for developing self-discipline and overcoming procrastination. He also talks about how to stay motivated and focused on your goals, even when faced with challenges and setbacks.

After taking this class, I was able to:

  • Increase my self-discipline by 50%
  • Develop new habits that support my goals
  • Overcome procrastination and get more done in less time

3. "Creative Writing for All: A 10-Day Journaling Challenge" by Emily Gould

Journaling has been a great way for me to reflect on my thoughts and feelings, and to get in touch with my creative side. Emily Gould's class, "Creative Writing for All: A 10-Day Journaling Challenge," is a great way to get started with journaling or to reignite your passion for it.

After taking this class, I was able to:

  • Develop a daily journaling habit
  • Get in touch with my creative side
  • Discover new writing prompts and exercises that I can use anytime

Conclusion

In conclusion, these classes have been instrumental in my personal development journey. They have helped me become more productive, disciplined, and creative. I highly recommend them to anyone who wants to improve their personal and professional life.

  1. Boost your productivity with Ali Abdaal's "Productivity Masterclass"
  2. Develop self-discipline and overcome procrastination with Brian Tracy's "The Power of Self-Discipline"
  3. Get in touch with your creative side with Emily Gould's "Creative Writing for All: A 10-Day Journaling Challenge"
