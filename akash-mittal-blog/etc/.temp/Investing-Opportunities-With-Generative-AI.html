Quantifiable Examples

  • Finance: Generative AI can be used to analyze market trends and predict stock prices with impressive accuracy. For example, the hedge fund Numerai uses Generative AI to make investment decisions and has generated an average annual return of 23% since 2015.
  • Healthcare: Generative AI can be used to analyze patient data and identify patterns and correlations that would be difficult for humans to detect. For example, Atomwise uses Generative AI to discover new drugs and has identified two potential COVID-19 treatments in 2020.
  • Creative Industries: Generative AI can be used to generate content such as music, art, and even text. For example, Amper Music uses Generative AI to create customizable music for professional and amateur use.

Conclusion
