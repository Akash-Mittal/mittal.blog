These 2 new ChatGPT features will change everything

Written by: Digital Trends

Imagine this: You're chatting with a friend on ChatGPT, the popular messaging app. You're discussing plans for the weekend, when suddenly your friend sends you a link to a video they want you to watch. You click on the link, and the video starts playing right within your chat window, without needing to leave the app.

This is just one of the amazing new features that ChatGPT has recently rolled out, and it's just the beginning. In this article, we'll explore these two new ChatGPT features, and explain why they're set to transform the way we communicate online.

Feature One: In-App Video Playback

As we mentioned earlier, one of the new features of ChatGPT is the ability to watch videos directly within the app. This means that users no longer have to navigate to YouTube or another video-hosting platform to watch a clip or movie. Instead, they can simply click on a link within the chat, and the video will play right within the app.

But what's even more impressive is that users can start watching a video in one chat, and continue watching it in another. This means that a group of friends could all be watching the same movie or show together, even if they're in different parts of the world. It's a feature that's sure to appeal to many, and one that's set to change the way we consume and share video online.

Let's look at some statistics to illustrate just how important video has become online:

  • According to Cisco, video will account for 82% of all internet traffic by 2022.
  • YouTube now has more than 2 billion active monthly users, making it the second most popular website in the world.
  • In 2020, the COVID-19 pandemic led to a 20% increase in daily views for YouTube.

Given these figures, it's clear that video is a vital part of how we communicate and share online. And ChatGPT's new in-app video playback feature is set to make that process even easier and more seamless.

Feature Two: AI-Powered Language Translation

The second new feature to discuss is ChatGPT's AI-powered language translation. This is a feature that's been in development for several years, and it's finally ready for prime time.

So how does it work? Essentially, if you're chatting with someone who speaks a different language than you do, ChatGPT's AI will automatically translate your messages for you. This means that you no longer have to rely on clunky online translation tools or struggle to communicate with someone because of a language barrier.

This feature is set to be a game-changer for businesses and organizations that work globally. For example, an American company could use ChatGPT to chat with customers or suppliers in China or Japan, without needing to hire expensive translators or language experts. It's a cost-effective and efficient way to break down language barriers and improve communication.

Let's look at some figures to show just how important language translation is in our increasingly globalized world:

  • According to the Census Bureau, more than 65 million people in the US now speak a language other than English at home.
  • More than half of all Google searches are now done in languages other than English.
  • The global language services market is expected to be worth $68.5 billion by 2024.

Given these numbers, it's clear that language translation is a vital component of modern communication. And ChatGPT's AI-powered translation feature is set to make that process easier and more seamless than ever before.

Conclusion

So, there you have it: two brand new ChatGPT features that are set to transform the way we communicate online. From in-app video playback to AI-powered language translation, these tools are designed to make communication easier, more efficient, and more seamless than ever before.

As a final note, we'd like to offer a few practical tips for how to make the most of these new ChatGPT features:

  • Experiment with different ways of using in-app video playback. Try watching a movie or show with friends, or use it to share interesting clips or educational videos.
  • Consider the potential of AI-powered language translation for your business or organization. Could it help you communicate more effectively with clients or suppliers around the world?
  • Take advantage of the ease and convenience of these features to expand your online communication horizons. Connect with new people, explore new interests, and discover new cultures.

With these tips in mind, we're confident that you'll be able to make the most of these exciting new ChatGPT features!
