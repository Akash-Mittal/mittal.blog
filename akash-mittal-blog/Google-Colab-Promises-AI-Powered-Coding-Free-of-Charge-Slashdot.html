As the world becomes more reliant on technology, the demand for skilled programmers continues to rise. However, learning to code can often be an overwhelming and intimidating task. That's where Google Colab steps in.

An Interesting Story

For many high school students, the thought of coding brings to mind long hours spent sitting in front of a computer screen, furiously typing lines of code. But for a group of students in rural India, learning to code became a fun and collaborative experience, thanks to Google Colab.

Previously, these students had limited access to computers and had never written a single line of code. However, their enthusiasm for learning and willingness to try something new led them to Google Colab, which provided them with an AI-powered coding platform that was entirely free of charge.

With the help of Google Colab, these students were able to learn the basics of programming in a matter of weeks and even developed a simple game that they could play with each other. The experience not only gave them newfound confidence in their abilities, but also opened up a world of opportunities for them in the future.
