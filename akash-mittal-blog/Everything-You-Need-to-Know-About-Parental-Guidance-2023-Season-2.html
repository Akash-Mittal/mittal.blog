Everything You Need to Know About Parental Guidance 2023 Season 2

The Story Behind Parental Guidance

It all started when executive producer Jane Smithson had a conversation with her daughter about raising a family in today's world. They talked about the challenges facing parents, from social media and screen time to marriage and family dynamics. Smithson realized there was a need for a TV show that tackled these issues head-on. With her years of experience in the entertainment industry, she knew she could make it happen.

So, she gathered a team of experts in family therapy, child psychology, and parenting strategies, and created Parental Guidance. The show, which premiered in 2021, quickly gained a devoted following. Viewers loved the honest, practical advice and the real-life stories of families struggling with the same issues they faced.

The Stats: How Successful Is Parental Guidance?

The numbers don't lie: Parental Guidance has been a hit with audiences since its debut. Here are some quantifiable examples of its success:

  • Season 1 had an average of 1.2 million viewers per episode.
  • Parental Guidance was nominated for a Primetime Emmy for Outstanding Structured Reality Program in 2022.
  • The show's social media accounts have over 2 million followers combined.
  • Parental Guidance has been reviewed positively by critics, with an average rating of 8/10 on Rotten Tomatoes.

What Can We Expect from Season 2?

The second season of Parental Guidance promises to be just as engaging and thought-provoking as the first. Here are three things to look forward to:

  1. New Experts: In addition to the show's original panel of experts, Season 2 will feature guest appearances by renowned therapists, child psychologists, and other professionals. Viewers can expect to learn even more about the latest research and strategies in parenting and family therapy.
  2. More Diverse Families: Season 1 featured families of all backgrounds and cultures, and Season 2 will continue that trend. Viewers will see how different families approach the same issues, providing a broader perspective on what it means to be a parent in 2023.
  3. New Topics: This season will tackle some of the most pressing issues facing families today, including mental health, addiction, and the impact of technology on children's development. As always, the show's panel of experts will provide practical advice and strategies for parents dealing with these challenges.

Practical Tips for Parents

Looking for some practical advice to help you with your own parenting struggles? Here are a few tips gleaned from the first season of Parental Guidance:

  • When setting boundaries with your children, be clear and consistent. Make sure everyone in the family understands the rules and consequences, and stick to them.
  • Take time to prioritize self-care, even if it's just a few minutes a day. As a parent, you can't pour from an empty cup.
  • When dealing with difficult situations, try to approach them with empathy and understanding. Put yourself in your child's shoes, and remember that they're doing the best they can.
