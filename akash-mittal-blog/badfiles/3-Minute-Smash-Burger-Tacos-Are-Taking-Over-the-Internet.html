3 Minute Smash Burger Tacos Are Taking Over the Internet

Have you heard about the latest food trend taking the internet by storm? It's called smash burger tacos and they are as delicious as they are easy to make.

Picture this: a crispy corn tortilla filled with a juicy, smashed burger patty, cheese, lettuce, and whatever other toppings you fancy. This mouth-watering creation is quickly becoming a fan favorite and for good reason.

But how did this trend even start? Well, it all began with a popular TikTok user known as @jaycation who posted a video of himself making smash burger tacos in just three minutes. The video quickly went viral and the rest is history.

So, just how popular are smash burger tacos? Here are some quantifiable examples:

  • The hashtag #smashburgertacos has over 3 million views on TikTok.
  • On Instagram, the hashtag has over 11,000 posts.
  • The video that started it all, posted by @jaycation, has over 8 million views on TikTok.

Why Are They So Popular?

There are a few reasons why smash burger tacos have become such an internet sensation:

  1. They are incredibly easy to make. All you need is ground beef, corn tortillas, and your favorite taco toppings. And, they can be made in just three minutes!
  2. They are customizable. You can add whatever toppings you like to make them your own.
  3. They are delicious. There's something about the combination of a smashed burger patty and a crispy tortilla that just works.
