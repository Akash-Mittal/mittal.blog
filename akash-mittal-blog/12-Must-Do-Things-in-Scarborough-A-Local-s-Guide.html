12 Must-Do Things in Scarborough - A Local's Guide

By John Smith

Scarborough, a beautiful borough on the eastern side of Toronto, is packed with so much diversity that it can be difficult to tackle everything in a single visit. However, there are certain things and activities that everyone in Scarborough should experience at least once. Here are my top 12 must-do things in Scarborough that I recommend:

1. Visit the Scarborough Bluffs

Scarborough Bluffs is one of the most stunning natural wonders in the area, rising over 65 meters tall with a beautiful view of the lake. You can go hiking, have a picnic, or just enjoy the scenery. This place is perfect for watching the sunrise or the sunset.

Quantifiable example: Scarborough Bluffs attracts more than 2.4 million visitors annually and is one of the most photographed locations in Canada.

2. Experience the food scene

Scarborough is home to a diverse range of cultures, which means that there are endless food options- from Indian to Pakistani to Chinese and so on. Be sure to try some local dishes that represent the borough's culture.

Quantifiable example: There are more than 2000 restaurants in Scarborough, serving various cuisines.

3. Spend a day at the Toronto Zoo

The Toronto Zoo is the largest zoo in Canada and features a wide variety of animals from all over the world. It is a great place to spend a day with family and friends.

Quantifiable example: The Toronto Zoo attracts more than 1.3 million visitors annually.

4. Take a stroll at Morningside Park

Morningside Park is one of the best places to go for a leisurely walk. It provides scenic views and a serene atmosphere. You can go bird watching, hiking, and even fishing.

5. Go shopping at Scarborough Town Centre

Scarborough Town Centre is a newly renovated mall that offers a wide range of shopping options. From high-end stores to budget-friendly ones, there is something for everyone. It also features a range of dining options.

6. Visit the Aga Khan Museum

The Aga Khan Museum is an award-winning museum that represents a cross-cultural exchange of art and culture between the Islamic and Western world. It features a range of exhibits, performances, workshops, and educational programs.

7. Attend a concert at the Budweiser Stage

The Budweiser Stage is an open-air concert venue that hosts some of the biggest music names in the world. It offers a great ambiance and a perfect place to enjoy music outdoors.

8. Hike through the Rouge National Urban Park

The Rouge National Urban Park is a beautiful natural area that offers more than 12 hiking trails, as well as camping facilities. It is a great place to experience nature in the middle of the city.

9. Go to the Guild Inn Estate

The Guild Inn Estate is a historic site that features a range of restored buildings and gardens. It is a great place to learn about the borough's history.

10. Witness the famous Caribana Festival

The Caribana Festival is a popular annual event that celebrates Caribbean culture with music, dance, and food. It is one of the biggest cultural events in Canada.

Quantifiable example: The Caribana Festival attracts more than 1.2 million visitors annually.

11. Watch a movie at the Cineplex Odeon Eglinton Town Centre

Cineplex Odeon Eglinton Town Centre is a famous cinema hall that features the latest movies. It is a great place to unwind and enjoy a movie with friends and family.

12. Play golf at Scarborough Golf and Country Club

The Scarborough Golf and Country Club is a popular golf course that offers picturesque views and a challenging course. It is a great place to relax and enjoy the outdoors.

Conclusion:

  1. Scarborough has a diverse range of must-do activities that everyone can enjoy.
  2. The above 12 activities are some of the best that Scarborough has to offer.
  3. By experiencing these activities, you can fully appreciate the beauty and diversity of Scarborough.
