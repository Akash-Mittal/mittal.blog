How to Export Your ChatGPT Data

Fortunately, chatGPT allows you to export your chat history in a few simple steps. First, you need to open the chat conversation you want to save and click on the three dots in the top right corner. Then, select "More" and choose "Export Chat." Finally, choose whether you want to export the chat without media, or include media files, and click "Export." Your chat will be saved in a .zip file that you can download and save on your device.

In a recent example, WhatsApp announced a change in their privacy policy, causing concern among users about how the app handles their data.
