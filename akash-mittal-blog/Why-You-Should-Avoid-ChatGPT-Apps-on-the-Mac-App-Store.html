Why You Should Avoid ChatGPT Apps on the Mac App Store

Imagine you're a Mac user and you're looking for a chat app to keep in touch with your friends and family. You go to the Mac App Store and see a popular app called ChatGPT. You think it looks like a great choice and download it without a second thought.

However, what you don't know is that ChatGPT is a fraudulent app that can potentially harm your computer and steal your personal data.

Several Mac users have reported instances where they installed ChatGPT and then experienced problems such as excessive advertising, fake updates, and pop-ups. In some cases, the app even hijacked their Safari browser.

The developers behind ChatGPT claim that the app is a legitimate chat platform. However, this couldn't be further from the truth. In reality, ChatGPT is a fake app developed by scammers posing as an online chat company.
