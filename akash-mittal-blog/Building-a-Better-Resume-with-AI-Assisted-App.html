Practical Tips for Building a Better Resume

  • Use specific keywords and phrases that are relevant to the job and industry.
  • Keep your formatting clean and easy to read. Use bullet points and white space to break up text.
  • Highlight your most important accomplishments and skills, and quantify them whenever possible (e.g. "increased sales by 25%").
  • Proofread your resume multiple times, and ask someone else to review it as well.
  • Use an AI-assisted app like Zety or TopResume to get personalized feedback and insights on your resume.
