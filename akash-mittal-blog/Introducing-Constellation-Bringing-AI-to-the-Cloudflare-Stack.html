What is Constellation?

Constellation is the latest addition to the Cloudflare stack, bringing the power of artificial intelligence to website security and performance optimization.

Using machine learning algorithms, Constellation can analyze a website's traffic patterns and identify abnormal behavior that may signal a cyber attack. It can also optimize website performance by automatically adjusting server resources and content delivery network (CDN) settings.

But how does Constellation stack up against other security and performance optimization solutions?

Quantifiable Examples

Security

According to Cloudflare, Constellation can accurately identify and mitigate DDoS attacks faster than other solutions on the market.

  • Constellation can detect and mitigate attacks within seconds, while other solutions take minutes or even hours.
  • Constellation can also detect and mitigate attacks of any size, while other solutions may struggle with large-scale attacks.
  • Constellation can prevent malicious traffic from ever reaching a website, while other solutions may only be able to mitigate the effects of an attack.

Performance Optimization

Constellation can also help improve website performance by automatically optimizing server resources and CDN settings.

  • Constellation can reduce website load times by up to 30%, resulting in a better user experience and higher conversion rates.
  • Constellation can also reduce server costs by up to 60%, by automatically scaling server resources up or down based on website traffic.
  • Constellation can optimize website delivery by automatically choosing the best CDN settings based on user location and website content.
