An Insight into TMS Network and MASK Network

TMS Network is a decentralized, peer-to-peer payment gateway that operates on the blockchain. It is designed to enable cross-border transactions with insignificant fees, with the user experience being easy and seamless. The network is built on the Ethereum blockchain, and it uses smart contracts that allow for transparent, secure, and fast transactions.

On the other hand, the MASK Network focuses on enabling anonymity and a high degree of privacy in communications on the blockchain. It is designed to ensure that users' identities and the content of their communications are not exposed to unauthorized third parties.
