Web3 Games 101 - LCX

Web3 games are a new form of gaming that utilize blockchain technology and cryptocurrency. These games have been growing in popularity due to their decentralized nature and the potential for players to earn real money. LCX is one platform that has been at the forefront of the Web3 gaming revolution.

The Rise of Web3 Games

Web3 games have a fascinating origin story. It all started with the launch of the Ethereum blockchain in 2015. This allowed developers to create decentralized applications (dApps) that could execute smart contracts. One of the earliest dApps was CryptoKitties, a game where players could collect and breed digital cats. It became incredibly popular and caused congestion on the Ethereum network due to its high demand for transactions.

Since then, Web3 games have evolved in numerous ways. Some of the latest games are more complex and immersive, with gameplay mechanics that are similar to traditional video games but with added rewards and incentives. Others have taken a more casual approach, with simple gameplay and real money prizes that are awarded based on luck or skill.

Real Life Examples

One popular Web3 game is Axie Infinity, a game that involves collecting and breeding creatures called Axies. Players can battle their Axies against other players and earn cryptocurrency in the form of Axie Infinity Shards (AXS) and Smooth Love Potions (SLP). Axie Infinity has become so popular that some players are earning enough money to support themselves as full-time players.

Another example is ChainGuardian, a game that combines elements of tower defense and RPGs. Players can collect and train heroes, battle enemies, and earn cryptocurrency rewards. ChainGuardian recently launched an initial NFT sale, where players could purchase NFTs that grant access to special features and bonuses in the game.

Main Companies in Web3 Gaming

  1. LCX: LCX is a blockchain-based platform that offers several Web3 games, including ChainGuardian and DynastySino. They also provide a digital asset management system for traders and investors.
  2. Axie Infinity: Axie Infinity is a game that has taken the Web3 gaming world by storm. Its popularity is due to its engaging gameplay and rewards system.
  3. ChainGuardian: ChainGuardian is a tower defense/RPG game that rewards players with cryptocurrency for completing missions and quests.

Critical Comments

  1. Web3 games are still in their early stages and there are several challenges that need to be addressed, such as scalability and user experience.
  2. Some Web3 games can be addictive and potentially harmful. It's important for users to exercise caution and moderation when playing.
  3. There are concerns about the environmental impact of Web3 gaming due to the energy consumption required for blockchain transactions. Some companies are addressing this by using more eco-friendly systems or transitioning to proof-of-stake instead of proof-of-work.
