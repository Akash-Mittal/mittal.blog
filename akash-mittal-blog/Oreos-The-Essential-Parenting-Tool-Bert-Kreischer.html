Oreos: The Essential Parenting Tool

An interesting story to start

Comedian Bert Kreischer recently declared Oreos to be an essential part of parenting in a YouTube video that went viral. In the video, Kreischer recounts a time when his daughter was having a meltdown and nothing seemed to calm her down. Desperate for a solution, he offered her an Oreo and just like that, she was suddenly happy and content. Kreischer goes on to explain that Oreos have become a go-to tool for him as a parent, something he never thought he would rely on but has found to be incredibly effective.

Quantifiable examples

Kreischer isn't alone in his belief that Oreos are an essential part of parenting. A quick search on social media platforms like Instagram and Twitter reveals countless parents sharing stories of how Oreos have saved the day. Some examples include:

  • A mom who uses Oreos as a reward system for her kids to encourage good behavior
  • A dad who uses Oreos as a bribe to get his kids to do their homework
  • A parent who uses Oreos to distract their kids during long car rides

These are just a few examples, but they illustrate just how versatile Oreos can be when it comes to parenting. The cookies can be used as incentives, distractions, rewards, and so much more.

Kreischer also shares some personal anecdotes about his own use of Oreos as a parenting tool. For example, he often uses them as a way to bribe his daughters into taking pictures with him, something they're not always eager to do. He also talks about how a pack of Oreos can save him on long flights with his kids, keeping them occupied and satisfied for hours.

  1. Oreos have become an essential part of parenting for many parents, including comedian Bert Kreischer who swears by them.
