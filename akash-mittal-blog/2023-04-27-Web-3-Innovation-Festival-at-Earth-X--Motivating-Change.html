Web 3 Innovation Festival at Earth X: Motivating Change

An Inspiring Story

When a team of blockchain enthusiasts gathered at Earth X, the world's largest environmental exposition, they had one goal in mind - to inspire change. They believed that blockchain technology could be a powerful tool to address environmental issues and bring about a sustainable future. And so, the Web 3 Innovation Festival was born.

Concrete Examples

  • One of the key highlights of the festival was the Blockchain for Impact Challenge, which brought together innovators and entrepreneurs to develop blockchain-based solutions to address environmental and social challenges.
  • The festival also showcased several blockchain projects that are already making a difference in the environmental space, such as Plastic Bank, which uses blockchain to incentivize the collection and recycling of plastic waste.
  • Another notable project was Provenance, which uses blockchain to create transparent supply chains for products, ensuring that they are sustainably sourced and manufactured.
