It was a hot summer day in New York City and Robert De Niro was walking in Central Park with his two-year-old son. Suddenly, his son ran away from him and ran towards a pond. De Niro panicked and chased after him. Just as he reached his son, the child stumbled and almost fell into the pond. De Niro was able to catch him just in time, but the experience left him shaken. This incident made him realise that being a parent is not easy, but it is also one of the most rewarding experiences of his life.

The Exciting Part of Parenting

Robert De Niro has seven children, and he finds that each one is unique and brings something special to his life. For him, the exciting part of parenting is watching his children develop into independent and capable individuals. He enjoys seeing them achieve their goals and succeed in their chosen career paths. He is proud of his children and the people they have become.

De Niro also considers it exciting to be able to share experiences with his children and to see the world through their eyes. He loves to travel with them and expose them to different cultures and ways of life. He believes that these experiences help his children become well-rounded individuals with a broader perspective on life.

Finally, De Niro is also excited about the opportunity to pass on his values and beliefs to his children. He believes that parenting is about setting a good example and teaching his children to be kind, compassionate and empathetic. He takes great pride in the fact that his children have a strong moral compass and are good citizens of the world.

These are just a few examples of the exciting aspects of parenting. There are many others, and each parent will find their own unique joys in the experience.

The Scary Part of Parenting

Despite the many joys of parenting, there are also many challenges and fears that come with the responsibility of raising children. Robert De Niro knows this all too well, having raised seven children.

One of the scariest parts of parenting, according to De Niro, is the fear of something happening to your child. Whether it's an accident, illness, or any other potential danger, the thought of something happening to your child can be overwhelming. This is why De Niro is always vigilant and takes every precaution to keep his children safe.

Another scary aspect of parenting is the worry that you're not doing a good enough job. De Niro admits that he sometimes feels this way, despite having raised seven successful children. He believes that every parent doubts themselves at some point, but it's important to remember that it's normal and to keep doing your best.

Finally, De Niro also believes that the responsibility of parenting can be scary in and of itself. It's a huge responsibility to raise a child and prepare them for the world. The decisions you make can have a profound impact on their lives, and this can be intimidating at times.

Conclusion

Robert De Niro believes that parenting is an exciting and scary adventure, full of joys and challenges. His personal experiences as a father of seven have taught him that every child is unique and brings something special to the world. Being a parent is not easy, but it is also one of the most rewarding experiences of his life.

In conclusion, the excitement and fear of parenting go hand in hand. As parents, we must learn to embrace both and enjoy the journey.
