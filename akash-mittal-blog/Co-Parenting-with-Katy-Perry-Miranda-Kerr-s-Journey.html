Tips for Successful Co-Parenting

Based on her experience, here are a few tips that Kerr would recommend for successful co-parenting:

  1. Communication is key. Whether it involves a weekly call or daily text messages, it is important to stay in touch with your co-parent regarding any updates, changes, or concerns about your child.
  2. Respect each other's boundaries. This includes each other's personal life and relationships. Understanding and accepting that your child will have different experiences in each home is vital to a peaceful co-parenting relationship.
  3. Put the child's needs first. This may seem like a given, but it can be challenging in practice. By keeping your child's best interests at heart, you can navigate any bumps in the road that may arise.
