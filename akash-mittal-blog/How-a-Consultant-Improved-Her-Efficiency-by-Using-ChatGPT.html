How a Consultant Used ChatGPT to Free Up Time and Focus on Pitching Clients

By Akash Mittal | Feb 2021 | Category: Productivity

As a consultant, time is of the essence. One of the biggest challenges I faced was managing my workload efficiently and effectively.

One day, while browsing through Yahoo Finance, I stumbled upon an article discussing the benefits of ChatGPT. Intrigued, I decided to do some research to see if it could help me with my workload.

After some testing, I discovered that ChatGPT was an incredibly useful tool. It allowed me to automate a lot of my workload, freeing up a significant amount of time for me to focus on pitching clients. Here are a few examples of how I used ChatGPT:

  1. Automating Email Responses: With ChatGPT, I was able to set up automatic email responses for common questions. This saved me a lot of time and allowed me to focus on crafting customized emails for potential clients.
  2. Scheduling: ChatGPT also helped me automate my scheduling. It synced with my calendar and used natural language processing to schedule appointments with clients. This saved me from the back and forth of scheduling emails and allowed me to book more meetings with less effort.
  3. Research: ChatGPT helped me automate research tasks. It could quickly find information on potential clients and competitors and compile it into a report. This saved me a lot of time and allowed me to focus on analyzing the data and coming up with action plans.

Overall, ChatGPT was a game changer for me. It allowed me to focus on pitching clients and growing my business, instead of getting bogged down in the minutia of day to day tasks. If you're a consultant or business owner looking to improve your efficiency, I highly recommend giving ChatGPT a try.
