About the Contest

The MyAnimeList Honeyfeed 3rd Web Novel Contest is an annual competition organized by MyAnimeList and Honeyfeed. The contest is open to writers from all over the world, and the aim is to find and promote new talent in the world of web novels.

Participants are required to submit a web novel of at least three chapters and a total of 10,000 words. The submissions are then evaluated by a panel of judges based on the following criteria:

  • Engaging Storyline
  • Well-developed Characters
  • Effective Use of Language
  • Overall Presentation and Coherence

Benefits for Aspiring Writers

The MyAnimeList Honeyfeed 3rd Web Novel Contest is not just another writing competition. It offers several benefits to aspiring writers, and here are some of them:

Increased Visibility

One of the main benefits of participating in the contest is the increased visibility for your work. The contest is widely promoted across various social media platforms, and winning or being a finalist will ensure that your work reaches a wider audience.

According to the organizers, the previous contest had over 1,000 submissions from all over the world, which is a testament to the reach of the platform.

Recognition and Rewards

The MyAnimeList Honeyfeed 3rd Web Novel Contest doesn't just offer recognition, but also rewards for the winners and finalists. The top three winners will receive cash prizes, with the grand prize winner receiving $3,000, the second-place winner receiving $1,500, and the third-place winner receiving $500.

In addition to these cash prizes, winners and finalists will also receive an opportunity to work with Honeyfeed to turn their web novel into a professional manga series. This is an exciting opportunity for aspiring writers who are interested in exploring other aspects of the creative industry.

Tips for Winning

While the MyAnimeList Honeyfeed 3rd Web Novel Contest offers a great opportunity for aspiring writers, winning the competition is no easy feat. Here are some tips to increase your chances of success:

Focus on Your Story

The most critical aspect of any web novel is the storyline. Ensure that your story is engaging and has a clear direction. Create characters that are relatable and have a distinct personality. Develop your plot and make sure there are no plot holes or inconsistencies.

Remember, the judges will be evaluating your story based on its ability to captivate their attention and maintain their interest throughout the web novel.

Edit, Edit, and Edit Some More

Before submitting your web novel, ensure that it is free of any grammatical errors, typos, or formatting issues. Have someone else proofread your work or use editing software to identify any issues that you may have overlooked. A well-polished web novel shows that you have given thought to your work and are serious about your craft.

Be Unique and Original

The web novel market is saturated with similar plot lines and characters. To make your work stand out, ensure that it is unique and original. Avoid cliches, overused character archetypes, or weak plot points. Work on creating something that is different, intriguing, and will capture the reader's imagination.
