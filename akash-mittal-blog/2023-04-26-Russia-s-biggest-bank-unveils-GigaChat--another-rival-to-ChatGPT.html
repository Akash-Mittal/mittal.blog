Russia's biggest bank Sberbank unveils GigaChat, competing with ChatGPT

By Akash Mittal

Sberbank, the biggest bank in Russia, has unveiled its own chat platform GigaChat. The platform is designed to enable businesses and individuals to communicate with each other easily, effectively and securely, keeping the data safe and private.

With GigaChat, Sberbank aims to tap into the growing trend of communication platforms that can be used across multiple devices, including smartphones, desktops and tablets. The platform is set to become a direct rival to ChatGPT, a popular chat platform that has been gaining momentum in recent years.

An interesting story

In 2020, Sberbank launched a pilot version of GigaChat for its corporate clients in Russia. The bank realized that communication is a key aspect of running any business, and thus, decided to offer a secure and efficient way for its clients to communicate with each other. The platform was well received, and Sberbank decided to expand its audience by offering GigaChat to the general public.

Concrete examples

GigaChat is designed to be a user-friendly platform that enables businesses and individuals to communicate with each other easily, no matter where they are. The platform offers a range of features, such as voice and video calling, file sharing, and screen sharing. The platform is also available on multiple devices, including smartphones, desktops, and tablets, making it easy for users to stay connected on-the-go.

Sberbank has also added some new features to GigaChat, such as chatbots, to enhance the user experience. Chatbots can assist users with a range of tasks, such as scheduling meetings, making payments, and booking travel arrangements. This feature is particularly useful for businesses, as it can help them to save time and money by streamlining their operations.

Conclusion

In summary, Sberbank's launch of GigaChat is a significant move in the communication platform market. Sberbank has a strong reputation in Russia, and as such, GigaChat is set to become a direct rival to ChatGPT. The platform is user-friendly, efficient, and secure, offering businesses and individuals an easy way to communicate with each other. With the addition of chatbots, GigaChat is likely to become an even more popular communication platform in the coming years.
