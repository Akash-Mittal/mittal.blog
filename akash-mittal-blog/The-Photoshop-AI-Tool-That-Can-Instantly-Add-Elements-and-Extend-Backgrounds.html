How Does the Photoshop AI Tool Work?

The Photoshop AI tool uses machine learning to examine the existing photo's composition and identify the elements that naturally fit into the scene. It then seamlessly blends any new elements into the photograph, making it look as if they were present all along.

The AI tool can also extend backgrounds, giving you a wider range of creative possibilities. The tool works by examining the image's content and identifying the edges of the photograph. It then generates a new background that fits seamlessly into the existing scene.
