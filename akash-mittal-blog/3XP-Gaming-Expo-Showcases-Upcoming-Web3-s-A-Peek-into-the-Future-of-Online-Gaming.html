1. Decentraland

Decentraland is a blockchain-based virtual world that allows players to buy, sell, and build on virtual land. Each piece of land is a non-fungible token (NFT) that can be traded on Decentraland's open marketplace. Players can create their own games and experiences within their virtual land. Decentraland offers endless possibilities for players who want to create and share their own virtual reality (VR) experiences.

At the 3XP Gaming Expo, Decentraland showcased its latest feature - a decentralised casino. Players can use cryptocurrency to play casino games, and all transactions are recorded on the blockchain, ensuring transparency and fairness. With Decentraland, players can enjoy online gambling without worrying about rigged games or censorship.

3. The Sandbox

The Sandbox is a metaverse that allows players to own, create, and monetize their own gaming experiences. Players can use SAND, The Sandbox's native cryptocurrency, to buy and sell virtual land and assets. Developers can create games and experiences using The Sandbox's creation tools, and they can earn revenue from their creations by monetizing them through in-game purchases.

One of the most exciting features of The Sandbox is its virtual real estate market. Players can own and build on virtual land, creating their own unique gaming experiences.
