The Story of Europe's New AI Law

Europe's new artificial intelligence law is making waves across the tech industry. The law, which was proposed by the European Union in April 2021, would impose strict regulations on the development and use of AI in the region. Its goal is to create a "human-centric" approach to AI, protect citizens' fundamental rights and freedoms, and foster trust in AI technology.

But what does this mean for US firms that operate or sell AI technology in Europe? The law poses a significant threat, as it could force US companies to either comply with EU regulations or face heavy fines and penalties. This could result in a loss of revenue and market share for US firms, as well as stifle innovation in the AI industry.
