Join the movement to build a sustainable future!

How It Works

If you're a cyclist who wants to make an impact on the environment and your health, IWG has got you covered. During Bike to Work Week, we're offering free access to our flexible workplace locations across the world. All you need to do is register and show up on your bike.

  • Find a location Check our website to find a IWG location near you. Find a location
  • Show up On Bike to Work Day, show up at the IWG location and enjoy free access to our facilities. Show up

Why Biking to Work Makes Sense

Biking to work can have a positive impact on both your health and the environment. Here are some quantifiable examples:

  • Health benefits: Regular physical activity like biking can reduce your risk of heart disease, stroke, and diabetes by up to 50%. It can also improve your mental health and well-being.
  • Environmental benefits: Biking is a zero-emission mode of transportation. By biking to work, you can reduce your carbon footprint by up to 3.6 metric tons per year and help improve air quality in your city.
  • Savings: Biking to work can save you money in the long run by reducing the need for a car, gas, and parking. It can also save you time by avoiding traffic and improving your productivity.

How IWG Helps Build a Sustainable Future

At IWG, we're committed to creating a sustainable future for the planet and its people. One of our main initiatives is to reduce carbon emissions by promoting remote work and flexible working arrangements.

  • Reducing commute: By providing flexible workspace options, we help reduce the need for long commutes and promote alternative modes of transportation like biking, walking, or public transit.
  • Minimizing waste: We strive to minimize waste and pollution by reducing paper use, increasing recycling, and implementing sustainable practices in our facilities.
  • Promoting sustainability: We partner with organizations that promote sustainability and community engagement to create a positive impact on the environment and the society.

Conclusion

If you're a cyclist who cares about the environment and your health, Bike to Work Week is a great opportunity to make a difference. By signing up for IWG's free access, you can enjoy the benefits of flexible workspace while reducing your carbon footprint and improving your fitness. Here are three key takeaways:

  1. Biking to work can have significant health, environmental, and financial benefits.
  2. IWG's flexible workspace options promote sustainable transportation and reduce commute time and carbon emissions.
  3. Bike to Work Week is a great opportunity to join the movement for a greener and healthier future!
