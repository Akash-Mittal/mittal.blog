Why SAP ChatGPT Opportunities Should Make On-Premises Low Priority?

Imagine yourself as a small business owner, one day you realize that your traditional methods are not sufficient to keep your business up and running.

One of your employees suggests investing in an on-premises ERP system. After listening to his suggestion, you start researching for a solution that fits your business requirements and budget.

But after few days of research, you figure out that the system is way too expensive and it requires dedicated IT staff to maintain it. You decide to put this on hold and look for alternative solutions.

This scenario is not uncommon, and many businesses face the same dilemma when looking for an ERP system. But, SAP ChatGPT Opportunities with CEO Christian Klein suggest to keep on-premises low priority and focus on the cloud-based solutions instead.

Concrete Examples

SAP ChatGPT Opportunities are vast, and they offer a wide range of solutions to businesses of all sizes. SAP S/4 HANA cloud is one such solution that offers all the benefits of an on-premises system, but with the flexibility and scalability of a cloud-based solution.

Another example is SAP SuccessFactors, a cloud-based HR solution that offers everything from recruitment, onboarding, payroll, and performance management. It is a cost-effective solution that is easy to deploy and maintain.

Conclusion

  • Investing in on-premises ERP is expensive
  • Dedicated IT staff is required to maintain on-premises ERP
  • Cloud-based solutions offer more flexibility and scalability at lower costs
