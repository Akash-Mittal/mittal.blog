The Unforgettable Web Gem: Del Tin's Incredible Catch

Introduction

The Web Gem

Shore Regional had a runner on first base with one out when their batter hit a line drive towards the third baseman, Aaron Del Tin. The crowd erupted in cheers and his teammates rushed to congratulate him on his incredible catch.

His web gem had not only prevented a potential run, but it had also given Rumson Fair Haven a much-needed momentum boost. The team rallied around Del Tin's effort and went on to win the game 2-1, advancing to the next round of the SCT tournament.

Del Tin's web gem wasn't just a great moment for the team, it was also a statistically significant play.
