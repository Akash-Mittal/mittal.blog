Kim Kardashian on Parenting: Balancing Fame and Family

Published on News.com.au | August 1, 2021

An interesting story

Kim Kardashian is one of the most famous and controversial celebrities of our time. From her reality show "Keeping Up with the Kardashians" to her fashion and beauty empire, she has built an empire worth billions of dollars and a following of millions of fans. However, in the midst of her busy and glamorous lifestyle, there is one role that Kardashian values above all others: being a mother.

Recently, Kardashian opened up in an interview about her experience as a parent, and how she tries to balance her career and her family. Here are some of the insights she shared, along with some quantifiable examples:

Quantifiable examples

  • Kardashian has four children with her husband Kanye West: North (8), Saint (5), Chicago (3), and Psalm (2).
  • She tries to spend as much time as possible with her children, especially during their formative years.
  • She has a team of nannies and assistants to help her with childcare and household tasks, but she is very hands-on and involved in her children's lives.
  • In a 2019 interview with Vogue, Kardashian said that she wakes up before 6 a.m. every day to work out and have some "me time" before her kids wake up.
  • Kardashian is also an advocate for mental health, and has spoken openly about her struggles with anxiety and other issues.

Practical tips and personal anecdotes

Based on her own experience as a busy and successful parent, Kardashian shared some practical tips for other parents who are trying to balance work, family, and personal well-being:

  1. Make time for yourself: Whether it's waking up early to exercise or taking a few minutes to meditate, Kardashian believes in the importance of self-care for parents. "You have to take care of yourself in order to be a good parent," she said.
  2. Delegate and prioritize: Kardashian is not afraid to ask for help when she needs it, and she encourages other parents to do the same. "You can't do everything by yourself," she said. "If you have a team of people that you trust, you can delegate some tasks and focus on what's most important."
  3. Be present and engaged: When Kardashian is with her children, she tries to be fully present and engaged, whether they are playing together or doing homework. She also encourages parents to put down their phones and other distractions and focus on their children. "Kids grow up so fast, and you don't want to miss a moment," she said.

In addition to these tips, Kardashian also shared some personal anecdotes and case studies to illustrate her points. For example, she talked about how she and her children love to do yoga and meditation together, and how she tries to create a calm and positive environment at home. She also talked about how she has been working to manage her own stress and anxiety, including using therapy and other resources.

Conclusion: Three key takeaways

  1. Being a parent is one of the most important and challenging roles in life, and it requires a lot of hard work, dedication, and love.
  2. Even when juggling a busy and demanding career, it is possible to be a present and engaged parent, by prioritizing and delegating tasks, and making time for self-care.
  3. By sharing her own experience and insights, Kim Kardashian has become an inspiration and role model for many parents who are trying to balance fame, family, and personal well-being.
