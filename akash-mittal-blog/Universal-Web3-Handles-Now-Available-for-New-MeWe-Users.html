What is Web3?

Web3 is a new generation of the internet that is decentralized and powered by blockchain technology. Unlike the current internet infrastructure, which is dominated by large corporations that centralize and monetize user data, Web3 is peer-to-peer and transparent. This means that users retain ownership and control of their data and can interact with each other directly without the need for intermediaries.

At its core, Web3 is about creating a more democratized and equitable internet that empowers individuals and communities.

How Do Universal Web3 Handles Work?

Universal Web3 Handles are a unique and secure identity system that is interoperable across different Web3 platforms. By providing a user with a digital signature, a cryptographic key that authenticates their identity, Universal Web3 Handles allow users to access and control their data across multiple networks without having to create a new identity each time. This makes it easy for users to switch between platforms without losing their data or having to recreate their connections.

Universal Web3 Handles also enable users to access decentralized applications (dApps) and services that are built on a Web3 infrastructure. These dApps can range from social networks and messaging apps to financial services and governance platforms. By providing a universal identity, Universal Web3 Handles make it easy for users to interact with these dApps and services without having to create new accounts or go through complicated authentication processes.
